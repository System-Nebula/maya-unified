– 1 Tsp Salt

INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the skinless salmon into equal fillets/steaks. 
Season both sides with garlic powder, smoked 
paprika, salt, and black pepper. Rub the spices in 
evenly for maximum flavor absorption.
	
z S T E P 2 
Heat a pan over medium heat, add a little olive oil, 
and cook the seasoned salmon for 4–5 minutes per 
side until beautifully golden brown and seared. Set 
aside to rest, keeping it warm.
	
z S T E P 3 
While the salmon cooks, prepare your noodles 
according to package instructions or boil for 5 
minutes until slightly underdone (they’ll finish 
cooking in the sauce). Drain and rinse under cold 
water to stop the cooking process, then set aside.
	
z S T E P 4
In the same pan over medium-low heat, sauté 
chopped garlic for 2 minutes until fragrant. Add 
chili garlic paste and light coconut milk, then 
season with salt, black pepper, and paprika. 
Simmer for 4–5 minutes until slightly thickened.
	
z S T E P 5
Add the cooked noodles, chopped green onion, 
and light & dark soy sauce. Mix gently until 
combined, then let it simmer for 3-4 minutes to 
thicken further.
	
z S T E P 6
Serve and divide the creamy noodles with the juicy 
seared salmon on top into 4 portions, and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Follow the packet instructions for cooking the noodles (I simmered mine for 5 minutes).
	
z
After adding the coconut milk, bring it to a light bubble before adding the noodles. Once added, mix well 
and let it simmer for a few minutes to thicken the sauce.
	
z
Serve the salmon on top and enjoy! For meal prep, store the salmon and noodles separately for easier 
reheating.

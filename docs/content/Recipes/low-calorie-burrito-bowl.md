How to make
1.	 Cube the chicken breast and season with all listed marinade ingredients. Mix 
well and set aside. Chop the bell peppers and red or white onion.
2.	 In a blender cup, add half an avocado, Greek yogurt, coriander, garlic powder, 
salt, pepper, lime juice, and a little water. Blend until smooth and creamy.
3.	 In a pot, add water and washed basmati rice along with the same seasonings 
used for the chicken and 1–2 tbsp of tomato paste. Bring to a boil, then simmer 
for 8–10 minutes until cooked.
4.	 Heat a pan on high, spray with oil, and cook the chicken for 3–4 minutes on 
each side until golden brown. Remove and set aside. In the same pan, cook the 
chopped peppers and onions for 5 minutes until soft.
5.	 Serve with the tomato rice, cooked chicken, sautéed veggies, shredded 
lettuce, black beans, sweet corn, lime, sour cream or yogurt, and a generous 
drizzle of the green sauce.
If you want the sauce to be more

INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breasts into small cubes and 
place in a bowl. Add soy sauce, black pepper, 
garlic, and chili flakes. Mix thoroughly until the 
chicken is evenly coated — the color should deepen. 
Set aside to marinate while you prep the rest.

In a separate bowl, make the Pad Thai sauce by 
combining soy sauce, tamarind paste, honey, fish 
sauce, sriracha, and lime juice. Whisk until fully 
combined and smooth, then set aside for later.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan over medium heat and add a drizzle 
of olive oil. Cook the marinated chicken for 3–4 
minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked 
through. Once done, remove and set aside.

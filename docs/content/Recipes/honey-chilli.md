– 2 Tsp Paprika

to keep mostly the crisp (1.4oz) 
	
– 35g Honey (1.25oz / ~2 Tbsp)
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z STEP 1: PREP & MARINATE THE CHICKEN
Trim any excess fat from the boneless chicken 
thighs, then place them in a mixing bowl. Add salt, 
black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, chilli crisp 
(mostly the crisp, minimal oil), and honey. Mix 
thoroughly with your hands until the chicken is 
evenly coated and the colour deepens. Set aside to 
marinate for at least 30 minutes.
	
z S T E P 2 : C O O K T H E C H I C K E N
Spread the marinated chicken thighs evenly on a lined 
sheet pan. Oven bake or air fry at 190°C / 385°F for 
16-18 minutes, until golden brown, juicy, and lightly 
charred on the edges.
	
z S T E P 3 : R E S T & S TO R E T H E 
C H I C K E N ( K E E P T H I G H S W H O L E )
Once the chicken is cooked, allow it to rest for 5 
minutes. For meal prep purposes, keep the chicken 
whole to retain maximum juiciness throughout the 
week. Pour all the tray juices over the thighs - this 
adds flavour, prevents drying, and enhances the 
honey chilli glaze as it cools. Slice into cubes only 
when serving to maintain the best texture and 
moisture. 
Enjoy your flavour packed, high protein chicken for 
the week!
BEST WAYS TO SERVE AND PAIR THIS PROTEIN 
This Honey Chilli Crisp Chicken works extremely well with fresh, bright Asian-inspired sides, for example:
	
–with coconut rice and a pineapple, avocado, cucumber salsa 
	
–with jasmine rice and quick pickled cucumbers 
	
–in a warm wrap with shredded cabbage and lime
SAUCE PAIRING SUGGESTIONS 
Sauces designed to match the flavour and suitable for meal prep:
	
–honey chilli yogurt drizzle (yogurt, lime, honey, pinch of chilli flakes) 
	
–chilli-lime mayo (olive-oil-based mayo, lime juice, garlic) 
	
–cilantro yogurt sauce (yogurt, coriander, lime, garlic, pinch of salt)

Garlic Herb Cajun Chicken
	
– 1000g (35oz / 2.2lb) Raw Boneless 
Skinless Chicken Thighs 
	
– 2 Tsp Salt 
	
– 3 Tsp Italian Herbs 
	
– 1.5 Tsp Chilli Flakes »»
	» Continue
	
– 2 Tsp Parsley 
	
– 3 Tsp Garlic Powder 
	
– 3 Tsp Onion Powder 
	
– 3 Tsp Paprika 
	
– 3 Tsp Olive Oil (15ml)
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Get your boneless, skinless chicken thighs and 
place them in a large bowl. Season with salt, Italian 
herbs, chilli flakes, parsley, garlic powder, onion 
powder and paprika, then drizzle over the olive oil. 
Mix thoroughly until the chicken is well coated and 
fully combined.
	
z S T E P 2 
Place all the chicken thighs flat and evenly spread 
on a lined sheet pan. Oven bake or air fry at 200°C / 
400°F for 22-25 minutes. The chicken should come 
out golden and charred on the edges.
	
z S T E P 3
Once cooked, let the chicken rest for 4-5 minutes. 
For best meal prep results, store the chicken whole 
to help retain juices and prevent drying out over the 
week. Make sure to include any tray juices when 
storing for extra flavour and moisture. Slice into 
cubes only when serving, or leave whole depending 
on preference. Store in large airtight glass containers 
and enjoy a delicious protein source ready for the 
week, ENJOY!
BEST WAYS TO SERVE AND PAIR THIS PROTEIN 
This Garlic Herb Cajun Chicken works extremely well in simple, flavourful combinations, for example:
	
–with Cajun butter rice and sweetcorn 
	
–with crispy roasted potatoes and a simple coleslaw 
	
–in a warm tortilla wrap with lettuce and pickles
SAUCE PAIRING SUGGESTIONS 
This chicken also pairs well with:
	
–lemon garlic yogurt dip 
	
–spicy mayo (light mayo mixed with a little hot sauce) 
	
–honey mustard yogurt sauce

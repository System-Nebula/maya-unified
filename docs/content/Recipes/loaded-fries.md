Loaded Fries!
Healthy Loaded Fries!! These taste absolutely incredible, so much flavor and will definitely satisfy your 
cravings! You can make this with any protein source such as ground chicken, turkey or even tofu!

How to make
1.	 Cut your potatoes into fry-shaped pieces. Toss them in olive oil, salt, and paprika, then spread them out 
evenly on a lined baking tray.
2.	 Bake in a preheated oven at 220°C for about 15–20 minutes, or until crispy and golden, flipping halfway 
through if needed.
3.	 While the fries are baking, cook your ground beef in a pan with chopped onions and bell pepper. Season 
with salt, cumin, garlic powder, paprika, oregano, and a splash of Worcestershire sauce.
4.	 Once the beef is nicely browned, sprinkle some low-fat cheese over the top and let it melt.
5.	 When everything is ready, plate the fries, top with the cheesy beef, then finish with jalapeños and spicy 
sauce. Enjoy!

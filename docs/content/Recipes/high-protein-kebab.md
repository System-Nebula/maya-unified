How to make
1.	 In a bowl combine ground beef with minced garlic, your preferred spices, and chopped coriander. Mix 
thoroughly until everything is well incorporated. 
2.	 Place the mixture in the fridge for about 10 minutes to help the meat firm up and hold its shape better.
3.	 Shape the meat into kebab-style pieces and bake in a preheated oven for around 10 minutes. Be careful not 
to overcook them, as they can dry out.
4.	 While the kebabs are cooking, prepare your flatbread by getting lettuce, sliced tomato, and red onion 
ready. Also, mix your white sauce if you haven’t already.
5.	 Once cooked, place three kebabs onto each flatbread, add the veggies, drizzle with white sauce and 
sriracha, then wrap it up and enjoy!

## Source Text

```
Salt and Pepper To Taste
	
z
Serve the Chicken On Top of 
the Rice With the Mint Sauce
How to make
1.	 Cut the chicken breast into thick cubes and marinate with all the listed 
ingredients for the chicken. Mix well to coat evenly, then thread the pieces 
onto 4 skewers, placing 4–5 cubes on each.
2.	 Wash the rice and chop the vegetables. Heat a pan over medium heat, 
add 1 teaspoon of olive oil, and sauté the vegetables for 2 minutes. Add 
the uncooked rice along with the listed seasonings and toast for 1 minute.
3.	 Pour in water, bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 8–10 
minutes until the rice is fully cooked. While the rice is cooking, prepare the 
mint sauce by blending all the sauce ingredients until smooth.
4.	 Once the rice is done, set it aside. In the same pan, increase the heat to 
high, spray with oil, and cook the chicken skewers for 4–5 minutes on 
each side until golden brown and cooked through.
5.	 Serve the chicken skewers over the rice, drizzle generously with the mint 
sauce, and enjoy!
You can use chicken thighs but 
keep in mind the extra calories 
and lower protein.
68

```

Serve with 140g (5oz) of 
Cooked Rice per Serving
How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken into cubes, then add all the seasonings along with 
Greek yogurt (or any yogurt you prefer). Mix well until the color changes, 
then marinate overnight for the best flavor.
2.	 In a pot, add washed uncooked basmati rice, water, turmeric, cumin, 
and salt. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce to a simmer and cook until 
the rice is fluffy and fully cooked.
3.	 Heat a pan on medium, cook the chicken for 4 minutes on each side 
until golden, then set aside.
4.	 In the same pan, add chopped onion with more seasoning and cook 
for 2 minutes. Then add tomato sauce or canned tomatoes along with 
light cream cheese, stirring well. Return the chicken to the pan.
5.	 Mix everything together and let it simmer for 2-3 minutes. Garnish with 
coriander, serve with the yellow rice, and enjoy!
You may need to add a little 
water or tomato sauce to add 
more sauce.

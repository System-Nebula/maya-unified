INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Garlic Herb Cajun Chicken
	
– 800g Raw Chicken Thighs boneless skinless
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 2 Tsp Italian Herbs
	
– 1.5 Tsp Chilli Flakes
	
– 1.5 Tsp Parsley
	
– 1.5 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1.5 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 1.5 Tsp Paprika
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
Light & Fluffy Mash
	
– 800g Raw White Potatoes peeled and 
diced into 1 inch chuncks »»
	» Continue
	
– 20g Room Temperature Light Butter
	
– 125ml (½ Cup) Warm Skimmed Milk
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt or to taste
	
– 1.5 Tsp Garlic Powder
Creamy Cajun Sauce
	
– 10g Light Butter
	
– ¾ Tsp Each Salt, Italian Herbs, Chilli Flakes, 
Garlic, Onion Powder, Parsley, Paprika
	
– 250ml (1 Cup) Skimmed Milk
	
– 40g Grated Parmigiano Cheese
	
– Good Handful Chopped Spinach
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
First get your boneless skinless chicken thighs, 
season with salt, Italian herbs, chilli flakes, parsley, 
garlic and onion powder, paprika and olive oil. Mix 
till well coated and combined
	
z S T E P 2 
Next peel all your potatoes then slice into chunks. 
Place them in a pot of cold water then boil for 12-14 
mins until fork tender. Drain all the water then cover 
with a lid for 5 mins to steam
	
z S T E P 3 
Uncover then mash the potatoes using a masher or 
ricer till smooth and fluffy. Note - don’t overly mash 
them!
	
z S T E P 4
Next add a knob of room temperature butter with 
warm milk, salt and garlic powder. Use a spatula to 
mix everything till smooth and combined
	
z S T E P 5
Now place all your chicken thighs flat and evenly 
spread on a lined sheet pan. Now oven bake or 
air fry for 22-25mins at 200C / 400F. The chicken 
should come out golden and charred!
	
z S T E P 6
For the cream sauce get a pan on low heat, melt 
down some butter then add the spice mix 
consisting of the same spices used for the 
chicken. Toast the spices for 2 mins in the butter
	
z S T E P 7
Add milk followed by cream cheese, parmesan 
cheese and chopped spinach. Mix gently till 
everything is melted, thickened and the spinach wilted
	
z S T E P 8
Finally when the sauce is ready, chop your chicken 
into delicious cubes then serve with the mashed 
potatoes topped with the sauce and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Oven Bake or Air Fry for 22-25mins at 200C / 400F. You can cook these on a pan in some butter too for 
4-5 mins each side on medium heat
	
z
Toast the spices in butter for 2mins on low heat then add milk, cream cheese, parmesan and spinach. Keep 
stirring till smooth, creamy and slightly thick. Let this warm up for 3-4 mins and your sauce is ready
	
z
Slice your chicken into cubes then serve with the fluffy mash. I recommend storing the creamy sauce 
separately and serving when ready to eat

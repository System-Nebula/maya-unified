Note: To make the rice fluffy, cover

How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken breast into cubes and season with all the listed spices 
and lime juice. Mix thoroughly until evenly coated—add more seasoning if the 
color looks too pale.
2.	 In a blender, combine avocado, yogurt, coriander, garlic powder, salt, and 
pepper. Blend until smooth and creamy, then store in the fridge if prepping ahead.
3.	 Rinse the basmati rice and add it to a pot with double the amount of water. 
Stir in the spices and tomato paste, bring to a light boil, then cover and 
simmer on low heat until fully cooked.
4.	 Heat a pan over medium heat with cooking spray or olive oil. Cook the 
chicken for about 3 minutes per side until golden brown and fully cooked. 
Set aside.
5.	 In the same pan, cook sliced onion and bell pepper for about 5 minutes until 
softened and slightly charred.
6.	 Plate the rice and top with chicken, shredded lettuce, sautéed onions 
and peppers, sweet corn, black beans, and a drizzle of green sauce. 
Divide into 4 portions and enjoy!
I would store the green sauce and

to 4-5 days, the sauce stores best 
in a sealed bottle.

How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken breasts into four burger-sized fillets. Season both sides 
with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder until evenly coated.
2.	 On one plate, mix the crushed cornflakes with the same seasonings. In a 
separate bowl, crack 1–2 eggs and whisk to create an egg wash. Dip each 
chicken fillet into the egg wash, then press into the cornflake crumbs until 
fully coated. Place the coated pieces on a baking tray.
3.	 Spray with cooking spray, then bake in the oven for 20 minutes at 200°C or 
air fry for 12–14 minutes at the same temperature until crispy and golden. 
4.	 While the chicken cooks, make the glaze. In a pan, combine orange juice, 
soy sauce, honey, sriracha, and orange zest. Bring to a boil, then stir in a 
cornflour slurry (cornflour mixed with water) and let it thicken.
5.	 Prepare the slaw by combining finely chopped cabbage, red onion, grated 
carrot, and green onion in a bowl. Add a splash of light soy sauce and rice 
vinegar. Mix well and let it sit to absorb the flavors.
6.	 Once the chicken is cooked, brush or spoon the glaze generously over each 
piece.
7.	 Assemble your burgers: spread spicy mayo on the bottom brioche bun, 
add the slaw, place the glazed crispy chicken on top, sprinkle with sesame 
seeds, and finish with the top bun. Enjoy!

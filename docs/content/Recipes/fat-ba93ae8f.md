INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Garlic Butter Chicken
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Chicken Breast, cubed
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 2 Tsp Parsley
	
– 1 Tsp Chilli Flakes
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Paprika
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
Cooking and Final Glaze
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Reduced Fat Butter
	
– 30g (2 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 1.5 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, chopped
Creamy Garlic Herb Potatoes
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Potatoes, cubed
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 1 Tsp Parsley
	
– 1 Tsp Chilli Flakes
	
– 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 1 Tsp Paprika
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil (Optional) or Cooking Spray
	
– 250g (9oz) Light Evaporated Milk
	
– 150g (5.3oz) Garlic & Herb Cream Cheese
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breasts into cubes. In a bowl, 
season them with salt, parsley, chilli flakes, garlic & 
onion powder, paprika, and olive oil. Mix thoroughly 
until the color deepens and all cubes are well coated.
	
z S T E P 2 
Wash and dice the potatoes into small, even 
cubes. In another bowl, season them with the same 
spices and a light drizzle of olive oil. Toss until every 
piece is evenly coated.
	
z S T E P 3 
Place the seasoned potatoes on a lined sheet 
pan (use baking paper) and bake or air fry at 
200°C/400°F for 20–22 minutes until golden and 
crispy.
	
z S T E P 4
Meanwhile, heat a pan over medium-high heat and 
melt 10g of butter. Add half of the marinated 
chicken in a single layer »»
	» Continue
and cook for 3–4 minutes per side until a golden-
brown crust forms.
	
z S T E P 5
Once cooked, lower the heat and add 5g extra 
butter, minced garlic, and parsley. Stir until each 
piece is evenly coated in the rich, garlic butter, 
then transfer to a plate. Repeat this process for 
the remaining chicken.
	
z S T E P 6
Back to the pan on low heat, add evaporated milk 
with garlic & herb cream cheese. Stir until the 
cheese melts and the sauce thickens slightly for 
about 5 minutes. Add the crispy potatoes in the 
sauce, gently mix until well combined and creamy
	
z S T E P 7
Finally serve and distribute into equal servings with

are ready for the week, ENJOY!

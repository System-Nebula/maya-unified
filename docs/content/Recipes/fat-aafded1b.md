INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
In a bowl, combine dark soy sauce, light soy sauce, 
honey, minced garlic, gochujang paste, and

a spatula to break it up as it browns. Once fully

chopped green onion. Stir and cook for another 
5 minutes until well combined, golden brown, and 
slightly crispy. Remove from heat.

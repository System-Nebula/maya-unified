z

z

z
Eggs (I Did 3 Eggs + 1 Egg White)
	
z
Handful Spinach
	
z
Siracha
How to make
1.	 Dice the potatoes into small pieces and chop all your vegetables. Heat 
a pan with cooking spray, add the potatoes, season with salt, pepper, 
and paprika, then cook for about 10 minutes. Spray more oil if needed 
and cover the pan to speed up cooking.
2.	 Push the potatoes to one side of the pan, add the chopped onion and 
bell pepper, season again, and cook for 2 minutes. Then mix everything 
together and add the chopped tomatoes.
3.	 Make a well in the center of the pan and pour in the eggs. Stir 
continuously over medium heat until the eggs begin to scramble.
4.	 Mix the eggs with the veggies, add a handful of chopped spinach, cook 
briefly until wilted, then serve hot. Don’t forget to add sriracha for that 
extra kick! Enjoy!
You can reduce cals by just 
using egg whites.

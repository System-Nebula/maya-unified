This is genuinely one of the best combos with 67g Protein! 
Thinly sliced steak strips coated in a velvety protein cheese 
sauce paired with the most incredible garlic parmesan rice. 
Perfect for meal prep

INGREDIENTS (5 SERVINGS)
Steak Marinade
	
– 1000g (35oz) Raw Top Sirloin 
Beef, sliced into thin strips
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 1.5 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 70g (2.4oz) Worcestershire Sauce
	
– 3 Tsp Olive Oil (Optional)
	
– 20g (1oz) Light Butter (for cooking)
Sautéed Veggies Mix
	
– 20g (1oz) Light Butter (for cooking)
	
– 200g (7.1oz) White Onion, sliced thin
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Green Bell Pepper, sliced thin
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Red Bell Pepper, sliced thin
	
– 1/2 Tsp Salt
	
– 1/2 Tsp Black Pepper
Cheese Sauce
	
– 8 Reduced Fat Cheese Slices
	
– 250g (8.8oz) 1% Milk
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Low Fat Cheddar
Garlic Parmesan Rice
	
– 10g (0.3oz) Light Butter
	
– 25g (1oz) Minced Garlic
	
– 260g (9oz) Uncooked Basmati Rice/
Approx 600g Cooked Rice
	
– 1/2 Tsp Chilli Flakes (Optional)
	
– 1 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, chopped
	
– 60g (2.1oz) Grated Parmesan
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1
Slice the beef steaks into extra thin strips and place 
in a mixing bowl. Season with salt, black pepper, 
garlic powder, onion powder, Worcestershire 
sauce, and olive oil. Mix well with your hands until 
evenly coated, then set aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2
Heat a pan over medium heat and melt some butter. 
Add sliced onions and mixed bell peppers, then 
cook for 5-7 minutes until softened and fragrant. 
Season with a pinch of salt and pepper, mix well, 
then set aside.
	
z S T E P 3
In the same pan, melt a little more butter on medium 
heat. Sauté minced garlic for 30 seconds, then add in 
your leftover cooked rice along with chilli flakes,  »»
plenty of grated parmesan, and freshly chopped 
parsley. Mix everything until fluffy, golden, and 
aromatic. Set aside once done.
	
z S T E P 4
Bring the heat up to medium-high, then add the 
marinated steak strips in a single layer. Sear for 4-6 
minutes until browned and slightly charred. Once 
cooked, add the sautéed onions and peppers back 
in. Then add light cheese slices, a splash of milk, 
and low-fat cheddar cheese. Mix thoroughly until 
the cheese is melted, creamy, and well combined 
with the steak and veggies.
	
z S T E P 5
Serve and divide the garlic parmesan rice and 
Philly cheesy steak mix into 5 equal portions. Your 
high-protein meals are ready to ENJOY!

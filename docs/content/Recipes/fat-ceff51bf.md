INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Chicken Marinade
	
– 700g (25oz) Raw Chicken Breast, cubed
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1.5 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Paprika
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Corn Flour/Cornstarch
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil (for cooking)
Korean Glaze
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Dark Soy Sauce
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Water
	
– 25g (1oz) Honey »»

– 40g (1.4oz) Gochujang Paste
 Pickled Cucumbers
	
– 400g (14oz) Cucumber, thinly sliced
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 20g (0.7oz) Dark Soy Sauce
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Rice Vinegar
	
– 1 Tsp Chilli Flakes (omit for no spice)
	
– 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1 Tbsp Sesame Seeds
Serve With
	
– 210g (7.4oz) Uncooked White Rice/560g 
Cooked Rice (140g per serving)
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breasts into bite-sized cubes, 
then season with garlic powder, black pepper, 
paprika, salt, and cornflour. Mix until evenly coated 
then set aside.
	
z S T E P 2 
Cook your rice by bringing a pot of water to a boil 
and simmering for 8–10 minutes until fully cooked. I 
use basmati rice, but jasmine rice works too! Once 
done, set aside.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan over medium heat, add olive oil, and 
cook the coated chicken for 4 minutes per side 
until golden brown with a crispy crust. Set aside and 
repeat for any remaining chicken.
	
z S T E P 4
In the same pan on low heat, add soy sauce mixed 
with water, a drizzle of honey, and gochujang 
paste. Stir gently until smooth and well combined. 
Let it simmer for 2–3 minutes until thickened, then 
return the crispy chicken and mix until fully coated 
in the spicy glaze.
	
z S T E P 5
For the cucumber salad, thinly slice cucumber 
and place in a bowl. Add light & dark soy sauce, 
rice vinegar, garlic powder, chili flakes, and a 
generous amount of sesame seeds. Mix well until 
combined and fragrant. Serve alongside the glazed 
chicken and hot rice, then ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Ensure the chicken is fully coated in cornflour for extra crispiness, or skip it if unavailable.
	
z
When making the glaze, add ingredients over low heat, stirring continuously until it lightly bubbles, then let it 
reduce for 3–4 minutes until thickened.
	
z
Store the cucumber salad separately and reheat the meal without it—it’s best served cold.

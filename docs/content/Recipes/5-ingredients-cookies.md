5 Ingredients Cookies
One of the easiest recipes for making healthy cookies!
Macros per serving
85 CALORIES
 5g PROTEIN
14g  CARBS
2g FAT
Ingredients (8 medium cookies)
	
z
1.5 Medium Ripe Bananas
	
z
80g (2.8oz) Rolled Oats
	
z
1 Scoop Protein Powder (Vanilla Flavoured)
	
z
1 Whole Egg
	
z
Dark Chocolate Chips (Low Sugar)
How to make
1.	 In a bowl, mash 2 ripe bananas with a fork, then add the egg, oats, and a scoop of protein powder. Mix 
thoroughly until fully combined, then fold in some dark chocolate chips.
2.	 Line a baking tray and spoon the mixture into 8 evenly sized cookies. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C for 
8–10 minutes. Keep an eye on them—don’t overbake. Let cool slightly and enjoy warm!

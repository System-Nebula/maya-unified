Grated Parmigiano Reggiano
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Red Onion, chopped

– Handful Walnuts, crushed 
White Sauce
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Low Fat Yogurt
	
– 80g (2.8oz) 50% Fat Mayo
	
– 5 Roasted Garlic Cloves
	
– 80g (2.8oz) Light Feta Cheese
	
– 20g (1oz) Honey
	
– Juice of ¼ Lemon
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Butterfly cut the chicken breasts and season both 
sides evenly with oregano, garlic powder, chilli 
flakes, paprika, and olive oil. Set aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2 
Wash and chop the broccoli into small florets. 
Place on a tray and toss with chilli flakes, garlic 
powder, salt, pepper, grated Parmesan, thinly 
sliced red onion, and olive oil.
	
z S T E P 3 
Arrange 5 whole garlic cloves in the center of the 
tray. Air fry or oven bake the broccoli for 15–16 
minutes at 200°C (400°F).
	
z S T E P 4
While the broccoli cooks, heat a pan over medium »»
	» Continue
heat and cook the seasoned chicken for 5–6 
minutes per side until golden and juicy. Let the 
chicken rest, then cut it into cubes.
	
z S T E P 5
Remove the roasted garlic cloves from the tray. In a 
blender, combine yogurt, mayo, the roasted garlic, 
crumbled feta, honey, and lemon juice. Blend until 
completely smooth to create your white sauce.
	
z S T E P 6 
Layer the broccoli on a serving dish, top with 
the chicken cubes, and drizzle with the white 
sauce. Gently mix with two large spoons until well 
combined. Garnish with crushed walnuts and 
serve. ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
For the juiciest chicken, cook on medium heat for 5–6 minutes per side, then let it rest before chopping 
into cubes.
	
z
Oven-bake or air-fry the broccoli at 200°C/400°F for 15–16 minutes, taking care not to burn it to avoid a 
bitter flavor.
	
z
This dish stores well in the fridge and tastes great cold, or reheat in the air fryer at 180°C/370°F for 5 
minutes. It pairs wonderfully with sourdough bread.

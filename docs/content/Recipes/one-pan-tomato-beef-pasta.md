One-Pan Tomato Beef Pasta! Perfect for meal prep all in one pan, it’s so easy to make! Saves

480 CALORIES

1 Onion, Chopped

z
1 Cup Pasta Sauce
	
z
1 Bell Pepper, Chopped
	
z
2 Cups Water
	
z
120-150g (5.3oz) Raw Pasta
(Wholewheat Preferably)
	
z
Extra Seasoning to Taste
	
z
Sprinkle Shredded Mozzarella (Optional)
How to make
1.	 Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add ground beef and 
chopped onions, then season with garlic powder, salt, pepper, Italian 
herbs, and Worcestershire sauce. Cook until the beef is browned.
2.	 Add pasta sauce or canned tomatoes, chopped bell peppers, water, 
and uncooked pasta directly into the pan.
3.	 Stir everything together, then add more seasoning if needed.
4.	 Cover the pan and let it simmer for 15–18 minutes, stirring occasionally 
to prevent sticking.
5.	 Once the pasta is fully cooked and the sauce has thickened, garnish 
with parsley and a sprinkle of mozzarella if desired. Serve and enjoy!

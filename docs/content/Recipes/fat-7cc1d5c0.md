INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Taco Spice Mix (1 Serving)
	
– 1 Tsp each: Salt, Black Pepper, 
Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, 
Paprika, Oregano, Cumin
	
– ½ or 1 Tsp Chilli Powder
Juicy Jalapeno Beef
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw 95% 
Ground/Mince Beef
	
– 1 Serving Taco Spice Mix
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Tomato Paste
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Chopped 
Pickled Jalapeños
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Jalapeño Juice
	
– 25g (1oz) Reduced Fat 
Butter (for cooking)
Crispy Taco Fries
	
– 900g Raw Potatoes, 
cut into thin fries
	
– 1 Serving Taco Seasoning Mix
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oill
Nacho Cheese Sauce
	
– 180g (6.4oz) Light 
Evaporated Milk
	
– 5x20g Slices (100g/3.5oz) 
American Cheese Slices
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Light Cheddar 
or Sharp Cheddar
	
– 20g (1oz) Jalapeño 
Juice (Optional)
Fresh Side Salad
	
– 150g (5.3oz) Tomatoes, chopped
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Red 
Onion, chopped
	
– 150g (5.3oz) Red Bell 
Pepper, chopped
	
– 150g (5.3oz) Green Bell 
Pepper, chopped
	
– 200g (7.1oz) Avocados, cubed
	
– 25g (1oz) Fresh 
Coriander, chopped
	
– Juice of 1 Lime
	
– 15g (0.5oz) Honey
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 1 Tsp Black Pepper
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the washed potatoes into thin French fries. 
Mix with 1 serving of taco spice mix and a drizzle of 
olive oil until evenly coated.
	
z S T E P 2 
Arrange the fries on a lined sheet pan and oven 
bake or air fry at 200°C/400°F for 20–25 minutes 
until golden and crispy.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan over medium-high heat, add butter, and 
cook all the ground beef for 5–7 minutes, breaking 
it up with a spatula until browned
	
z S T E P 4
Once the beef is browned, stir in 1 serving of taco 
spice mix, tomato paste, chopped jalapeños, and 
jalapeño juice. Cook for 2 more minutes until the 
mixture is juicy and flavours intensify then set aside
	
z S T E P 5
Using the same pan on low heat, add evaporated 
milk, American cheese slices, sharp cheddar, and 
a splash of jalapeño juice. Stir until the cheeses 
melt and the sauce becomes silky smooth. (This 
sauce can be stored in a container and refrigerated 
for later use)
	
z S T E P 6 
Prepare the side salad by combining chopped 
tomatoes, red onion, red and green bell peppers, 
avocado, coriander, lime juice, honey, salt, and 
black pepper in a bowl. Toss until well combined 
and store separately.
	
z S T E P 7
To serve, distribute the crispy taco fries evenly and 
top with the juicy jalapeño beef. Drizzle generously 
with the nacho cheese sauce and serve alongside 
the fresh salad. ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Prepare the nacho cheese sauce on low heat, stirring until thick and smooth. Store in a bottle and reheat 
before use, as it will harden when cooled.
	
z
Keep the side salad in a separate container—it stays fresh for 4–5 days.
	
z
For reheating, warm the beef and fries in a pan, or air fry the fries for 4–5 minutes for extra crispiness.

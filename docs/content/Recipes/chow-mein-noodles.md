How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken breast into thin strips, then marinate with garlic 
powder, paprika, black pepper, and soy sauce. Mix well and set aside.
2.	 In a bowl, mix together soy sauce, oyster sauce, garlic powder, 
sesame oil, and water to make the stir-fry sauce.
3.	 Heat a pan on medium with cooking spray or a little oil, then cook the 
marinated chicken for 6–8 minutes until golden brown. Remove and 
set aside.
4.	 In the same pan, stir-fry sliced onions and bell peppers for 5 minutes. 
Add cooked egg noodles and pour in the prepared sauce. Mix until 
everything is well coated.
5.	 Return the cooked chicken to the pan, add chopped green onions 
and bean sprouts, mix everything one last time, then serve and enjoy!

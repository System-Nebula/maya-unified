Long Grain Rice

Crunchy Cucumber Salad
	
– 400g (14.1oz) Cucumber, thinly sliced
	
– 300g (10.6oz) Carrots, grated
	
– 250g (8.8oz) Red Onion, thinly sliced
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Green Onion, chopped
	
– 25g (1oz) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 20g (1oz) Rice Vinegar
	
– 25g (1oz) Sriracha
	
– 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 2 Tbsp Sesame Seeds
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1
In a bowl, combine light and dark soy sauce, garlic 
and ginger paste, gochujang, honey, oyster sauce, 
rice vinegar, and sesame seeds. Mix until fully 
combined, then set aside.
	
z S T E P 2
Heat a pan on medium, melt some butter, and cook 
chopped onions for 5 minutes until soft. Increase 
heat to high, add lean ground beef, and cook for 8 
minutes until browned, using a spatula to break it up.
	
z S T E P 3
Lower the heat to medium, pour in the prepared 
sauce, and cook for 4-5 minutes until the beef turns 
golden and slightly caramelised. Finish with chopped 
green onion and sesame seeds, then set aside.
	
z S T E P 4
For the Korean spicy sauce, mix low-fat yogurt, 
gochujang, and honey in a bowl. Add a splash of 
milk to loosen the consistency if needed. Bottle and 
store for future use.
	
z S T E P 5
In a large bowl, combine thinly sliced cucumber, 
grated carrots, red onion, green onion, light soy 
sauce, sriracha, rice vinegar, and garlic powder. 
Toss gently until evenly coated. Store separately to 
keep fresh and make reheating easier.
	
z S T E P 6
Serve and divide the Korean beef, white rice, 
crunchy slaw, and spicy sauce into 5 portions. 
Your high-protein Korean-style meals are ready to 
ENJOY!

– 2 Tsp Onion Powder

– 2 Tsp Olive Oil (optional)

– 1 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, finely chopped
Onion Butter Fried Rice
	
– 280g (10oz) Uncooked White

– 150g (5.3oz) White Onion, finely chopped
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Spring Onion, white part chopped
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Carrots, chopped »»

– Extra Seasoning: 1 Tsp each Black

– 2 Whole Large Eggs
	
– 20g (1oz) Dark Soy Sauce (add more if needed)
	
– 20g (1oz) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Oyster Sauce
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Spring Onion, green part chopped
Garlic Yum Yum Sauce (enough for 8+ meals)
	
– 150g (5.3oz) Light Mayo
	
– 75g (2.7oz) Reduced Sugar Ketchup
	
– 75g (2.7oz) Sriracha
	
– 1 Tsp Minced Garlic
	
– 1/2 Tsp Ginger Powder
	
– 1 Tbsp Rice Vinegar
	
– Dash of Milk or Water for desired consistency
Garnish
	
– Fresh Parsley chopped
	
– Chilli & Garlic grinder mix
	
– Sesame Seeds
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the boneless, skinless chicken thighs into 
bite-sized cubes. Season with black pepper, chilli 
flakes, garlic powder, onion powder, light and 
dark soy sauce, and olive oil. Mix thoroughly until 
well coated and the colour deepens. Set aside to 
marinate.
	
z S T E P 2 
To make the Garlic Yum Yum sauce, combine 
light mayo, ketchup, sriracha, minced garlic, 
ginger powder, and rice vinegar in a bowl. Mix until 
completely smooth. Add a splash of milk or water 
to adjust the consistency if needed. Bottle and store 
in the fridge to use throughout the week.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan over medium heat and melt a little 
butter. Cook the marinated chicken for about 5 
minutes per side until golden, crispy, and cooked 
through. Add a handful of freshly chopped parsley, 
stir to coat the chicken well, then set aside to rest.
	
z S T E P 4
In the same pan, add a knob of butter and the 
chopped onion. Cook on medium heat for 8 to 10 
minutes, »»
	» Continue
stirring halfway through, until softened and 
caramelised.
	
z S T E P 5
Add chopped green onion (white part) and finely 
chopped carrots. Season with black pepper, 
onion powder, and parsley. Stir everything 
together until fragrant, then push the vegetables to 
one side of the pan.
	
z S T E P 6
In the empty side of the pan, pour in the beaten 
eggs. Stir continuously until scrambled and fully 
cooked, then mix in with the sautéed vegetables.
	
z S T E P 7
Add your leftover cooked rice to the pan, followed 
by soy sauce, oyster sauce, and chopped green 
onion (green part). Mix everything thoroughly until 
well combined and each grain of rice catches a deep 
colour. Add more soy sauce, if needed.
	
z S T E P 8
Serve the fried rice with the crispy chicken in 5 
equal portions. Drizzle with Garlic Yum Yum sauce, 
garnish with sesame seeds, and ENJOY!

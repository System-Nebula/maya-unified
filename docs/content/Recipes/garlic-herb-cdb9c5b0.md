INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 : M A R I N AT E T H E C H I C K E N
Place the chicken thighs in a large mixing bowl. Add 
garlic paste, salt, black pepper, mixed herbs, 
parsley, paprika, chilli flakes, and olive oil. Mix 
thoroughly until every piece is coated and the colour 
deepens. Set aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2 : C O O K U N T I L G O L D E N
Heat a pan over medium heat and melt the butter. Add 
the chicken thighs in a single layer and cook for  »»
	» Continue
5-6 minutes per side until a deep golden crust forms 
and the chicken is cooked through. Remove and set 
aside to rest, then repeat with the remaining chicken.
	
z S T E P 3 : M E A L P R E P S TO R AG E
Keep the chicken whole for storing (this prevents 
moisture loss). Let it rest fully, then transfer the thighs 
to airtight glass containers along with the buttery pan 
juices for flavour. Slice into cubes only when serving 
to keep each portion juicy and tender. ENJOY!
BEST WAYS TO SERVE AND PAIR THIS PROTEIN 
This Garlic Herb Chicken works extremely well with rich, creamy, fragrant sides that complement its herb-
forward flavour. For example:
	
–creamy mashed potatoes (with garlic, butter, and a splash of warm milk) 
	
–parmesan butter rice (fragrant, golden, and ultra comforting)
SAUCE PAIRING SUGGESTIONS 
This chicken also pairs well with:
	
–creamy garlic mushroom sauce 
	
–lemon herb yogurt sauce 
	
–parmesan cream sauce (lightened with milk or broth)

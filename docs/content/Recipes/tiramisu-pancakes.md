Tiramisu Pancakes
If you love Tiramisu, you can’t go wrong with this one! Perfect Breakfast for Coffee Lovers!
Macros per serving
459 CALORIES
43g PROTEIN

z

z

z

z
1 Coffee Shot (1 Tsp Instant Coffee 
mixed with 2 Tbsp Almond Milk)
	
z
100ml (3.5oz) Almond Milk
	
z
Cooking Spray
Filling:
	
z
1 Tsp Instant Coffee (mixed in water)
	
z
150g (5.3oz) Fat Free Vanilla Greek Yogurt (any 
yogurt works with some vanilla extract)
	
z
Sprinkle of Cocoa Powder
How to make
1.	 Add all your ingredients to a blender cup, starting with the dry ingredients followed by the wet ones. Blend 
for 1–2 minutes until the batter is completely smooth and lump-free.
2.	 Heat a non-stick pan over medium-low heat and lightly spray with oil. Pour in 1/4 cup of the pancake batter 
and cook for about 2 minutes until bubbles form and the edges are set. Flip and cook for another minute. 
Repeat with the remaining batter to make 4 pancakes.
3.	 Once all pancakes are done, stack them layer by layer—starting with a pancake, then a drizzle of coffee, 
followed by a spoonful of yogurt. Repeat for all layers. On the final pancake, dust with cocoa powder and 
enjoy!

– 3 Tbsp (25g / 0.9oz) Sesame Seeds
Gochujang Spicy Sauce
	
– 80g Light Mayo (2.8oz / ~5 Tbsp) 
	
– 100g Any Yogurt (3.5oz / ~6-7 Tbsp) 
	
– 1 Tbsp Gochujang Paste (15g / 0.5oz) 
	
– 2 Tsp Rice Vinegar (10g / 0.35oz) 
	
– 20g Honey (0.7oz / 1 Tbsp)
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 : M A R I N AT E T H E C H I C K E N
Trim any excess fat from your boneless chicken 
thighs, then place them in a large mixing bowl. 
Add black pepper, garlic paste, ginger paste, 
gochujang, sriracha, sesame seeds, and light 
soy sauce. Mix thoroughly until the chicken is 
fully coated and the colour deepens. Set aside 
to marinate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for 
more intense flavour. You can also freeze the raw 
marinated chicken and defrost it whenever you 
need a quick protein base for the week.
	
z S T E P 2 : C O O K T H E C H I C K E N
When ready to cook, spread the marinated chicken 
thighs evenly in a single layer on a lined sheet pan 
(baking paper/parchment paper). Oven bake or air 
fry at 200°C / 400°F for 16-18 minutes, until the 
chicken is cooked through, juicy, and has a light 
golden char on the edges.
	
z S T E P 3 : R E S T T H E C H I C K E N 
( K E E P W H O L E F O R S TO R AG E )
Let the chicken thighs rest for 3-4 minutes, then leave 
them whole for storage. Keeping the thighs intact 
helps retain their natural juices and prevents them 
from drying out over the week. Make sure to pack all 
the resting juices from the sheet pan together with 
the chicken when storing. These juices are essential for 
extra flavour and help keep the chicken moist during 
storage and reheating, ensuring nothing gets wasted.
	
z S T E P 4 : S TO R E F O R T H E W E E K
Allow the chicken to cool completely, then store 
the whole pieces in airtight glass containers and 
refrigerate for the week. Slice or cube the chicken 
only when serving to maintain maximum tenderness 
and juiciness. ENJOY!
BEST WAYS TO SERVE AND PAIR THIS PROTEIN 
This Korean BBQ chicken is very versatile and works extremely well in a variety of meal prep 
combinations, for example: 
	
–Korean rice bowls with cucumber slaw, pickled carrots and spring onions 
	
–tortilla wraps or Korean inspired tacos with shredded lettuce and slaw 
	
–lettuce wraps with rice and a spoon of gochujang sauce
SAUCE PAIRING SUGGESTIONS 
This chicken also pairs well with: 
	
–gochujang spicy sauce (included in the recipe) 
	
–sesame soy drizzle 
	
–kimchi or pickled vegetables for contrast

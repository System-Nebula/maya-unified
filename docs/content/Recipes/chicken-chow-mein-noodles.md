How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken into thin strips, then marinate with paprika, black pepper, 
garlic powder, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Mix well and let it marinate for as 
long as possible to enhance the flavor.
2.	 Slice all the vegetables while the chicken marinates. Cook the noodles 
according to the package instructions—usually by boiling and simmering for 4–5 
minutes. Drain the noodles and set them aside.
3.	 Heat a pan over medium heat and add a light coating of cooking spray. Cook the 
marinated chicken for 6–8 minutes, or until golden brown and fully cooked. 
Remove and set aside.
4.	 In the same pan, add the sliced onion, bell pepper, and green onion. Stir-fry 
for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened.
5.	 Add minced ginger, soy sauce, oyster sauce, rice vinegar, and the cooked 
noodles to the pan. Stir everything together until the noodles are evenly coated 
and well combined.
6.	 Add the cooked chicken back into the pan along with bean sprouts. Mix 
once more, ensuring everything is evenly incorporated.
7.	 Garnish with sesame seeds and chilli flakes. Distribute into 4 equal servings 
and enjoy!

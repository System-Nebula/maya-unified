How to make
1.	 Heat a pan over medium heat, spray lightly with oil, and crack in your

2.	 Toast your bread and place it on a plate. Spread a layer of cream 
cheese on the toast, then add fresh spinach, avocado slices, and a 
drizzle of sriracha.
3.	 Place the cooked eggs on top of everything.
4.	 It’s ready to enjoy, simple, hearty, and full of flavour!

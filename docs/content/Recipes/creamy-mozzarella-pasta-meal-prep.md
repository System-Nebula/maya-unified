How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken into cubes and season with all the listed spices. Mix 
well until evenly coated.
2.	 Heat a pan on medium heat with olive oil or cooking spray. Cook the 
chicken for 3–4 minutes on each side until golden brown, then set aside.
3.	 Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Cook your pasta for 8–9 minutes 
until al dente. Reserve ½ cup of pasta water, then drain the rest.
4.	 In the same pan, add chopped garlic and onion. Sauté for about 2 
minutes until fragrant.
5.	 Lower the heat completely and add milk, cream cheese, reserved 
pasta water, and mozzarella. Stir gently until the sauce thickens and 
becomes smooth.
6.	 Add the cooked pasta and chicken to the sauce. Mix everything 
together, garnish with chili flakes and parsley, then portion into 4 
servings and enjoy!

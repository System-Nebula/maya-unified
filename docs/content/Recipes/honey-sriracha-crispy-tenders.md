Honey Sriracha Crispy Tenders
A new healthier way for you to enjoy crispy chicken tenders USING NO OIL! 
Went through a lot of testing for the sauce and these come out perfect every time!

 225 ml (8oz) Low Fat Milk

Crumbs:
	
z

Cornflakes
	
z

z

z
1 Tsp Salt and Pepper
	
z

Glaze:
	
z

z
1/2 Lemon Juice
How to make
1.	 Cut the chicken breast into strips (about 6 per breast) and place them 
in a bowl. Add all the marinade ingredients, mix well to coat evenly, and 
let it marinate for as long as possible for maximum flavor.
2.	 Preheat the oven to 200°C (390°F). In a separate dish, combine 
cornflake crumbs with the same spices used in the marinade along 
with extra paprika. Mix thoroughly to season the coating.
3.	 Take each chicken strip from the marinade and press it into the crumb 
mixture, ensuring each piece is fully coated. Place the coated strips 
onto a wire rack set over a baking tray.
4.	 Bake in the preheated oven for 18 minutes until crispy and cooked 
through. While the chicken is baking, prepare the glaze by mixing all the 
glaze ingredients in a bowl until smooth.
5.	 Once the chicken tenders are done, coat each piece generously with 
the glaze. Serve immediately and enjoy!
This makes between 12-15 strips 
depending on how big your 
chicken breast is.
This recipe uses corn flakes, NOT 
frosted flakes.

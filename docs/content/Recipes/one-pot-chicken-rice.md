How to make
1.	 Butterfly cut your chicken breasts and season generously with Cajun seasoning. Add olive oil and rub 
until both sides are evenly coated.
2.	 Heat a pan over high heat, add the chicken, and cook for 5 minutes. Flip the chicken, then immediately 
lower the heat.
3.	 Add diced onion, bell pepper, chili pepper, uncooked rice, and 1.5 to 2 cups of water to the pan. Season 
again with Cajun seasoning for extra flavour.
4.	 Cover the pan and let it simmer for 10–12 minutes. At the 6-minute mark, uncover and stir the rice to 
prevent sticking and ensure even cooking.
5.	 Once the rice is fully cooked and the chicken is tender, remove from heat and serve hot. Enjoy!

## Source Text

```
Fresh or Dried Parsley
	
z
Or Serve with 140g 
(5oz) Basmati Rice
How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken into cubes, then marinate with all the listed 
ingredients. Mix until the colour changes and set aside.
2.	 In a pan over medium heat, add light butter and sliced onion with the 
same seasonings. Cook for 2 minutes, then add chopped tomatoes, 
peanut butter, and yogurt. Mix until fully combined.
3.	 Let the mixture cool for 5 minutes, then transfer to a blender and blend 
in 10–15 second increments until smooth and creamy.
4.	 In the same pan, cook the marinated chicken on high heat for 4 
minutes. Lower the heat, pour in the blended sauce, mix, and simmer 
for another 2–3 minutes.
5.	 To make the garlic naan, combine flour, yogurt, baking powder, salt, 
and garlic in a bowl. Mix and knead into a smooth dough.
6.	 Roll out the dough into thin rounds, sprinkle with parsley, and cook on a 
pan for 2–3 minutes on each side. Serve with the chicken and ENJOY!
When mixing the dough, if it 
gets too sticky, add flour to your 
hands to make it easier to work 
with the dough.
You can use a glass bottle to roll 
out the dough.
Make sure you let the mix cool 
down before adding into you 
blender. Some blenders can 
explode if it’s hot.
```

How to make
1.	 Butterfly cut the chicken breast into thinner fillets. Season both sides with smoked paprika, onion 
powder, and garlic powder, rubbing the spices in well.
2.	 In one bowl, add crushed cornflakes and season with the same spices used for the chicken. In a second 
bowl, crack in 2 eggs and whisk until smooth.
3.	 Dip each chicken fillet into the egg wash, then coat thoroughly in the seasoned cornflakes. Place the 
coated chicken onto a baking tray (preferably with a wired rack).
4.	 Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C (390°F) for 18–20 minutes or air fry at 180°C (360°F) for 18 minutes until 
golden and crispy.
5.	 While the chicken cooks, toast the brioche buns and prep your toppings: lettuce, tomato, pickles, light 
cheese, and special sauce.
6.	 Assemble the burger: spread the sauce on the buns, layer with lettuce, tomato, crispy chicken, cheese, 
and pickles. Serve & ENJOY!

Lean Stuffed Peppers
Struggling to lose weight? These are perfect, low cal, high protein, filling and Delicious!
Macros per serving
210 CALORIES
30g PROTEIN
8g  CARBS
6g FAT
Ingredients 
	
z
300g (10.6oz) 2 Chicken Breast, cut into small pieces
	
z
4 Bell Peppers
	
z
1 Tsp Salt & Pepper
	
z
1 Tsp Olive Oil
	
z
1 Tbsp Garlic Powder
	
z

z
1 Onion, Finely Chopped
	
z
1/2 Courgette/Zucchini, Chopped
Toppings: 
	
z
Jalapeños 
	
z
Low Fat Cheese
How to make
1.	 Cut the chicken into small pieces and place in a bowl. Add salt, pepper, olive oil, garlic, and paprika, then 
mix thoroughly until well coated. Set aside to marinate briefly.
2.	 Heat a pan on high, spray with oil, then add chopped onions and courgette/zucchini. Season with the 
same spices as the chicken and cook for 5 minutes until slightly softened. Remove and set aside.
3.	 In the same pan, add the marinated chicken and cook for about 5 minutes until golden brown. Add the 
cooked vegetables back in, mix everything together, then turn off the heat.
4.	 Slice bell peppers in half, remove the seeds, and place them on a lined baking tray. Fill each pepper with 
the chicken and veggie mixture, then bake at 180°C (350°F) for 15 minutes.
5.	 Remove the tray, top each pepper with jalapeños and low-fat cheese, and return to the oven for another 
5 minutes until the cheese is melted. Serve hot and enjoy!

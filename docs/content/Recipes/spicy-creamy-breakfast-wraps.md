How to make
1.	 Bring a pot of water to a gentle boil and carefully add 6 eggs. Boil for 8 minutes, then immediately transfer 
the eggs to a bowl of cold water to cool. Once cooled, peel the eggs and separate the yolks from the whites 
into two separate bowls.
2.	 Mash the egg yolks until smooth, then add a spoonful of Greek yogurt or light mayo, a squeeze of mustard 
(optional), salt, pepper, and a pinch of paprika. Mix until it becomes a creamy, well-seasoned base.
3.	 Finely chop the egg whites and add them to the yolk mixture. Stir thoroughly to combine into a cohesive, 
creamy egg salad.
4.	 Lay out your tortilla wrap of choice. Add a layer of shredded lettuce, a generous spoonful of the egg 
salad, a sprinkle of grated cheese, and a touch of fresh parsley. Fold the wrap, toast it in a pan on medium 
heat until golden and warm, then slice and enjoy!

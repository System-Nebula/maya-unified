TAP FOR VIDEO
THE MEAL PREP COOK BOOK 
163
Return to Table of Contents  
INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Shrimp Marinade
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Argentinian Shrimp
	
– 20g (1.5 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 2 Tsp Italian Herbs
	
– 1 Tsp Chilli Flakes
	
– 2 Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– Juice of ¾ Lemon
	
– 2-3 Tsp Olive Oil
Cooking & Finishing
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Reduced Fat Butter (for cooking)
	
– 1 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, chopped
	
– Extra Juice of ½ Lemon
Creamy Sauce
	
– 250g (8.8oz) 1% Milk
	
– 120g (4.2oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Grated Parmigiano Reggiano
	
– ¾ Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– ¾ Tsp Paprika
	
– ¾ Tsp Chilli Flakes
	
– ¾ Tsp Salt
	
– 2 Tsp Italian Herbs
	
– 25g (1oz) Pasta Water
	
– Handful Spinach
Pasta
	
– 230g (8.1oz) Uncooked Linguine 
Pasta (or pasta of choice)
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Marinate the raw shrimp by mixing with minced 
garlic, Italian herbs, chilli flakes, smoked paprika, 
salt, lemon juice, and olive oil. Mix thoroughly and 
set aside.
	
z S T E P 2 
Cook your linguine (or pasta of your choice) in 
boiling water for 7-8 minutes until al dente. Reserve 
some pasta water for the sauce, then drain the 
pasta.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan over medium heat and add a little light 
butter. Sauté the marinated shrimp for about 
2 minutes per side until golden. Lower the heat, 
then add a bit more butter, fresh parsley, and a 
squeeze of lemon juice. Toss to coat, then remove 
the shrimp from the pan and set aside.
	
z S T E P 4
In the same pan on low heat, combine milk, light 
cream cheese, Parmesan, extra seasoning, and 
some reserved pasta water. Stir continuously until 
the sauce thickens and becomes creamy.
	
z S T E P 5
Add the cooked pasta along with a handful of 
spinach, the cooked shrimp, extra parsley, and 
another squeeze of lemon juice. Toss everything 
until well combined, top with additional Parmesan, 
and serve. ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Marinate shrimp with garlic powder (if desired) and skip olive oil for lower calories..
	
z
After cooking, reduce heat and add butter, fresh parsley, and a squeeze of lemon for extra flavor.
	
z
When preparing the sauce, keep the heat low before adding milk and cream cheese; stir slowly until 
thickened, then fold in the pasta.

z
Low Fat Cream Cheese
How to make
1.	 Crack the eggs into a bowl, add a splash of almond or low fat milk, and whisk until fully combined and 
slightly frothy.
2.	 In a pan over medium heat, cook the turkey slices until crispy, then set aside on a plate.
3.	 Using the same pan, add halved cherry tomatoes and season with salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic 
powder. Cook for about 2 minutes, then add spinach and cover until it wilts.
4.	 Add the cooked veggies and turkey to the egg mixture, gently stirring to combine.
5.	 Pour the egg mixture back into the pan over low heat. Add a few small dollops of cream cheese and lightly 
season the top. Cook gently until set, or finish under the grill for 2 minutes until lightly golden. Serve and 
enjoy!

How to make
1.	 Preheat your oven or air fryer to 180°C (350°F). Line a circular baking 
dish or pan with baking paper, then gently press a tortilla wrap into the 
dish, shaping it to fit the base and sides like a crust.
2.	 Pour the whisked eggs into the tortilla base. Evenly distribute chopped 
cherry tomatoes, bell pepper, and red onion over the top. Season 
with salt, pepper, oregano, and chilli flakes, then sprinkle a generous 
layer of cheese.
3.	 Bake or air fry for about 10 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the 
cheese is melted and golden.
4.	 Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, then slice into portions and 
serve warm. Enjoy!

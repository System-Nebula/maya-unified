Fresh Crunchy Slaw

– 250g (5.3oz) Red Onion, thinly sliced

– 200g (7oz) Pickled Gherkins, thinly sliced
	
– 2 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, chopped
	
– 1 Tsp each Salt & Black Pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Place boneless, skinless chicken thighs into a large 
mixing bowl. Add minced garlic, your homemade 
shawarma spice mix, tomato paste, olive oil, and a 
squeeze of fresh lemon juice. Mix thoroughly until 
the chicken is evenly coated and vibrant in colour. 
Set aside to marinate for at least 30 minutes-or 
longer for deeper flavour. Be patient-it’s worth it!
	
z S T E P 2 
For the garlic white sauce, combine yogurt, light 
mayo, honey, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, 
and a splash of pickle juice for added tang. Mix until 
smooth and creamy, then bottle it up and store in the 
fridge. It’ll keep well for multiple meals throughout the 
week.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan on medium, melt a knob of butter, then 
sauté minced garlic and chopped onions for 5–6 
minutes until softened and fragrant. Add turmeric, 
smoked paprika, and salt, then stir until the onions 
take on a deep yellow colour. Add washed basmati 
rice and toast for 2–3 minutes to build flavour. Pour 
in double the amount of water (e.g. 1 cup rice = 2 
cups water),bring to a gentle bubble, stir once, »»
	» Continue
cover with a lid, and reduce to a low simmer for 10–
12 minutes. Check halfway and gently mix to avoid 
sticking and ensure even cooking.
	
z S T E P 4
Spread the marinated chicken thighs evenly on a 
lined sheet pan. Bake or air fry at 200°C (400°F) for 
20–23 minutes until golden, slightly charred, and 
juicy. Once cooked, let the chicken rest for 5 minutes 
before slicing into bite-size cubes.
	
z S T E P 5
To make the slaw, combine thinly sliced red 
onion, halved cherry tomatoes, chopped pickled 
gherkins, and fresh parsley in a bowl. Season with 
salt, black pepper, sumac, and a drizzle of olive oil. 
Toss until fully combined. For meal prep, store this 
slaw in a separate container to keep it crisp and fresh.
	
z S T E P 6
To serve, divide the golden turmeric rice into 5 
meal prep containers with the juicy shawarma 
chicken, a portion of the fresh slaw, and finish 
with a generous drizzle of the garlic white sauce. 
Your high-protein shawarma bowls are prepped and 
ready to ENJOY!

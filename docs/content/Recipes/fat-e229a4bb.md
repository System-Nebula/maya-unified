INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1
Trim excess fat from the chicken thighs and place them 
in a large mixing bowl. Season with salt, black pepper, 
lots of minced garlic, chilli flakes, smoked paprika, a

light soy sauce. Mix thoroughly with your hands until well 
coated and the colour deepens. Set aside to marinate.

INGREDIENTS (5 SERVINGS)
Garlic Butter Steak Bites
	
– 1000g (35oz) Raw Top Sirloin 
Steaks, cut into 1 inch cubes 
	
– 2 Tsp Salt 
	
– 2 Tsp Black Pepper 
	
– 2.5 Tsp Italian Herbs 
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder 
	
– 1.5 Tsp Paprika
	
– 3 Tsp Olive Oil 
	
– 25g (1oz) Light Butter, for cooking all 
	
– 4 Tsp Minced Garlic 
	
– 2 Tbsp Fresh Parsley
Tuscan Parmesan Rice
	
– 10g (0.3oz) Light Butter 
	
– 120g (4.2oz) Sundried Tomatoes, chopped 
	
– 3 Tsp Italian Herbs 
	
– 240g (8.4oz) Uncooked Basmati Rice 
/ approx 580g (20oz) Cooked 
	
– 50g (1.7oz) Grated Parmesan Cheese 
	
– 2 Tbsp Fresh Parsley
Creamy Sauce
	
– 250g (8.8oz) Low Fat Milk or Light Evaporated Milk 
	
– 150g (5.3oz) Light Cream Cheese 
	
– 1 Tsp Paprika 
	
– 1 Tsp Mixed Herbs
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1
Slice the beef steaks into 1-inch cubes and place 
them in a mixing bowl. Season with salt, black pepper, 
Italian herbs, garlic powder, paprika, and a drizzle of 
olive oil. Mix thoroughly with your hands until evenly 
coated, then set aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2
In a large pan over low heat, melt a little butter or 
spray lightly with oil. Add chopped sun-dried 
tomatoes and a sprinkle of Italian herbs. Sauté for 
2 minutes until fragrant. 
Add the cooked white rice and mix well until 
everything is incorporated. Remove from heat, stir in 
plenty of grated parmesan and freshly chopped 
parsley, then fluff until rich, cheesy, and aromatic.
	
z S T E P 3
Heat a separate large pan over medium heat and melt 
a little butter. Add the marinated steak bites »»
	» Continue
and sear for 3–4 minutes on each side until browned 
and juicy. 
Reduce the heat, then add a bit more butter, 
minced garlic, and chopped parsley. Toss until the 
beef is coated in the garlicky butter glaze. Cook in 
batches if needed to avoid overcrowding.
	
z S T E P 4
Using the same pan on low heat, add low-fat milk, 
light cream cheese, paprika, and mixed herbs. Stir 
continuously until the cream cheese is fully melted 
and the sauce is smooth. Let it bubble gently to 
thicken to your desired consistency. Store separately 
for the best texture and taste when reheating.
	
z S T E P 5
Distribute the Tuscan-style rice and steak bites 
evenly into 5 meal prep containers. Your high-protein 
meals are now ready to enjoy throughout the week!

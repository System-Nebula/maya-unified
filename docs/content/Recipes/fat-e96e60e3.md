Chicken Marinade
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Chicken Breast, cubed
	
– 20g (1.5 Tbsp) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Dark Soy Sauce
	
– All Purpose Seasoning:
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 1 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 1 Tsp Paprika
	
– 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1 Tsp Parsley
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
Teriyaki Sauce
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Honey
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Minced Garlic »»
	» Continue
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Water
	
– 15g (2 Tsp) Cornflour/Corn Starch
Fried Rice Mix
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil (for cooking)
	
– 4 Whole Eggs
	
– 100g (3.5oz) White Onion, finely chopped
	
– 20g (1.5 Tbsp) Fresh Ginger, chopped
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Carrots, chopped
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Fresh or Frozen Peas
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Bean Sprouts
	
– 200g (7oz) Uncooked White Rice/500g (17.5oz)
Cooked Rice (leftover/day old rice is best)
	
– 20g (1.5 Tbsp) Dark Soy Sauce
	
– 30g (2 Tbsp) Oyster Sauce
	
– Garnish Green Onion & Sesame Seeds
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breast into bite-sized cubes, 
then add soy sauce, all-purpose seasoning, and 
olive oil. Mix thoroughly until well coated and set 
aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2 
In a separate bowl, make the teriyaki sauce by 
combining soy sauce, honey, minced garlic, 
cornflour (or cornstarch), and water. Mix until 
smooth and set aside for later.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan over medium-high heat, add cooking 
spray or a little olive oil, add the beaten eggs then 
scramble for 2–3 minutes until fluffy. Remove from 
the pan and set aside.
	
z S T E P 4
In the same pan on medium high heat, cook the 
marinated chicken »»
	» Continue
for about 7–8 minutes until golden brown. lower the 
heat then pour in the teriyaki sauce, mix and let it 
bubble and thicken for 2–3 minutes until all pieces 
are well coated, then set the chicken aside.
	
z S T E P 5
Using the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed, 
then sauté chopped onions, ginger, carrots, peas, 
and bean sprouts for 5–8 minutes until soft and 
fragrant. Add cooked white rice, soy sauce, and 
oyster sauce. Stir well to combine.
	
z S T E P 6
Add the teriyaki chicken, scrambled eggs, 
and green onions to the fried rice mixture. Mix 
everything thoroughly until well combined. Garnish 
with sesame seeds and distriute into 4 even 
portions and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Marinate the chicken for at least 30 minutes (or longer) for deeper flavor.
	
z
Scramble the eggs on medium high heat, stirring constantly for 3–4 minutes until fluffy — don’t overcook.
	
z
Cook the chicken on high heat, stirring frequently to develop a golden brown color. Once browned, add the 
sauce on medium heat, let it thicken, then remove from heat with all the sauce.
	
z
When cooking the vegetables, make sure the carrots have fully softened before adding the rice.

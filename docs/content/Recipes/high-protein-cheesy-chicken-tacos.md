How to make
1.	 Butterfly cut the chicken breasts, then season both sides generously 
with oregano, garlic powder, paprika, onion powder, chilli flakes, 
salt, pepper, and olive oil.
2.	 Heat a pan over medium heat and cook the chicken for 3–4 minutes 
on each side until golden brown. Lower the heat if it starts to brown too 
quickly. Once cooked, transfer to a board and shred using two forks or 
knives.
3.	 In the same pan, add chopped onions and bell peppers with a bit 
more seasoning. Cook for about 5 minutes until softened, then stir in 
canned tomatoes, a splash of water, and the shredded chicken. Let it 
simmer for 3–4 minutes.
4.	 Take your taco wraps and lightly dip them into the chicken mixture for 
extra flavor. Place them on a separate pan, fill with the chicken mix and 
shredded mozzarella, then fold in half.
5.	 Toast both sides of the tacos for 1–2 minutes until crisp and golden. 
Serve hot and enjoy!

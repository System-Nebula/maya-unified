FAT
There’s no reason to be eating plain chicken & rice! One of the 
best tasting chicken recipes i’ve made, the sauce has so much 
flavour, macro friendly & perfect for meal prep!
TAP FOR VIDEO

INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Chicken & Seasoning
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Chicken Breast, cubed
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 2 Tsp Dried Basil
	
– 1 Tsp Chilli Flakes
	
– 2 Tsp Salt
	
– 2 Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– 3 Tsp Olive Oil
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Cherry Tomatoes
	
– 1 cup or Big Handful Spinach
Tuscan Creamy Sauce
	
– 350g (12.4oz) 1% Milk (or any milk)
	
– 150g (5.3oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Grated Parmigiano Reggiano
	
– 70g (2.5oz) Tomato Paste
	
– 1 Tsp Dried Basil
	
– Salt to taste
Serve With
	
– 210g Uncooked Basmati/Approx 
140g Cooked Rice per serving
	
– Sprinkle Mixed Herbs and Salt to taste
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breasts into cubes and place 
them in a bowl. Add minced garlic, dried basil, chili 
flakes, salt, smoked paprika, and a drizzle of olive 
oil. Toss until well coated, then set aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2 
For the creamy sauce, blend milk, cream cheese, 
grated Parmesan, tomato paste, dried basil, and 
salt to taste until completely smooth. Set aside. 
(Tip: Start cooking the rice now so it’s ready when 
serving the creamy chicken)
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan over medium-high heat and add a little 
olive oil or butter. Cook the chicken for 4 minutes 
on one side, then flip. Lower the heat and pour in 
the creamy sauce, followed by cherry tomatoes 
and spinach. Stir lightly, cover with a lid, and let it 
simmer on low heat for 5–7 minutes.
	
z S T E P 4
Remove the lid halfway through and stir gently to 
prevent sticking while allowing the sauce to thicken. 
By now, the chicken will be coated in the creamy 
sauce, the spinach will have wilted, and the cherry 
tomatoes will be soft and bursting with flavor.
	
z S T E P 5 
Serve the creamy chicken over hot herb-seasoned 
rice, distribute evenly, and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Lower the heat after cooking the chicken for 4 minutes before adding the creamy sauce to prevent splitting.
	
z
Cook rice in advance so it’s ready to serve with the chicken and sauce.

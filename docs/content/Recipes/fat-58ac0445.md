FAT
Combining two of the most popular dishes into one!
So indulgent, creamy, packed with protein and perfect to meal 
prep for the week ahead!
TAP FOR VIDEO
THE MEAL PREP COOK BOOK 
109

INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Butter Chicken
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Chicken Breast, cubed
	
– 30g (2 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 1 Tbsp Smoked Paprika
	
– 1 Tsp Turmeric
	
– 1.5 Tsp Garam Masala
	
– 1.5 Tsp Chilli Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Salt
	
– 2 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 120g (4.2oz) 0% Yogurt
	
– Juice of 1 Lemon
Pasta
	
– 270g Uncooked Penne Pasta 
(Or use protein pasta)
Butter Chicken Sauce
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Reduced Fat Butter
	
– 100g (3.5oz) White Onion, thinly sliced
	
– 500g (17.5oz) Canned Chopped Tomatoes
	
– Spice Mix: ½ Tsp each Smoked Paprika, Turmeric, 
Garam Masala, Chilli Powder, Salt, Black Pepper
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Raw Cashew Nuts (or roasted)
	
– 100g (3.5oz) 0% Yogurt
	
– 125g (½ Cup) Pasta Water
	
– Garnish Coriander/Cilantro, chopped
White Sauce
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Yogurt
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Milk
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1
Slice the chicken breasts into cubes and place 
them in a bowl. Add minced garlic, smoked 
paprika, turmeric, garam masala, chili powder, 
salt, black pepper, yogurt, and lemon juice. Mix 
until evenly coated, then set aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2 
Heat a pan over medium heat and melt butter. Add 
thinly sliced onion and cook for 5 minutes until 
soft and fragrant. Stir in the spice mix, ensuring the 
onions are well coated, then add canned chopped 
tomatoes and cashew nuts.
	
z S T E P 3 
Mix well and let everything cook for another 5 
minutes on medium-low heat. Turn off the heat and 
stir in yogurt, then let the mixture cool for 5 minutes. 
(Tip: Transfer to a large dish for faster cooling)
	
z S T E P 4
While the sauce cools, bring a pot of water to a boil 
and cook the penne pasta for 7–8 minutes until al 
dente. Once cooked, reserve 1 cup of pasta water, 
then drain the rest.
	
z S T E P 5
Transfer the cooled sauce to a blender, add ½ cup 
of pasta water, and blend for 2–3 minutes until 
completely smooth and creamy. 
	
z S T E P 6
Reheat the pan over medium-high heat, melt butter, 
and cook the marinated chicken for 4 minutes per 
side until golden brown. Lower the heat, then pour 
in the butter chicken sauce and add the cooked 
pasta. Stir until well combined and heated through.
	
z S T E P 7
Garnish with fresh coriander and white sauce, then 
evenly portion, serve and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Marinate the chicken overnight for the best flavor, but 10–15 minutes works if short on time.
	
z
Use fresh tomatoes instead of canned to reduce acidity and add natural sweetness.
	
z
Let the sauce cool before blending to prevent pressure buildup and spills.
	
z
Turn off the heat before adding yogurt to keep the sauce smooth and prevent curdling.

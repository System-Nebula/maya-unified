How to make
1.	 Marinate the chicken with light mayo, sriracha or your preferred hot sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, 
cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix thoroughly until the chicken is evenly coated.
2.	 Heat a pan over medium-high heat and cook the chicken for 3–4 minutes on each side, or 5 minutes if the 
pieces are thicker. Once cooked, let the chicken rest for 5 minutes to allow the juices to absorb.
3.	 While the chicken rests, lightly toast the burger buns until golden and warm.
4.	 To assemble, spread a layer of spicy mayo on the bottom bun, add the juicy piece of chicken, then layer 
on slices of tomato, pickled red onion, lettuce, and cheese.
5.	 Place the top bun on, press gently, and enjoy your spicy chicken sandwich!

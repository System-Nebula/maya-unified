INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Garlic Beef
	
– 600g (22oz) Raw 95% Ground/Mince Beef
	
– 1.5 Tsp Italian Herbs
	
– 1.5 Tsp Paprika
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 1.5 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil (for cooking)
Pasta
	
– 250g Uncooked Fusilli Pasta (or pasta of choice)
	
– Pinch of Salt
	
– Garnish Fresh Parsley & Parmesan Cheese
Creamy Red Pepper Sauce
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Garlic Cloves, chopped
	
– 200g (7oz) Tomatoes, chopped
	
– 150g (5.3oz) Red Bell Pepper, chopped
	
– 1.5 Tsp Italian Herbs
	
– 2 Tsp Paprika
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 1 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 150g (5.3oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 125g (½ Cup) Pasta Water
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Chop the garlic cloves, red bell pepper, and 
tomatoes. Heat a pan over medium heat, add 
olive oil, and sauté the garlic for 2 minutes until 
fragrant. Then, add the chopped bell peppers and 
tomatoes.
	
z S T E P 2
Season with Italian herbs, paprika, salt, and black 
pepper, then cook for 5 minutes until soft and 
fragrant. Transfer the mixture to a dish and let it cool 
while preparing the next step.
	
z S T E P 3
Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add the fusilli 
pasta and cook for 8 minutes until al dente. Reserve 
½ cup of pasta water, then drain the rest and set 
the pasta aside.
	
z S T E P 4
In a blender cup, combine the cooled vegetable 
mixture, cream cheese, »»
	» Continue
and reserved pasta water. Blend for 2 minutes until 
completely smooth and creamy. The sauce should 
be a vibrant orange color.
	
z S T E P 5
Return the pan to high heat, add a drizzle of olive 
oil, and cook the ground beef with extra seasoning 
(Italian herbs, paprika, salt, black pepper) for 
6–8 minutes. Use a spatula to break up the meat 
while cooking until fully browned with no pink bits 
remaining.
	
z S T E P 6
Lower the heat and pour the creamy sauce over the 
beef, followed by the cooked pasta. Stir gently until 
well combined and indulgent. Let it simmer on low 
heat for 3–4 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
	
z S T E P 7 
Garnish with fresh parsley and parmesan, then 
serve and distribute evenly. ENJOY!

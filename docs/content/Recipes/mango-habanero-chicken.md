How to make
1.	 Cube the chicken breasts and season with all the listed spices and ingredients. Let it marinate for a few 
minutes.
2.	 Heat a pan on medium with cooking spray or olive oil. Cook the chicken for 3–4 minutes on each side 
until golden brown, then set aside.
3.	 In the same pan, add light butter and crushed garlic. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
4.	 Add mango juice, honey, hot sauce, paprika, and lime juice. Stir to combine and let it bubble and 
simmer for 3–4 minutes until slightly thickened.
5.	 Return the cooked chicken to the pan, stir to coat well in the sauce, and let it simmer for another 2 
minutes.
6.	 Garnish with chopped coriander and serve with hot rice. ENJOY!

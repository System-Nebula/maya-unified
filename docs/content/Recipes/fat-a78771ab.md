INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Crispy Chicken
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Chicken Breast, cubed
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 1 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Paprika
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Cornflour/Cornstarch
Sticky Orange Sauce
	
– 350g (12.4oz) Orange Juice, freshly squeezed
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Honey »»
	» Continue
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Water 
	
– 1/2 or 1 Tsp Chilli Flakes (omit for less heat)
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Garlic Cloves, chopped
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Ginger, chopped
	
– 1 Tsp Orange Zest (optional)
To Serve
	
– 200g (7oz) Uncooked White Rice
	
– Garnish Orange Slices
	
– Garnish Sesame Seeds
	
– Garnish Green Onion/Scallion
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breasts into cubes, season with 
salt, pepper, garlic powder, and cornflour. Mix well 
until evenly coated — using a ziplock bag makes this 
easier and mess-free.
	
z S T E P 2 
In a bowl, combine orange juice, soy sauce, honey, 
and orange zest — set this sauce aside for later.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan over medium-high heat, add olive oil 
or butter, and cook the coated chicken for 4–5 
minutes on each side until golden brown and crisp. 
Set aside when cooked.
	
z S T E P 4
In the same pan on medium heat, add chopped 
garlic, ginger, and chili flakes. Sauté for 1–2 minutes 
until fragrant, then pour in the orange sauce. Let 
it bubble and reduce for 3–4 minutes, then stir in a 
cornstarch slurry to thicken.
	
z S T E P 5
Once slighty thickened, return the crispy chicken to 
the sauce, mixing until well coated and glossy.
	
z S T E P 6
Meanwhile, cook your rice in a pot of water. Once 
cooked, serve everything evenly into portions, 
garnish with orange slices, green onions, and 
sesame seeds — then ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Make sure each piece of chicken is evenly coated in cornflour for the crispiest result.
	
z
You can also oven-bake or air fry the chicken for a lighter, crispy option.
	
z
Great for meal prep — store in the fridge for 4–5 days or freeze for longer.
	
z
To reheat from frozen, microwave for 5 minutes, or thaw overnight in the fridge before reheating.

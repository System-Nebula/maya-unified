How to make
1.	 Boil water in a pot, add a teaspoon of salt, then cook the pasta for 8–10 minutes. Before draining, reserve 
½ cup of the pasta water, then drain and set the pasta aside.
2.	 In a pan over medium heat, add olive oil and garlic cloves. Cook for 1 minute, then add chopped bell pepper 
and tomato. Season with salt, pepper, Italian herbs, and paprika. Cook for 5 minutes until softened.
3.	 Transfer the cooked vegetables to a blender along with the reserved pasta water and cream cheese. 
Blend until smooth and creamy, about 1 minute.
4.	 In the same pan, cook the beef over medium heat for 5 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, Italian herbs, 
and paprika as before.
5.	 Once the beef is cooked, add the drained pasta and blended sauce. Stir everything together, let it 
simmer for a few minutes, then top with grated parmesan and enjoy!

How to make
1.	 Slice the steaks into thin strips and place in a bowl. Marinate with soy 
sauce, Worcestershire sauce, black pepper, garlic, and ginger. Mix 
well and let it sit for a few minutes while prepping other ingredients.
2.	 Heat a pan over medium heat and add a bit of olive oil. Add diced 
carrots, broccoli, bell pepper, and cashews. Season with soy sauce 
and Worcestershire sauce, then cook for about 5 minutes until the 
vegetables are slightly soft. Remove from the pan and set aside.
3.	 Increase the heat to high and cook the marinated beef in the same pan. 
Let it sear for about 5 minutes until browned and most of the liquid has 
evaporated.
4.	 Once the beef is browned, return the cooked vegetables to the pan. 
Mix everything together and let it simmer for a minute so the flavors 
combine.
5.	 Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy!

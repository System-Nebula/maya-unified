Chilli Peanut Noodles

– 30g (1.1oz) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Sriracha
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Rice Vinegar
	
– 1 Tsp Chilli Flakes (omit for less heat)

– 250g (8.8oz) Uncooked Medium Egg

– 50g (1.8oz) Green Onion/Scallion, chopped
	
– Handful Fresh Coriander, chopped
	
– Handful Sesame Seeds
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breasts into cubes. In a bowl, 
marinate with black pepper, garlic & ginger paste, 
chilli flakes, onion powder, sriracha, and soy 
sauce. Mix until the color deepens, then set aside.
	
z S T E P 2 
While the chicken marinates, cook the noodles 
for 5–6 minutes (or follow package instructions). 
Reserve some noodle water, then drain and rinse 
the noodles under cold water.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan over medium heat and add butter. Cook 
the chicken cubes for 3–4 minutes per side until 
golden and crispy. Once cooked, add extra butter »»

and chopped coriander, tossing until well coated. 
Set aside.
	
z S T E P 4
In the same pan over low heat, combine peanut 
butter, soy sauce, sriracha, rice vinegar, optional 
chilli flakes, and a splash of reserved noodle 
water. Stir continuously for 3–4 minutes until the 
sauce is silky smooth.
	
z S T E P 5
Toss the cooked noodles in the sauce along with 
chopped green onions, additional coriander, and 
sesame seeds. Mix thoroughly, then serve alongside 
the juicy chicken and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z

z

How to make
1.	 Cut the chicken into strips and season with all the listed spices, tomato paste, and lemon juice. Mix 
thoroughly to coat the chicken evenly and set aside to marinate.
2.	 Preheat the oven to 220°C (430°F). Slice the potato into fries, then toss with cornstarch, paprika, black 
pepper, and a light spray of cooking oil. Spread the fries out on a lined baking tray and bake for 20 
minutes, turning halfway through if needed.
3.	 While the fries are in the oven, heat a pan over medium heat, spray with oil, and cook the marinated 
chicken for 3–4 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through. Avoid overcooking to keep 
the chicken tender.
4.	 In a small bowl, mix together all the ingredients for the garlic sauce until smooth and creamy. Adjust 
seasoning to taste if needed.
5.	 To assemble, take a flatbread and spread a generous layer of garlic sauce. Add a few pieces of cooked 
chicken, about 50g of fries, and some sliced pickle. Drizzle with more garlic sauce on top, wrap or fold, 
and enjoy!

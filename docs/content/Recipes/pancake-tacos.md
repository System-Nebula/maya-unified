How to make
1.	 In a blender cup, combine oats, Greek yogurt, eggs, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Blend for about 
1–2 minutes until the mixture is completely smooth and pourable.
2.	 Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Pour the pancake batter into the pan in small rounds and cook for 
1.5–2 minutes per side, or until golden and cooked through. In the same or a separate pan, scramble your 
eggs and cook 3 pieces of turkey bacon until crispy.
3.	 Assemble your plate with the pancakes, scrambled eggs, and turkey bacon. Top with low-fat cheese, a 
drizzle of sriracha, and your favorite seasonings. Enjoy!

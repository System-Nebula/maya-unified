How to make
1.	 Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly spray with oil. Pour in your whisked egg mixture, then 
immediately lay two slices of wholemeal bread on top of the uncooked egg. Allow the egg to fully cook and 
set for a few minutes.
2.	 Use a spatula to gently loosen the edges of the egg from the pan. Slide the spatula underneath and carefully 
flip the entire egg and bread layer in one motion.
3.	 Once flipped, fold the sides of the cooked egg inward. Layer your fillings on one side: lettuce or greens, 
sliced tomato, seasonings (salt, pepper, chili flakes, garlic powder), cooked turkey bacon, sliced 
avocado, and a drizzle of spicy mayo.
4.	 Fold the other slice of bread over the filling to form a sandwich. Toast both sides for a couple more minutes 
until golden and crispy. Slice and serve warm — enjoy!

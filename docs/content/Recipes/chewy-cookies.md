Chewy Cookies
Soft and chewy for the perfect texture! Great to satisfy that sweet tooth!
Macros per serving
104 CALORIES
 7g PROTEIN
5g  CARBS
5g FAT
Ingredients (12 cookies)
	
z
1/2 Cup (4oz) Natural Peanut Butter 
	
z
1 Whole Egg + 1 Egg White
	
z
1/4 Cup (2oz) Coconut sugar
	
z
2 Scoops Protein Powder
	
z
1/4 Tsp Baking Soda
	
z
2 Tbsp Coconut Flour
	
z
1/4 Cup (2oz) Dark Chocolate (Optional)
How to make
1.	 In a mixing bowl, combine peanut butter, eggs, and coconut sugar. Stir until the mixture is smooth and 
well blended.
2.	 Add protein powder, baking soda, and coconut flour. Mix until a soft dough forms. If you like, fold in some 
dark chocolate chips.
3.	 Line a baking tray with parchment paper. Scoop and shape the dough into 12 small cookies, gently flattening 
each one with your fingers.
4.	 Bake in a preheated oven at 170°C (340°F) for 5 minutes. Let them cool slightly—they should be soft in the 
center. Enjoy!

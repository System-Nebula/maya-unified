Cheesy Beef Bolognese Tacos
This combo is absolutely INSANE! Bolognese and tacos are some of my favourite dishes - 
so flavourful, easy to make and so filling!

Garnish with Parmesan

How to make
1.	 Dice the onion and grate the carrots. Heat a pan over medium heat, 
add olive oil, then sauté the onion and carrots with salt, pepper, and 
tomato paste. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes until softened.
2.	 Add the lean ground beef along with oregano, garlic powder, and 
smoked paprika. Break it up with a spoon, mix thoroughly, and cook for 
5–8 minutes until browned.
3.	 Pour in the chopped tomatoes, stir to combine, and let the mixture 
simmer for 4–5 minutes to deepen the flavour.
4.	 Heat a second pan. Dip each taco shell briefly into the meat mixture 
to coat, then place onto the second pan to grill.
5.	 Fill each taco with the beef mixture and mozzarella, toast on both 
sides until golden and crispy, then garnish with grated parmesan and 
chopped parsley. Serve hot and enjoy!

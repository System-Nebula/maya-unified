INGREDIENTS (5 SERVINGS)
Cajun Honey Butter Chicken
	
– 1000g (35oz) Raw Chicken Breast, cubed
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 1.5 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 2.5 Tsp Paprika
	
– 2 Tsp Oregano
	
– 1 Tsp Chilli Flakes
	
– 3 Tsp Olive Oil
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Light Butter (for cooking)
	
– Extra Seasoning: garlic, Onion 
Powder, Paprika, Oregano
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Honey (for all the chicken)
	
– Fresh Parsley
Creamy Garlic Herb Potatoes
	
– 1100g (38.8oz) Raw Potatoes, cubed
	
– 1 Tbsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1 Tbsp Italian Herbs
	
– 1 Tbsp Paprika
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Red Onion, finely chopped
	
– 40-50g (1.7oz) Tomato Paste
	
– 250g (8.8oz) Light/Fat Free Evaporated Milk
	
– 150g (5.3oz) Garlic & Herb Cream Cheese
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1
Slice the chicken breasts into cubes and place in a 
mixing bowl. Season with salt, black pepper, garlic 
powder, onion powder, paprika, oregano, chilli flakes, 
and a drizzle of olive oil. Mix well until fully coated and 
the colour deepens, then set aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2
Cut the uncooked potatoes into small cubes and 
place in a separate bowl. Season with garlic powder, 
Italian herbs, paprika, and olive oil. Mix thoroughly, 
then lay them flat on a lined sheet pan. Oven bake 
or air fry at 200°C (400°F) for 20-22 minutes until 
golden and crispy.
	
z S T E P 3
While the potatoes cook, heat a pan on medium, 
melt a little butter, and cook the seasoned chicken 
for 6-8 minutes until golden. Lower the heat, then 
add extra butter, this seasoning mix (garlic & 
onion powder, paprika, oregano), a drizzle of 
honey, and fresh parsley. Mix well until fully glazed 
and juicy, then set aside. Repeat if needed for 
remaining chicken.
	
z S T E P 4
In the same pan, add chopped onion and cook for 
5 minutes until soft. Stir in tomato paste and cook 
for another 2 minutes until fragrant. Lower the heat, 
then pour in evaporated milk and add garlic & herb 
cream cheese. Stir continuously until the sauce is 
smooth and slightly thickened.
	
z S T E P 5
Once the potatoes are cooked, fold them into the 
creamy sauce gently until fully coated and indulgent. 
Divide the creamy potatoes and honey butter 
chicken into 5 even portions - your high-protein 
meals are ready to ENJOY!

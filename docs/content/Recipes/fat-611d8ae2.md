INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Chicken Marinade
	
– 700g (25oz) Raw Chicken Breast, 
cut into thin strips
	
– 1.5 Tsp Paprika
	
– 1 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 1.5 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 20g (1oz) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 20g (1oz) Dark Soy Sauce
Chow Mein Noodles + Sauce
	
– 220g (7.8oz) Uncooked Egg Noodles 
(or noodles of choice)
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
Noodles + Sauce
	
– 220g Uncooked Egg Noodles 
(Brand: Asia’s Specialties)
	
– 1 tbsp Minced Garlic
	
– 1 Medium Red Onion Sliced
	
– 1 Medium Red Bell Pepper Sliced
	
– 1 cup Chopped Green Onion
	
– 1 cup Bean Sprouts
	
– 50ml Low Sodium Soy Sauce
	
– 50-60g Oyster Sauce (Brand: M&S)
	
– (Optional) 10ml Rice Vinegar
	
– Garnish Sesame Seeds + Chilli Flakes
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breasts into thin strips and place 
them in a mixing bowl. Marinate with paprika, black 
pepper, garlic powder, and light & dark soy sauce. 
Mix thoroughly and let sit for a few minutes to 
deepen the flavour.
	
z S T E P 2 
Slice the red onion, bell pepper, and green 
onion. Cook the noodles according to package 
instructions—I boiled and simmered them in water 
for 4–5 minutes. Drain, rinse under cold water, and 
set aside.
	
z S T E P 3
Heat a pan over medium heat, add cooking spray or 
olive oil, and cook the marinated chicken for 6-8 
minutes until golden brown and slightly charred. Set 
aside and repeat if needed.
	
z S T E P 4
In the same pan over medium-high heat, stir-fry 
the sliced onion, bell pepper, and green onion 
for 5 minutes till fragrant. Add minced garlic, soy 
sauce, oyster sauce, rice vinegar, and the cooked 
noodles. Mix gently but thoroughly until well 
combined.
	
z S T E P 5
Finally add the cooked chicken and bean sprouts, 
mix again, then garnish with sesame seeds and chili 
flakes. Distribute into 4 servings and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Marinate the chicken for at least 30 minutes for deeper flavor.
	
z
Cook the noodles according to package instructions—I simmered them in boiling water for 4–5 minutes.
	
z
Prep all vegetables before cooking the chicken to streamline the process.
	
z
Adjust seasoning by adding extra soy sauce or oyster sauce after mixing in the cooked noodles.

INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Chicken & Seasoning 
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Chicken 
Breast, butterflied halves
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Parsley
	
– 2 Tsp Paprika
	
– 2 Tsp Dried Basil
	
– 2 Tsp Salt
	
– 2 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
Tomato Mix 
	
– 800g (28oz) Canned Tomatoes
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Red Onion, thinly sliced
	
– 1/2 Tsp each: Garlic Powder, Paprika, 
Dried Basil, Salt, Black Pepper
	
– Garnish Fresh Parsley
Serve With
	
– 200g (7oz) Uncooked Basmati Rice/540g 
Cooked Rice (140g per serving)
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice your chicken breasts into thinner halves. 
Season both sides with garlic powder, parsley, 
paprika, dried basil, salt, pepper, and olive oil. Rub 
the seasoning in until evenly coated.
	
z S T E P 2 
Heat a pan over medium heat, then cook the 
seasoned chicken for 4–5 minutes on the first side 
until golden and slightly charred. Flip and cook the 
other side for 2–3 minutes.
	
z S T E P 3 
Pour in canned chopped tomatoes around the 
chicken along with thinly sliced red onion. Sprinkle 
in a bit more seasoning for extra flavour.
	
z S T E P 4
Gently mix the onions and tomatoes together, 
spreading evenly around the chicken. Cover with a 
lid and simmer on low heat for 10 minutes, stirring 
halfway to blend the flavours.
	
z S T E P 5
Meanwhile, boil your rice in a pot of water until 
cooked. Once done, serve alongside the saucy 
chicken. Garnish with fresh parsley and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Cook the chicken on medium heat until golden, then flip before adding the canned tomatoes.
	
z
If needed, layer any remaining chicken pieces on top before covering the pan.
	
z
Simmer for 10 minutes on low heat, stirring occasionally to let the flavours develop.

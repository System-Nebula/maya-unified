How to make
1.	 Start by slicing your potatoes into small, evenly sized cubes. Transfer 
them to a bowl and season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and 
oregano. Microwave the seasoned potatoes for 4–5 minutes until they 
become soft and partially cooked.
2.	 Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add a bit of olive oil or 
cooking spray. Add the microwaved potatoes and cook for about 4 
minutes until they begin to turn golden. Then sprinkle in 1 teaspoon 
of paprika and continue cooking for another 4 minutes, stirring 
occasionally, until they’re crispy and golden brown.
3.	 While the potatoes are finishing up, prepare your scrambled eggs. 
Crack your eggs into a bowl, whisk well, then pour into a pan over low 
heat. Stir gently and continuously for soft, fluffy eggs.
4.	 To serve, plate the crispy potatoes and fluffy eggs together. Drizzle 
with spicy mayo, season the eggs with salt, pepper, and chili flakes, 
and garnish with fresh parsley. Enjoy your hearty, flavour-packed 
breakfast!

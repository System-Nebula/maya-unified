– 1 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, chopped

Crispy Potatoes
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw White Potatoes, cubed
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1 Tsp Paprika
	
– 1 Tsp Oregano
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil or Cooking Spray
	
– 60g (2.1oz) Shredded Mozzarella
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Start by slicing your chicken breasts into bite-
sized cubes. Season with salt, oregano, chili flakes, 
onion powder, paprika, and olive oil. Mix thoroughly 
until well coated, then set aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2 
Chop your potatoes into small cubes. Season with 
salt, garlic powder, paprika, oregano, and olive oil. 
Toss until every piece is evenly coated.
	
z S T E P 3 
Spread the seasoned potatoes out on a lined sheet 
pan in a single layer. Oven bake at 200°C (400°F) for 
20 minutes or air fry for 18 minutes until golden and 
crispy.
	
z S T E P 4
Heat a pan over medium heat, melt a bit of butter, 
then cook the marinated chicken »»
	» Continue
for 6–8 minutes until golden brown and cooked 
through.
	
z S T E P 5
Once the chicken is cooked, lower the heat, then stir 
in extra butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, 
and drizzle of BBQ sauce. Using a spatula, mix until 
the chicken is well coated and glazed.
	
z S T E P 6
Add the crispy potatoes to the pan and mix with 
the chicken till well incorporated. Sprinkle over 
shredded mozzarella, cover with a lid on low heat, 
and let it melt for 2–3 minutes.
	
z S T E P 7
Once the cheese is melted and gooey, drizzle with 
extra BBQ sauce, divide into four equal portions, 
and your high protein meal prep is ready to ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Cook the chicken over medium heat to avoid drying it out, then reduce to low heat before adding the garlic, 
parsley, and BBQ sauce.
	
z
Stir everything together until the chicken is well coated and glazed—this creates a rich, smoky flavour.
	
z
Add the cheese while the pan is on low heat, then cover to let it melt. You can add a splash of water to help 
it steam and melt faster.
	
z
Store in the fridge for up to 4 days or freeze for longer. Reheat in the air fryer or oven until hot and crispy.

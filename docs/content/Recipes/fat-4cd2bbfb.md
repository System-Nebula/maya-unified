INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breast cutlets and boneless

z S T E P 2 
Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers and 
place on a lined baking tray. Oven-bake or air-fry at 
200°C (400°F) for 25 minutes until golden brown and 
juicy then set aside covered to rest and keep warm.
	
z S T E P 3

drizzle of olive oil. Toss until well coated, then oven-
bake or air-fry at 200°C (400°F) for 20–25 minutes 
until crispy and golden brown. (If space allows, cook

z S T E P 5
To prepare the garlic naan, combine flour, yogurt,

z S T E P 6
Divide the dough into four equal smaller balls, then

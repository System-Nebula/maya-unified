BBQ Chicken Tenders
These tenders are so crispy, easy to make with minimal ingredients and taste

z

How to make
1.	 Cut the chicken into strips and place them in a bowl. Add BBQ sauce, black pepper, garlic powder, and 
paprika. Mix well until all the strips are fully coated in the marinade.
2.	 In a separate bowl, mix crushed cornflakes with paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper to make the 
dry coating.
3.	 Take each marinated chicken strip and coat it in the cornflake mixture, pressing gently to ensure they’re 
well coated. Place them on a wired rack over a baking tray.
4.	 Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C (390°F) for 18 minutes until crispy and golden.
5.	 Once cooked, brush each piece with more BBQ glaze for extra flavour, then serve and enjoy!

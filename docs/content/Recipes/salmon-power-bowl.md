How to make
1.	 Slice your skinless salmon into bite-sized cubes. Add your seasonings, garlic powder, paprika or chilli flakes, 
soy sauce, and a touch of oil—then mix until the salmon is evenly coated. Set aside while you prepare the rest.
2.	 Thinly slice your red onion and carrot, then make a quick cucumber salad by combining sliced cucumber 
with soy sauce, garlic powder, chilli flakes or paprika, and a splash of rice vinegar. Stir well and let it sit 
to marinate.
3.	 Rinse your jasmine rice thoroughly, then cook it in boiling water until fluffy. While the rice cooks, heat a pan 
on medium heat with a bit of oil and sear the salmon cubes for 2–3 minutes on each side until golden and 
slightly crisp on the edges.
4.	 Prepare your toppings: slice avocado, and if using, mix up a spicy lemon mayo by combining light mayo 
with a bit of hot sauce and lemon juice.
5.	 Assemble your bowl with a portion of rice, cooked salmon, cucumber salad, sliced onion, carrot, and 
avocado. Drizzle over the spicy lemon mayo and top with sesame seeds and chopped green onion if you 
like. Serve and enjoy!

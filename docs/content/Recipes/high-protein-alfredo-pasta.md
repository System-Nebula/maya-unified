How to make
1.	 Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook for about 8 minutes or until al dente. Reserve ½ cup of 
the pasta water, then drain the rest and set the pasta aside.
2.	 Slice the chicken into cutlets for quicker, even cooking. Season with garlic powder, Italian herbs, paprika, 
onion powder, salt, pepper, and olive oil. Mix until the chicken is fully coated.
3.	 In a blender, combine milk, cream cheese, parmesan, Italian herbs, and the reserved pasta water. Blend until 
the sauce is smooth and creamy. Add salt if needed.
4.	 Heat a pan over medium heat. Cook the seasoned chicken for about 5 minutes on each side until golden and fully 
cooked. Remove from the pan and set aside.
5.	 In the same pan, add chopped onions and fresh garlic. Cook for a minute or two until fragrant and softened.
6.	 Pour in the blended sauce and bring it to a gentle bubble. Add the cooked pasta and sliced chicken back into the 
pan. Simmer everything together for 3–5 minutes to let the flavors combine.
7.	 Garnish with chopped parsley and extra parmesan. Serve hot and enjoy!

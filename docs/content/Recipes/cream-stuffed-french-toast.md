How to make
1.	 In a bowl or dish, combine the eggs and egg whites, almond milk, vanilla extract, and a dash of cinnamon. 
Whisk thoroughly until the mixture is frothy and bubbles form on the surface.
2.	 Dip the slices of bread into the egg mixture, ensuring both sides are lightly soaked. Heat a pan over 
medium heat and cook each slice for about 2 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
3.	 In a small bowl, mix together cream cheese and Greek yogurt until smooth. Add a teaspoon of your 
favorite jam and gently fold it in to create a swirled effect.
4.	 On a serving plate, start layering by placing a slice of the cooked bread, then spread some of the cream 
cheese mixture, and top with sliced strawberries. Repeat this layering two more times. Finish with a 
sprinkle of cinnamon or a drizzle of maple syrup, then enjoy!

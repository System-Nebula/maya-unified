How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken breast into 2 thinner fillets, then season both sides with paprika, garlic powder, onion 
powder, salt, pepper, and olive oil. Rub the spices in to evenly coat.
2.	 Cook the chicken on medium heat for 4–5 minutes on each side until fully cooked. Remove from the pan 
and shred into pieces using two forks.
3.	 To make the cheese sauce, add cottage cheese, cream cheese, buffalo sauce, cheddar, milk, garlic 
powder, and onion powder into a blender. Blend for 2 minutes until smooth and creamy.
4.	 In a pot, bring salted water to a boil. Add the uncooked pasta and boil for 8 minutes. Drain and set aside.
5.	 On low heat, add the cooked pasta to a pan along with the cheese sauce. Mix together until well combined 
and heated through.
6.	 Add the shredded chicken to the pasta, mix it all together, and serve hot. ENJOY!

How to make
1.	 Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped 
onions, carrots, and red bell pepper. Sauté for 3–4 minutes until the 
vegetables begin to soften and the onions turn translucent.
2.	 Push the vegetables to the sides to make a well in the center of the 
pan. Add the washed, uncooked rice into the well. Season with salt, 
black pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and turmeric. Stir to evenly 
coat the rice in the spices and let it toast for 2–3 minutes, allowing the 
flavors to infuse.
3.	 Pour in water or chicken broth, just enough to cover the rice. Stir 
everything together, then bring the mixture to a light boil. Once boiling, 
reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and let it simmer for 8–10 minutes 
until the rice is fully cooked. Stir gently halfway through to prevent 
sticking and ensure even cooking.
4.	 Once the rice is tender and the liquid absorbed, fluff it gently. Serve 
warm with your favorite protein—like grilled chicken or fish—and garnish 
with fresh parsley or coriander. Enjoy!

How to make
1.	 Take 2 tortilla wraps and cut them into triangle-shaped pieces using a knife. Place the pieces on a baking 
tray, spray with oil, and season with salt, garlic powder, parsley, and chili flakes. Bake at 180°C (350°F) 
for 5 minutes until crisp.
2.	 Cut the chicken breast in half, then rub with olive oil, salt, chili powder, paprika, parsley, garlic powder, 
and cumin until fully coated. Cook over medium heat for 4 minutes on each side, then shred the chicken 
once cooked.
3.	 In a bowl, combine chopped tomato, red onion, avocado, and coriander. Season with salt, pepper, and 
a squeeze of lime juice, then mix everything together to make a fresh salsa.
4.	 Layer the baked tortilla chips with shredded chicken and grated cheese. Return to the oven and bake for 
3–4 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
5.	 Remove from the oven and top with the fresh salsa and a dollop of sour cream or yogurt. Serve immediately 
and enjoy!

– Salt To Taste (after cooking)
	
– 3 Tsp Olive Oil
Creamy Chipotle Sauce (enough for 8+ meals)
	
– 250g (8.8oz) Low Fat Greek Yogurt 
(or yogurt of choice)
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Light Mayo
	
– 80g (2.8oz) Chipotle Paste
	
– 25g (1oz) Honey
	
– Juice of 1/2 Lime
	
– 1/2 Tsp each: Salt, Garlic, Paprika
Tangy Pico De Gallo (enough for 6+ meals)
	
– 300g (10.6oz) Fresh Tomatoes, chopped
	
– 150g (5.3oz) Red Onion, chopped
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Red Bell Pepper, chopped
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Green Bell Pepper, chopped
	
– 1.5 Tbsp Fresh Coriander, finely chopped
	
– 1/2 Tsp each: Salt, Black Pepper
	
– Juice of 1/2 Lime
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1
Slice the potatoes into small cubes. Season with 
garlic powder, onion powder, regular or chipotle 
chilli flakes, parsley, smoked paprika, cumin, and 
a drizzle of olive oil. Use your hands to mix well until 
every piece is evenly coated.
	
z S T E P 2
Spread the seasoned potatoes on a lined sheet pan 
and oven bake or air fry at 200°C (400°F) for 20-
23 minutes until golden and crispy. Once cooked, 
sprinkle salt to taste.
	
z S T E P 3
While the potatoes cook, prepare the creamy 
chipotle sauce. In a blender, combine low-fat 
yogurt, light mayo, chipotle paste, honey, lime 
juice, salt, garlic powder, and paprika. Blend 
until smooth, then bottle and refrigerate to use 
throughout the week.
	
z S T E P 4
Heat a pan over high heat, melt butter, and cook 
the ground beef for 6-8 minutes until browned and 
fully cooked. Lower the heat to medium, then add 
salt, garlic powder, onion powder, parsley, paprika, 
cumin, and chipotle paste. Mix thoroughly. If too 
dry, add a splash of water, then finish with a drizzle 
of honey and cook until juicy and golden. Remove 
from heat.
	
z S T E P 5
For the salsa, combine chopped tomatoes, red 
onion, red and green bell peppers, coriander, salt, 
pepper, and lime juice in a large bowl. Mix well and 
store separately to keep fresh and make reheating 
easier.
	
z STEP 6
Serve into 5 even portions. Store in the fridge and 
reheat in a microwave or pan when ready to enjoy!

Garlic Parmesan Crusted Chicken
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Chicken Breasts, butterflied halves
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 3 Tsp Italian Herbs
	
– 2 Tsp Salt
	
– 2 Tsp Paprika
	
– 3 Tsp Olive Oil

Creamy Sauce

– 250g (8.5oz) 1% Milk »»

– C150g (5.3oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 20g (1oz) Grated Parmigiano Reggiano 
Creamy Mash Potatoes
	
– 900g (32oz) Raw Potatoes, 1-inch cubed
	
– 15g (0.5oz) Reduced Fat Butter
	
– 100g (3.4oz) Light Evaporated Milk
	
– 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 1 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– Additional seasoning to taste
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1
Slice the chicken breasts into thinner halves. In a 
bowl, season with garlic powder, Italian herbs, salt, 
paprika, olive oil, and grated parmesan. Rub in 
thoroughly to ensure both sides are evenly coated, 
then set aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2
Cut the washed potatoes into 1-inch cubes. Place 
them in a pot of water and boil for 12–14 minutes 
until fork-tender. Drain the water and allow the 
remaining steam to evaporate any excess moisture.
	
z S T E P 3
Using a potato masher, mash the potatoes until 
smooth and fluffy. Be careful not to over-mash; you 
want to maintain a pleasant texture.
	
z S T E P 4
Stir in a knob of butter, evaporated milk, garlic 
powder, salt, and freshly cracked black pepper. 
Mix until the mash is smooth and well combined.
	
z S T E P 5
Heat a pan over medium-low heat and cook the 
marinated chicken for 4 minutes per side until 
golden and crispy, forming a flavor-packed crust. 
Once cooked, set the chicken aside. Repeat for the 
remaining chicken cutlets.
	
z S T E P 6
In the same pan on low heat, add the same spices 
used for the chicken to toast in the remaining oil. 
Then, stir in milk, light cream cheese, and parmesan 
until the sauce is smooth and well blended.
	
z S T E P 7 :
Continue stirring the sauce until it becomes 
completely smooth and creamy, letting it bubble 
slightly to thicken. Chop the rested chicken into 
bite-sized pieces, then serve by layering the 
creamy mash with the sauce and topping with the 
chopped chicken. ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Coat the chicken evenly with seasoning on both sides; add more if needed.
	
z
Cook on medium-low heat for 3–4 minutes in a non-stick pan (no butter or oil) until a golden crust forms.
	
z
(Optional) Peel potatoes, boil until fork-tender (12–14 minutes), then drain and mash until smooth.
	
z
For reheating, warm everything in a pan on low for 5 minutes; store the creamy sauce separately for best 
results.

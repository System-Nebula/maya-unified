Butter Chicken Spice Mix (1 serving)
	
– 1 Tsp Salt

– 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1 Tsp Paprika
	
– 1 Tsp Garam Masala
	
– 1 Tsp Turmeric
	
– ½ Tsp Chilli Powder
Butter Chicken Sauce
	
– 20g (1oz) Grass Fed Butter
	
– 150g (5.3oz) White Onion, thinly sliced
	
– 1 Serving Butter Chicken Spice Mix
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Cashew Nuts
	
– 400g (14oz) Fresh Tomatoes, chopped 
(or 400g canned tomatoes)
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Fat Free Yogurt
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken into cubes and place in a bowl. 
Add fresh garlic, butter chicken spice mix, yogurt, 
and lemon juice. Toss until evenly coated and set 
aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2 
Wash the potatoes and cut them into fries. Season 
with salt, garlic, paprika, cumin, garam masala, and 
olive oil. Toss well, then spread on a baking tray. Air 
fry at 200°C (400°F) for 20 minutes until golden and 
crispy.
	
z S T E P 3 
While the fries are cooking, heat a pan over medium-
high heat and melt butter. Add sliced onions and 
sauté for 5 minutes until soft and fragrant.
	
z S T E P 4
Stir in chopped tomatoes, extra butter chicken 
spice mix, and cashew nuts. »»
	» Continue
Simmer for 8–10 minutes until the mixture reduces 
to a sauce-like consistency.
	
z S T E P 5
Turn off the heat and stir in yogurt. Let the mixture 
cool slightly before transferring it to a blender. 
Blend for 2–3 minutes until smooth. Allow the sauce 
to cool further if needed.
	
z S T E P 6 
In a clean pan over high heat, cook the marinated 
chicken for 8 minutes until golden. Reduce the heat 
to medium and add the blended butter chicken 
sauce. Stir until the chicken is well coated.
	
z S T E P 7
Gently combine the chicken with the crispy fries. 
Top with cheese and cover the pan for 2 minutes 
to allow the cheese to melt. Garnish with freshly 
chopped coriander and a sprinkle of chilli flakes.

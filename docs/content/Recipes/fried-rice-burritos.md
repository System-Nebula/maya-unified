```
Fried Rice Burritos
If you love egg fried rice and burritos, why not combine the 2? One of my new favorite recipes, 
tastes so delicious and is perfect to prep in batches to store in the fridge or freezer!
Macros per serving
470 CALORIES
 43g PROTEIN
34g  CARBS
18g FAT
Ingredients (2-3 burritos)
Chicken:
	
z
300g (10.6oz) Raw Chicken 
Breast Cut Into Cubes
	
z
1 Tsp Paprika
	
z
1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
z
1 Tsp Black Pepper
	
z
1.5 Tbsp Dark Soy Sauce 
Fried rice:
	
z
1 Tsp Olive Oil
	
z
130g (4.6oz) Mixed Veg 
(Carrot, Peas, Sweetcorn) 
	
z
2 Eggs
	
z
170g (6oz) Cooked Rice 
(Leftover Rice Works Best) 
	
z
1 Tbsp Soy Sauce
	
z
1 Tbsp Oyster Sauce 
	
z
1 Tsp Sesame Oil
Burrito:
	
z
Low Calorie Tortilla 
Wrap 
	
z
50g (1.3oz) Fried Rice Filling
	
z
80-100g (3.5oz) 
Chicken Pieces
	
z
Pickled Red Onions 
	
z
10g (0.3oz) Spicy 
Lemon Mayo
	
z
Green onion
How to make
1.	 Cut the chicken breast into cubes and season with paprika, black pepper, garlic powder, and soy sauce. 
Mix well.
2.	 Heat a pan on medium with olive oil or cooking spray. Cook the chicken for 3–4 minutes on each side until 
golden, then set aside.
3.	 In the same pan, add mixed vegetables and cook for 1–2 minutes. Push the veg to one side, crack in the 
eggs and scramble, then mix together.
4.	 Add cooked rice, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Stir well, then add the chicken back in and 
combine everything.
5.	 Fill a low carb tortilla with the fried rice mix, extra chicken, pickled onions, spicy lemon mayo, and 
green onion. Fold like a burrito and enjoy!
```

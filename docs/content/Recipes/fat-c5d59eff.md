Chipotle Creamy Sauce
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Garlic Cloves, chopped
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Red Onion, chopped
	
– 1 Tsp Italian Herbs
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Chipotle Paste 
	
– 250g (9oz) Light Evaporated Milk (or any milk)
	
– 100g (3.5oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Parmigiano Reggiano, grated
	
– 1 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, chopped
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the steaks into 1 inch cubes. Season with salt, 
garlic powder, parsley, cumin, smoked paprika, 
lime juice, and olive oil. Mix thoroughly until each 
piece is well coated.
	
z S T E P 2 
Wash the potatoes and cut into small cubes. Place 
them in a bowl and mix with Italian herbs, garlic 
powder, paprika, salt, and olive oil until evenly coated.
	
z S T E P 3 
Arrange the seasoned potatoes on a lined baking 
tray (use baking paper). Oven bake or air fry at 
200°C/400°F for 20–22 minutes until golden and crispy.
	
z S T E P 4
While the potatoes are cooking, heat a pan over 
medium-high heat and add 5g of butter.Cook half 
of the steak bites for about 2-3 minutes per side 
until a beautiful brown crust forms.
	
z S T E P 5
Once cooked, lower the heat completely, then add 
5g extra butter and freshly chopped parsley to 
the pan. Toss the steak bites until each piece is well 
coated, then set aside. Repeat for the second batch.
	
z S T E P 6
In the same pan over medium-low heat, add 
chopped garlic and red onion. Sauté for 5 minutes 
until softened, then stir in Italian herbs and chipotle 
paste for 2-3 minutes. Next, add evaporated milk, 
cream cheese, and grated parmesan; stir until the 
sauce is smooth and creamy.
	
z S T E P 7
Drop the crispy potatoes and fold into the sauce, 
ensuring every piece is well coated. Divide the dish 
evenly with the steak bites, and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES

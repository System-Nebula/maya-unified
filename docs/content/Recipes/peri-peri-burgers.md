How to make
1.	 Season chicken thighs with garlic powder, smoked paprika, cumin, onion powder, salt, pepper, Nando’s 
marinade, and lemon juice. Mix well (optional: marinate for 30 mins).
2.	 Heat a pan on medium, add light butter, and cook the chicken for 5–7 minutes on each side until a good 
crust forms. Set aside to rest.
3.	 In a bowl, combine all the listed ingredients to make the Perinaise. Mix until smooth and well combined.
4.	 Toast brioche buns. Layer with Perinaise, lettuce, tomato slices, one chicken piece, a cheese slice, 
then the second chicken piece and pickled red onions.
5.	 Serve hot and enjoy!

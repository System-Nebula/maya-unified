INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Chicken & Seasoning
	
– 700g (25oz) Raw Chicken Breast, cubed
	
– 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 1 Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 1 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Hot Sauce
	
– 2 Eggs, whisked
Crispy Coating
	
– 90g (3.2oz) Crushed Cornflakes
	
– 1/2 Tsp each: Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, 
Smoked Paprika, Salt, Black Pepper
Mac n Cheese + Sauce
	
– 200g (7oz) Uncooked Macaroni 
Pasta (or pasta of choice)
	
– 350g (12.4oz) 1% Milk
	
– 120g (4.2oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 60g (2.1oz) Low Fat Cheddar Cheese
	
– 1/2 Tsp each: Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, 
Smoked Paprika, Salt, Black Pepper
	
– 60g (1/4 Cup) Pasta Water
Spicy Honey Glaze
	
– 25g (1oz) Honey
	
– 25g (1oz) Hot Sauce
	
– Juice of 1/2 Lemon
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken into bite-sized pieces, then 
season with garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, 
salt, pepper, hot sauce and whisked eggs. Mix 
well until evenly coated in the wet batter. Set up the 
crushed cornflakes coating station.
	
z S T E P 2 
While the chicken marinates, cook the macaroni 
in boiling water according to packet instructions. 
Reserve some pasta water before draining, then set 
aside.
	
z S T E P 3
Drop and coat each chicken piece in the crushed 
cornflakes station. Ensure every piece is evenly 
coated for a crispy finish.
	
z S T E P 4
Place the coated chicken bites on a lined baking 
tray or in the air fryer basket. Spray lightly with »»
	» Continue
cooking spray, then oven bake or air fry at 200°C 
(400°F) for 18–20 minutes, until crispy and golden.
	
z S T E P 5
In a blender, combine milk, cream cheese, grated 
cheddar, and the same spices used for the 
chicken. Blend until smooth and creamy.
	
z S T E P 6
Pour the blended sauce into a pan over medium-
low heat. Stir for 2–3 minutes until slightly thickened, 
then add the cooked macaroni along with some 
reserved pasta water to adjust consistency. Mix 
well until the pasta is creamy and fully coated. 
Garnish with parsley.
	
z S T E P 7
Make the sticky glaze by combining honey, hot 
sauce and squeeze of lemon juice. Drizzle over the 
crispy chicken bites till well coated.
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Air fry at 200°C for 18 minutes or oven bake at 200°C for 25 minutes until golden and crispy.
	
z
Adding egg wash helps the crumbs stick better to the chicken for a crispier coating.
	
z
When coating, let excess egg drip off before pressing into the crumbs for an even layer.
	
z
You can use chicken strips instead of cubes—slice them into smaller pieces when serving if preferred.
	
z
No blender? Simply mix the sauce ingredients directly in the pan over low heat until smooth and creamy.
	
z
Store chicken and mac separately for meal prep. Air fry the chicken to make it crispy again when reheating.

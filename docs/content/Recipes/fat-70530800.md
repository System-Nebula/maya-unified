INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1
Slice the chicken breasts into thin strips and place 
them in a bowl. Add olive oil, minced garlic, 1.5 
servings of shawarma spice mix, and a squeeze of 
lemon juice. Mix thoroughly until well coated—if the 
color isn’t vibrant, add more seasoning. Let marinate 
while prepping the fries.
	
z S T E P 2
Slice the potatoes into thin fries and season with 
salt, pepper, paprika, cumin, and olive oil. Toss until 
evenly coated, then spread on a lined baking tray 
(use baking paper). Bake or air fry at 200°C/400°F 
for 20 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and 
crispy.

Bake or air fry at 200°C/400°F for 15 minutes, until 
golden brown with slightly charred edges. If possible, 
cook the chicken and fries at the same time to save 
time, or cook separately if needed.
	
z S T E P 4
While the chicken and fries cook, prepare the white 
sauce. In a bowl, whisk together sour cream, mayo, 
minced garlic, black pepper, and a squeeze of 
lemon juice until smooth. Transfer to a bottle and 
refrigerate for meal prep use.
	
z S T E P 5
To assemble, layer the shawarma-spiced chicken 
over the crispy fries. Top with pickled red onions, 
crumbled feta cheese, and a generous drizzle of 
white sauce. Serve and ENJOY!

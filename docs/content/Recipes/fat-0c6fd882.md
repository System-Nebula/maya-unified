INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Honey BBQ Chicken
	
– 700g (25oz) Raw Chicken Breast, cubed
	
– 20g (1.5 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 1 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Paprika
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Honey
	
– 40g (1.5oz) Reduced Sugar BBQ Sauce
Mac N Cheese
	
– 225g (8oz) Uncooked Macaroni Pasta
	
– 250g (8.8oz) 1% Milk
	
– 60g (2.1oz) Buffalo Hot Sauce (or any hot sauce)
	
– 120g (4.2oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Low Fat Cheddar Cheese
	
– Garnish with Fresh Parsley, chopped
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Cut the chicken breast into bite-sized cubes. 
In a bowl, mix with minced garlic, black pepper, 
paprika, salt, and olive oil until evenly coated. 
Adjust seasoning if you need a deeper color.
	
z S T E P 2 
Meanwhile, cook the macaroni pasta in a pot of 
water for 8 mins until al dente. Drain and set aside.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan over medium-high heat and lightly coat 
with cooking spray. Sear the chicken cubes for 
3–4 minutes per side until they are golden brown 
and nicely charred.
	
z S T E P 4
Turn off the heat completely, then drizzle honey and 
BBQ sauce over the chicken. Toss well until every 
piece is coated and juicy, then transfer to a bowl. 
Repeat for the remaining chicken.
	
z S T E P 5
In the same pan over low heat, add milk, hot 
sauce (such as buffalo sauce), cream cheese, 
and shredded cheddar cheese. Whisk gently until 
the mixture is smooth and creamy.
	
z S T E P 6
Stir the cooked macaroni into the cheese sauce 
until thoroughly combined. Garnish with chopped 
parsley, then serve with the delicious BBQ 
chicken, divide into 4 equal servings and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Cook chicken on medium-high heat for 3–4 minutes per side until golden brown.
	
z
Turn off the heat before adding honey and BBQ sauce to prevent burning.
	
z
For the creamy sauce, add all ingredients on low heat and stir gently (preferably with a whisk) until thick 
and smooth.
	
z
Store in the fridge for up to 4 days or freeze for later. Reheat in a pan or microwave when ready to eat.

TAP FOR VIDEO

INGREDIENTS (5 SERVINGS)
Crispy Honey Garlic Chicken
	
– 1100g Raw Boneless Chicken Thighs, cubed (39oz)
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 1.5 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 1 Tsp Cinnamon
	
– 1.5 Tsp Coriander Powder
	
– 3 Tsp Olive Oil
	
– 80g Yogurt, plain or Greek (3oz)
	
– 30g Honey (1.1oz)
Pickled Onions
	
– 200g Red Onions, thinly sliced (7.1oz)
	
– 200g Red Onions, thinly sliced (7.1oz)
	
– Big Handful Fresh Parsley, chopped
	
– 1.5 Tbsp Sumac (Middle Eastern spice)
	
– Pinch of Salt
	
– 1 Whole Lemon Juiced
Golden Aromatic Rice
	
– 250g Uncooked Basmati Rice (cook in 
advance) (8.8oz / ~1.5 cups uncooked)
	
– 20g Light Butter (1oz)
	
– Few Cinnamon Sticks or cinnamon powder
	
– 1/2 – 1 Tsp Turmeric
	
– 1/2 Tsp Salt
	
– 1/2 Tsp Paprika
	
– 1 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, chopped
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1
Trim any excess fat from the boneless chicken 
thighs, then slice into cubes and place in a mixing 
bowl. Season with salt, black pepper, paprika, garlic 
powder, onion powder, cinnamon, and coriander 
powder, then add a drizzle of olive oil, yogurt, and 
honey. Mix until fully coated and the colour deepens, 
then set aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2
In a large mixing bowl, add sliced red onion, plenty 
of freshly chopped parsley, sumac, a pinch of salt, 
and a squeeze of lemon juice. Mix well, then store in 
a separate container and refrigerate.
	
z S T E P 3
Rinse the uncooked basmati rice, then place it in a pot 
of water and bring to a boil. Reduce to a medium »»
	» Continue
low simmer and cook until fully tender and fluffy. 
Meanwhile, line a sheet pan with parchment paper 
and spread the marinated chicken in a single, even 
layer. Oven bake or air fry for 25 minutes, until 
golden orange with crispy edges.
	
z S T E P 4
In a large pan over low heat, melt butter, then add 
a few cinnamon sticks. Cook for 2-3 minutes, 
until fragrant, before adding the cooked basmati 
rice, along with turmeric, salt, paprika, and freshly 
chopped parsley. Mix until every grain is golden 
yellow, fluffy, and aromatic.
	
z S T E P 5
Serve the crispy, juicy honey garlic chicken with the 
pickled red onions, then ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Marinate the chicken for longer if possible. The yogurt, spices, and honey help tenderise the
	
z

z
Infuse the butter properly with cinnamon sticks over low heat. Rushing this step reduces aroma and depth 
of flavour in the rice.
	
z
Rest the chicken briefly before serving, then mix with any tray juices for extra flavour and
	
z
moisture.

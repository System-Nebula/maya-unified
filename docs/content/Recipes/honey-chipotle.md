INGREDIENTS
Honey Chipotle Chicken Marinade
	
– 1000g (35oz / 2.2lb) Raw Boneless 
Skinless Chicken Thighs 
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt 
	
– 1.5 Tsp Oregano 
	
– 1.5 Tsp Garlic Powder 
	
– 1.5 Tsp Smoked Paprika 
	
– 1.5 Tsp Cumin 
	
– 70g (2.5oz / ~3 Tbsp) Chipotle Paste

– 50g (1.8oz / ~2.5 Tbsp) Honey
	
– Juice of 3/4 Lime  »»

– 200g (7oz) Fresh Pineapple, chopped into chunks
Chipotle Sauce
	
– 100g Low Fat Yogurt (3.5oz / ~4 Tbsp) 
	
– 50g Light Mayo (1.8oz / ~3 Tbsp) 
	
– 20-30g Chipotle Paste (0.7-1oz / 1-2 Tbsp) 
	
– 50g Pineapple Chunks, fresh preferred (1.8oz) 
	
– 1/2 Tsp Salt 
	
– 1/2 Tsp Garlic Powder 
	
– 1/2 Tsp Onion Powder 
	
– Squeeze of Lime Juice
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Place the boneless, skinless chicken thighs in a 
bowl and season with salt, oregano, garlic powder, 
smoked paprika, cumin, chipotle paste, honey, and 
fresh lime juice. Mix thoroughly until the chicken is 
fully coated and deeply coloured, then set aside to 
marinate.
	
z S T E P 2 
Lay the marinated chicken thighs evenly on a lined 
sheet pan, then scatter pineapple chunks around 
the chicken. This allows the pineapple to caramelise 
and soften while releasing extra sweetness and 
moisture that enhances the flavour of the chicken. 
Oven bake or air fry at 200°C / 400°F for 20-23 
minutes, or until golden, juicy, and slightly charred 
at the edges, with the pineapple lightly caramelised.
	
z S T E P 3
While the chicken rests, make the pineapple chipotle 
sauce. In a blender cup add yogurt, mayo, chipotle 
paste, pineapple chunks, salt, garlic powder, onion 
powder, and lime juice. Blend for 30-60 seconds 
until completely smooth and creamy.
	
z S T E P 4
Bottle up the sauce for the week (great for wraps 
and bowls). For meal prep, keep the chicken whole 
when storing to retain juiciness, and include any 
tray juices for extra flavour and moisture. Allow the 
chicken to cool completely, then store the whole 
thighs in airtight containers. Slice into bite-sized 
cubes only when serving for maximum flavour and 
moisture retention and add the pineapple chipotle 
sauce as needed throughout the week. ENJOY!
BEST WAYS TO SERVE AND PAIR THIS PROTEIN 
This Honey Chipotle Chicken works extremely well in simple, flavourful combinations, for example:
	
–with coconut lime rice and pineapple salsa 
	
–with crispy roasted potatoes and corn salad 
	
–in a tortilla wrap with crunchy slaw and pickles
SAUCE PAIRING SUGGESTIONS 
This chicken also pairs well with:
	
–pineapple chipotle sauce (included in this recipe) 
	
–lime yogurt sauce 
	
–spicy mayo

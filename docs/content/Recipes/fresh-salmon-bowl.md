How to make
1.	 Cut the salmon into bite-sized cubes and prepare your marinade by 
mixing your preferred ingredients. Coat the salmon evenly with the 
marinade and let it sit briefly to absorb the flavors.
2.	 Heat a pan over medium heat and add a little oil or cooking spray. 
Cook the salmon cubes for about 2 minutes on each side until golden 
and just cooked through. Set aside.
3.	 In a large serving bowl, start by adding your cooked rice as the base.
4.	 Arrange the cooked salmon along with the avocado, cucumber, 
edamame, mango, and carrots, and place evenly around the rice.
5.	 Drizzle some extra dressing over the top and sprinkle with sesame 
seeds. Serve immediately and enjoy!

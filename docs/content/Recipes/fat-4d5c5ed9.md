FAT
Combining 2 very popular takeout meals! This tastes absolutely 
delicious, is low in calories, filling and easy to make for the week 
ahead!

INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Crispy Chicken
	
– 700g (25oz) Raw Chicken Breast, cubed
	
– 20g (1.5 Tbsp) Dark Soy Sauce
	
– 30g (2 Tbsp) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 1.5 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 25-30g (2 Tbsp) Corn Flour/Corn Starch
	
– 2-3 Tsp Olive Oil (for cooking)
Salt n Pepper Chips
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Potatoes, 
cut into chips »»
	» Continue
	
– 1 Tsp each Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder, Paprika 
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
For Cooking
	
– 100g (3.5oz) White Onion, chopped
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Red Bell Peppers, chopped
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Green Bell Peppers, chopped
	
– 1 Tsp each Salt, Pepper, Garlic 
Powder, Paprika, Chilli Flakes
	
– 20g (1.5 Tbsp) Honey
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Green Onion, chopped
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breasts into cubes. In a bowl, 
marinate the cubes with soy sauce, black pepper, 
garlic powder, and cornflour. Mix thoroughly until 
the color changes; if needed, add more soy sauce 
and spices.
	
z S T E P 2 
Slice your washed potatoes into chips. Arrange 
them on a lined baking tray (use baking paper) and 
season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, 
and olive oil until evenly coated. Oven bake at 
210°C/420°F for 25 minutes or air fry for 15 minutes.
	
z S T E P 3 
While the chips are cooking, heat a pan over medium 
heat and add olive oil. Cook the marinated chicken 
for 4–5 minutes on each side until a golden-brown, 
crispy crust forms. If needed, cook a few extra 
minutes. Remove the chicken and set aside, cooking 
in batches if necessary.
	
z S T E P 4
In the same pan, over medium heat, add the 
chopped onions and bell peppers. Sauté for 2–4 
minutes until softened. Then add extra salt, pepper, 
garlic powder, paprika, chilli flakes, a drizzle of 
honey, the cooked chips, and chopped green 
onion.
	
z S T E P 5
Mix everything together until well incorporated and 
let cook for an additional 1–2 minutes. Finally, divide 
into 4 equal servings and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Mix chicken thoroughly until dark and rich in color. Add more spices or soy sauce if needed. 
	
z
Avoid overcrowding the pan to ensure a crispy crust on the chicken.
	
z
Bake fries for 25 minutes or air fry for 15 minutes at 210°C (420°F).
	
z
Reheat fries in a pan or air fryer to restore crispiness.

How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken breast into thin strips. Place in a bowl, add minced 
garlic, oregano, paprika, onion powder, chili flakes, salt, pepper, and 
lemon juice. Mix everything well to evenly coat the chicken strips with the 
seasoning.
2.	 Heat a pan over medium heat and lightly coat it with cooking spray or 
a small amount of olive oil. Add the marinated chicken strips to the 
pan and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is golden 
brown and cooked through.
3.	 In a separate bowl, combine fat-free yogurt, sriracha, minced garlic, 
fresh parsley, salt, pepper, and a splash of lemon juice. Stir until smooth 
and well combined.
4.	 Take a low-calorie wrap and lay down a bed of shredded lettuce. Top 
with the cooked chicken strips, red onion, tomatoes, and a generous 
amount of the spicy sauce.
5.	 If you prefer a toasted wrap, heat a pan over medium heat and lightly 
toast the wrap for 1-2 minutes on each side until crispy and golden. Serve 
immediately and enjoy your flavorful chicken wrap!

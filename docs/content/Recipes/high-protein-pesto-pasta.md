How to make
1.	 Fill a pot with water, add 1 tsp of salt, bring to a boil, then cook the spaghetti for 8 minutes. Reserve ½ cup 
of pasta water, then drain the rest.
2.	 Slice your chicken breast and season both sides evenly with garlic powder, paprika, basil, salt, and pepper.
3.	 In a blender cup, combine spinach, basil, olive oil, garlic, cream cheese, parmesan, cashew nuts, salt, 
pepper, lemon juice, and reserved pasta water. Blend until completely smooth to make the pesto sauce.
4.	 Heat a pan on medium heat, spray with cooking spray, then cook the chicken for 5 minutes on each side 
until golden and cooked through. Set aside.
5.	 Lower the heat, add the blended pesto sauce to the same pan, then add the cooked spaghetti. Mix until 
well combined.
6.	 Place the chicken pieces on top, garnish with extra basil, parmesan, and chilli flakes. Serve and ENJOY!

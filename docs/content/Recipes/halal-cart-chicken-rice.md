How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken into cubes and season with olive oil, lemon juice, and 
all the listed spices. Mix well until fully coated.
2.	 In a pot, add washed uncooked rice, the spices, and water. Bring to a 
light bubble, then cover and simmer on low heat until the rice is fully 
cooked and fluffy.
3.	 While the rice cooks, heat a pan over medium-high heat. Cook the 
chicken for 3–4 minutes on each side until golden brown. Avoid 
overcrowding the pan to get a nice sear.
4.	 In a bowl, make the sauce by combining yogurt, light mayo, fresh 
garlic, chopped coriander, salt, pepper, cumin, and lemon juice. Mix 
until smooth and well blended.
5.	 Once everything is cooked, assemble the dish by plating the rice and 
topping it with the golden chicken.
6.	 Drizzle the garlic yogurt sauce over the top, garnish if you like, and 
enjoy — or distribute into 4 meal prep containers for later!
When reheating the meal prep, 
i would put the sauce and lettuce

chicken in a microwave.

containers in the meal prep box.

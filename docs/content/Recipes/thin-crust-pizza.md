z
1 Tsp Salt & Pepper
	
z
1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
z
1 Tsp Paprika
	
z
1 Tsp Italian Herbs
How to make
1.	 Cut the chicken breast into small bite-sized pieces and season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and 
paprika. Mix well to evenly coat, then cook over high heat for 5 minutes until golden. Set aside once cooked.
2.	 Place a flatbread on a pizza tray and spread a thin, even layer of tomato paste over the surface.
3.	 Top the flatbread with mozzarella, the cooked chicken pieces, sliced red onion, bell pepper, cherry 
tomatoes, and a generous sprinkle of Italian herbs.
4.	 Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 10 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
5.	 Remove from the oven and serve immediately. It’s light enough to enjoy the whole pizza yourself—enjoy!

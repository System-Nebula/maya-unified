INGREDIENTS
Taco Spice Mix (1 Serving)
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 1 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 1 Tsp Paprika
	
– 1 Tsp Oregano
	
– 1 Tsp Cumin
	
– 1/2-1 Tsp Chilli Powder
(adjust heat to preference)
For 1000g beef, you will use 2 servings of this spice 
mix.
Juicy Jalapeño Beef
	
– 1000g (35oz / 2.2lb) Raw 95% Lean Ground Beef
	
– 40g Tomato Paste (1.4oz / ~2.5 Tbsp)
	
– 2 Servings Taco Spice Mix
	
– 50g Chopped Pickled Jalapeños (1.8oz)
	
– 50g Jalapeño Juice (1.8oz)
	
– 30g Reduced-Fat Butter (1.1oz)
Nacho Cheese Sauce
	
– 200g (7oz) Light Evaporated Milk
	
– 6x20g Slices (120g/4.2oz) American Cheese Slices
	
– 80g (2.8oz) Light Cheddar or Sharp Cheddar
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Jalapeño Juice (Optional)
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 : C O O K T H E B E E F
Heat a large pan over medium high heat, add the 
butter, then cook the ground beef for 5-7 minutes, 
breaking it apart with a spatula until fully browned 
with no pink remaining.
	
z S T E P 2 : A D D S E A S O N I N G S
Once browned, stir in the taco spice mix, tomato 
paste, chopped jalapeños, and jalapeño juice. Cook 
for 2 more minutes until the flavours intensify and »»
	» Continue
the beef turns juicy and fragrant. Set aside.
	
z STEP 3: MAKE THE NACHO CHEESE SAUCE
Using the same pan on low heat, add the 
evaporated milk, American cheese slices, cheddar, 
and a splash of jalapeño juice (optional). Stir 
continuously until the cheese melts and the sauce 
becomes silky and smooth. Transfer to a container 
and refrigerate for later use.
BEST WAYS TO SERVE AND PAIR THIS PROTEIN
This Nacho Ground Beef works extremely well in bold, Tex-Mex inspired combinations, for example:
	
–with Mexican rice or tomato lime rice
	
–in a warm tortilla wrap with lettuce, jalapeños, and pico de gallo
	
–in a loaded salad bowl with black beans, corn, salsa, and crisp lettuce
SAUCE PAIRING SUGGESTIONS
This beef also pairs well with:
	
–nacho cheese sauce (included in this recipe)
	
–chipotle yogurt sauce (yogurt, chipotle, lime)
	
–salsa verde or tomatillo dressing

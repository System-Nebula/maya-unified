INGREDIENTS (5 SERVINGS)
Crispy Nashville Hot Chicken
	
– 1000g (35oz) Boneless Skinless 
Chicken Thighs, cubed
	
– 1.5 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2.5 Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 1.5 Tsp Chilli Powder
	
– 2 Tbsp Baking Powder
	
– 2 Tbsp Dark Brown Sugar
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Reduced Fat Butter (for cooking)
	
– Dry Spice Rub - Smoked Paprika, Garlic 
Powder, Onion Powder (once cooked)
	
– 2 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, chopped
Creamy Mac n Cheese
	
– 270g (9.5oz) Uncooked Macaroni Pasta
	
– 350g (12.3oz) 1% Milk
	
– 200g (7.1oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Low Fat Sharp Cheddar
	
– 1.5 Tbsp Mustard
	
– 1 Tsp Each: Smoked Paprika, Garlic & Onion Powder
	
– 1/2 Tsp each: Black Pepper, Chilli Powder
	
– 1.5 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, chopped
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1
Slice the boneless chicken thighs into cubes and 
place in a mixing bowl. Season with black pepper, 
smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, 
chilli powder, baking powder, dark brown sugar, 
and a drizzle of olive oil. Mix thoroughly with your 
hands until well coated, then set aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2
Bring a pot of water to a boil, add the uncooked 
macaroni, and cook for 8 minutes until al dente. 
Drain and set aside for later.
	
z S T E P 3
Heat a pan on medium heat, melt some butter, then 
cook the marinated chicken for 5 minutes on each 
side (don’t over crowd the pan) until golden brown 
with a crispy crust. Lower the heat, add a little more 
butter, a dry spice mix (smoked paprika, garlic 
powder, onion powder), and fresh parsley. Stir until 
the chicken is juicy and well coated, then set aside. 
Repeat for any remaining chicken.
	
z S T E P 4
Using the same pan on low heat, add low fat 
milk, light cream cheese, low fat cheddar, dijon 
mustard, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion 
powder, black pepper, chilli powder, and chopped 
parsley. Stir continuously until the sauce is smooth, 
melted, and creamy.
	
z S T E P 5
Add the cooked macaroni to the sauce and gently 
fold until evenly combined and creamy. Divide the 
mac and cheese with the crispy Nashville chicken 
into 5 portions - your high protein meals are ready 
to ENJOY!

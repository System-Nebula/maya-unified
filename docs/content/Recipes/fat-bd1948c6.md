Taco Spice Mix (1 Serving)
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 1 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 1 Tsp Paprika
	
– 1 Tsp Oregano
	
– 1 Tsp Onion
	
– 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1 Tsp Cumin
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the potatoes into fries, then season with salt, 
oregano, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked 
paprika, cumin, and olive oil. Toss until evenly 
coated, then oven bake or air fry for about 20 
minutes at 200°C (400°F) until golden and crispy 
then set aside.
	
z S T E P 2 
For the garlic sauce, combine yoghurt, mayo, salt, 
garlic powder, onion powder, and squeeze of lime 
juice in a bowl. Mix until smooth and creamy, then 
set aside.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan over medium-high heat, add light butter 
and chopped onions. Sauté for about 8 minutes until 
soft and caramelised, then remove and set aside.
	
z S T E P 4
In the same pan, cook the minced beef on medium-
high heat for 6 minutes until browned. Add 1 serving 
taco spice mix and the caramelised onions. Cook 
for another 4–5 minutes until well combined and the 
beef deepens in color.
	
z S T E P 5
Lower the heat, sprinkle over cheddar cheese, and 
cover the pan for a minute or two until the cheese 
melts.
	
z S T E P 6
Divide the crispy fries into portions, top with the 
cheesy taco beef, freshly chopped red onion, 
jalapenos, tomatoes, fresh corinader then drizzle 
generously with garlic sauce and sriracha, and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z

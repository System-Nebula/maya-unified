FAT
Crispy chicken coated in a low calorie dynamite sauce paired 
with seasoned fries - doesn’t get better than this.
TAP FOR VIDEO
THE MEAL PREP COOK BOOK 
183

INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Chicken Marinade
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Chicken Breast, cubed
	
– 2 Tsp Oregano
	
– 1.5 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1.5 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 1.5 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 20g (1oz) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 20g (1oz) Dark Soy Sauce
	
– 45g (1.6oz) Cornflour/Corn Starch (optional)
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil (for cooking)
Dynamite Sauce
	
– 80g (3oz) 50% Fat Mayo
	
– 25g (1oz) Chilli Garlic Sauce
	
– 20g (1oz) Sriracha »»
	» Continue
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Reduced Sugar Sweet Chilli Sauce
	
– Juice of 1/4 Lemon
Crispy Fries
	
– 800g (28oz) Uncooked Potatoes, cut into fries
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 1.5 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Oregano
	
– 1.5 Tsp Paprika
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil or Cooking Spray
Garnish
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Green Onion, chopped
	
– 2 Tsp Sesame Seeds
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breasts into cubes and season 
with oregano, garlic powder, onion powder, black 
pepper, soy sauce, and cornflour. Mix thoroughly 
until evenly coated.
	
z S T E P 2 
Cut the potatoes into fries, then season with salt, 
pepper, oregano, paprika, garlic powder, and olive 
oil. Mix well, then spread on a lined baking tray then 
oven-bake or air fry for 20-22 minutes at 200°C 
(400°F) until golden and crispy.
	
z S T E P 3 
For the dynamite sauce, combine mayo, chili garlic 
sauce, sriracha, sweet chili sauce, and lemon juice 
in a bowl. Mix until smooth and set aside.
	
z S T E P 4
Heat a pan over medium-high heat, add a little olive 
oil, and cook the seasoned chicken for 4 minutes 
per side until golden and a crispy crust forms then 
set aside. Repeat for the remaining chicken.
	
z S T E P 5
Mix in the cooked juicy chicken in the dynamite 
sauce until fully coated and indulgent.
	
z S T E P 6
Distribute the crispy fries and saucy dynamite 
chicken into 4 equal servings, garnish with green 
onion and sesame seeds, then ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Ensure the chicken is well coated in cornflour for the crispiest texture.
	
z
To reduce calories, skip the cornflour—the chicken will still be flavorful!
	
z
If air frying, use cooking spray instead of olive oil and avoid over-seasoning the fries.
	
z
Make the dynamite sauce in batches for easy storage; swap chili garlic sauce for chili flakes and garlic 
powder if needed.

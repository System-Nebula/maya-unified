KFC Zinger Rice Box

z

z
1/2 Chopped Onion, 
	
z

z

z
1 Tsp Paprika, Cumin and Salt
Bring To a Boil Then Cover and Simmer 
On Low Heat Until Fully Cooked
Serve With Shredded Lettuce, 
Sweetcorn, and Bell pepper
How to make
1.	 Slice your chicken breasts in halves, then season both sides with smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion 
salt, and chilli powder. Ensure they’re evenly coated.
2.	 Set up your breading stations. Plate 1: crushed cornflakes mixed with the same spices as the chicken. 
Plate 2: beaten eggs or egg whites.
3.	 Dip the chicken into the eggs, let the excess drip off, then coat in the cornflake crumbs. Place on a baking tray.
4.	 Bake in a preheated oven or air fry at 190°C / 380°F for 19 minutes until golden and crispy.
5.	 While the chicken cooks, heat olive oil in a pot, add diced onion and bell pepper, cook for 1 minute. Add 
tomato paste, uncooked rice, paprika, cumin, and salt. Simmer until the rice is fully cooked.
6.	 Make the garlic mayo, then assemble your bowl with a bed of rice, lettuce, sweetcorn, bell pepper, the 
crispy chicken, and a generous drizzle of garlic mayo. ENJOY!

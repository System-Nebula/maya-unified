How to make
1.	 Cut the chicken breasts in half to create thinner pieces. In a bowl, mix 
together parmesan, paprika, oregano, garlic powder, and salt. Use 
this mixture to thoroughly coat each piece of chicken.
2.	 Heat a pan over medium-high heat, spray with oil, and cook the chicken 
for 4 minutes on one side. Flip the chicken, immediately lower the heat, 
and add chopped tomatoes around the chicken in the pan.
3.	 Season the tomatoes with oregano, garlic powder, and salt, then 
place mozzarella on top of each chicken piece. Cover the pan and let 
it cook for 2–3 minutes, or until the cheese is melted.
4.	 Once the cheese has melted and the chicken is fully cooked, remove 
from the heat and let it rest for a minute.
5.	 Serve the chicken over rice or your favorite carb source—this also 
pairs perfectly with some warm bread. Enjoy!

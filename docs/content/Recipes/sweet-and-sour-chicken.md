How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken into bite-sized pieces, then season with garlic powder, salt, black pepper, soy sauce, 
and corn flour. Mix well until all pieces are evenly coated. Chop your vegetables into square pieces and 
set aside.
2.	 In a separate bowl, combine ketchup (preferably low sugar), light soy sauce, light brown sugar, rice 
vinegar, corn flour, and water. Stir until the mixture is smooth and the sugar has fully dissolved.
3.	 Heat a pan on high with cooking spray or olive oil, then cook the chicken for about 8 minutes (4 minutes 
per side) until golden and crispy. Remove the chicken and set aside.
4.	 In the same pan, add chopped onions and bell peppers. Cook for 4 minutes until slightly soft, then pour 
in the sauce and let it simmer until it thickens.
5.	 Add the cooked chicken back into the pan, mix everything together until well coated, then garnish with 
green onion and sesame seeds. Serve with rice and enjoy!

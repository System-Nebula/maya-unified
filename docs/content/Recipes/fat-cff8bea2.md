INGREDIENTS (5 SERVINGS)
Chicken Marinade + Creamy Sauce
	
– 1000g Raw Chicken Breast cut into cubes
	
– 1 Tbsp (30g) Minced Garlic or Garlic Paste
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 2 Tsp Mixed Herbs (Oregano, Parsley, Basil)
	
– 2 Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– 2 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 1.5 Tsp Chilli Flakes 
(Optional if you don’t like spicy)
	
– 3 Tsp Olive Oil
	
– 20g Light Butter For Cooking
	
– 300ml Skimmed Milk »»
	» Continue
	
– 160g Garlic & Herb Cream Cheese
	
– 80g Shredded Mozzarella or any Low Fat Cheese
Crispy Potatoes
	
– 1100g Raw Yellow or White Potatoes 
cut into small cubes
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 1.5 Tsp Mixed Herbs
	
– 1.5 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1.5 Tsp Paprika
	
– (Optional) 2 Tsp Olive Oill
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
First slice your chicken breasts into cubes then 
add minced garlic, salt, mixed herbs, smoked 
paprika, onion powder, chilli flakes and olive oil. 
Mix till the colour changes!
	
z S T E P 2 
Now slice your raw potatoes into small cubes 
and place in a bowl. Season with salt, garlic 
powder, mixed herbs, paprika and olive oil. Mix till 
everything is well coated
	
z S T E P 3 
Place them on a lined sheet pan then oven bake or 
air fry for 20-22 mins till golden and crispy then set 
aside
	
z S T E P 4
Next get a pan on medium heat, melt down butter 
then cook your chicken for 4-5 mins each side till 
golden and juicy. You want to develop a golden 
brown crust! »»
	» Continue
Note You will need to cook off all the chicken first 
before moving with the rest of the recipe
	
z S T E P 5
Now lower the heat and add milk and garlic and 
herb cream cheese. Give this a mix till everything 
melts and is well combined
	
z S T E P 6 
Bring the sauce to a light bubble then add all your 
crispy potatoes in the pan with the chicken. Give 
it a gentle but thorough mix till fully incorporated
	
z S T E P 7
Final touch, add a layer of shredded mozzarella over 
the top then cover the pan with a lid for 3-4 mins to 
let the cheese melt!
	
z S T E P 8
Finally distribute into 5 even servings and your high 
protein meals are ready to enjoy for the week!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Cook the chicken on medium heat till you get a good sear. Don’t overcrowd the pan and cook in batches
	
z
When adding all the dairy ingredients to the pan, make sure the pan is on low heat to prevent splitting

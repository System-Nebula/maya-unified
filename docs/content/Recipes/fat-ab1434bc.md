INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Tender Beef Marinade
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Topside/Top 
Sirloin Beef, thinly sliced
	
– 2 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 20g (1oz) Dark Soy Sauce
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Oyster Sauce
	
– 1 Tsp Baking Soda (tenderiser)
	
– 20g (1oz) Reduced Fat 
Butter (for cooking)
Golden Fried Rice Mix
	
– 150g (5.3oz) Scallion/Green Onion (white part)
	
– 80g (2.8oz) Scallion/Green Onion (green part)
	
– 25g (2 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 4 Whole Eggs (separate egg yolks from egg whites)
	
– 560g (20oz) Day Old Cooked White Rice
	
– 20g (1oz) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 15g (0.7oz) Dark Soy Sauce
	
– 15g (0.7oz) Reduced Fat Butter
	
– Sriracha Drizzle when serving
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the beef into thin strips and place in a mixing 
bowl. Marinate with black pepper, light and dark soy 
sauce, oyster sauce, and baking soda. Mix thoroughly 
until velvety and well combined then set aside.
	
z S T E P 2 
Separate the egg yolks from the whites. In a large 
bowl, combine the cooked rice with the yolks, mixing 
until every grain is coated in a golden yellow hue.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan over medium-high heat and melt 
butter. Cook the marinated beef strips for 2–3 
minutes per side until a crispy crust forms. Avoid 
overcrowding the pan to ensure even browning. 
Once cooked, set the beef aside.
	
z S T E P 4
In the same pan on medium-low heat, »»
	» Continue
add the white parts of the green onion along with 
minced garlic. Sauté for 3–4 minutes until fragrant.
	
z S T E P 5
Push the sautéed onions and garlic to one side of 
the pan, then add the egg whites. Stir continuously 
until scrambled, then mix them with the onions.
	
z S T E P 6
Add the golden-coated rice to the pan, along with 
extra soy sauce, the green parts of the green 
onion, a small knob of butter, and the cooked 
beef. Give everything a thoroug mix until well 
combined and flavourful.
	
z S T E P 7
Serve and distribute evenly into 4 portions, add a 
drizzle of sriracha and your high protein meals are 
ready to enjoy!

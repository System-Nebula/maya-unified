How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken into cubes, season with soy sauce, garlic powder, and black pepper. Mix until well 
coated and set aside.
2.	 In a separate bowl, mix soy sauce, tamarind paste, fish sauce, lime juice, brown sugar, and water to 
create the sauce.
3.	 Place rice noodles in a large bowl with boiling water and let them soak for 4–5 minutes (or follow packet 
instructions), then drain and set aside.
4.	 Heat a pan or wok on medium, add cooking spray, and cook the chicken for 5 minutes until golden brown. 
Remove and set aside.
5.	 In the same pan, sauté chopped garlic, shallots, and green onion for 30 seconds. Push to one side, crack 
in 2 eggs, scramble, then mix together.
6.	 Add cooked noodles and the sauce, mix well, then add back the chicken with crushed peanuts, bean 
sprouts, and green onion. Toss everything together, garnish with extra peanuts and lime juice, then enjoy!

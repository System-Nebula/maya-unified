How to make
1.	
Slice the chicken breasts into thinner fillets. Season both sides generously with oregano, 
paprika, garlic powder, chili flakes, salt, black pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil. Mix until 
evenly coated, then set aside.
2.	 Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add the penne pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes until 
al dente. Once cooked, reserve ¼ cup of pasta water, then drain the rest.
3.	 Heat a pan over medium heat and add olive oil. Cook the chicken fillets in batches for 
3-4 minutes per side, until golden brown and fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
4.	 In the same pan on medium heat, add chopped onions and red bell pepper. Sauté for 2 
minutes until softened, then stir in tomato paste and cook for another 2-3 minutes until 
fragrant.
5.	 Lower the heat then add skimmed milk, light cream cheese, reserved pasta water, 
and Nando’s Peri Peri sauce. Stir until the sauce thickens and becomes creamy. Adjust 
seasoning, mix in fresh parsley, and add the cooked pasta. Toss until fully coated and 
creamy.

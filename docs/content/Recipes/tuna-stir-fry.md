How to make
1.	 Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add a small amount of olive oil. Once hot, add your chopped 
vegetables (like bell peppers, carrots, or onions) along with your spices of choice — garlic powder, black 
pepper, and a pinch of chili flakes work great. Sauté everything for about 2 minutes until the veggies start 
to soften.
2.	 Add in the canned tuna and stir it through the veggies, letting it cook for another 2 minutes to warm up and 
absorb the flavors.
3.	 Next, add your cooked rice followed by soy sauce and oyster sauce. Mix everything together thoroughly to 
coat the rice and tuna in the sauces. Let it cook for another 2 minutes so the flavors blend well.
4.	 Finish by sprinkling over some sesame seeds and freshly chopped spring onion. Serve warm and enjoy your 
quick and flavorful tuna fried rice!

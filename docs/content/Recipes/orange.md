Orange Pepper Chicken

– 2.5 Tsp Black Pepper 
	
– 2.5 Tsp Garlic Powder  »»

– 30g Honey (1.1oz / ~2 Tbsp) 
	
– 25g Sriracha (1oz / ~2 Tbsp) 
	
– Juice + Zest of 1 large Orange (add orange 
pulp for extra caramelised texture) 
	
– 25g Light Soy Sauce (1oz / ~2 Tbsp)
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Trim any excess fat from the boneless chicken 
thighs, then place them in a large mixing bowl. Add 
salt, black pepper, garlic powder, honey, sriracha, 
fresh orange juice, orange zest, and light soy 
sauce. Mix thoroughly until fully combined and the 
colour deepens. Set aside to marinate for at least 
30 minutes.
	
z S T E P 2 
Line a sheet pan with parchment paper. Spread the 
marinated chicken evenly in a single layer. Sprinkle a 
little extra garlic powder and black pepper, then add 
a touch more orange juice and zest over the top to 
help create a sticky, caramelised glaze as it cooks. »»
	» Continue
Air fry or oven bake at 200°C / 400°F for 25-27 
minutes, until golden brown, sizzling, and slightly 
charred.
	
z S T E P 3
Once cooked, allow the chicken to rest for a few 
minutes, then keep the thighs whole for storage. 
Pour in all the sheet-pan juices for added moisture 
and flavour. Slice into juicy cubes only when 
serving for maximum tenderness.
BEST WAYS TO SERVE AND PAIR THIS PROTEIN 
This Orange Pepper Chicken works extremely well in simple, flavourful combinations, for example:
	
–with garlic herb rice (meal prep separately) 
	
–in a warm wrap with lettuce and pickles 
	
–in a rice bowl with crunchy cabbage slaw
SAUCE PAIRING SUGGESTIONS 
This chicken also pairs well with:
	
–orange pepper drizzle (orange juice, garlic, soy, honey reduced lightly) 
	
–chilli yogurt sauce (yogurt, chilli, lime) 
	
–coconut aminos and lime dipping sauce

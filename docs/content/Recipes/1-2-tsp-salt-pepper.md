## Source Text

```
1/2 Tsp Salt & Pepper
	
z
1/4 Lemon Juice
How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken breasts into thinner fillets for quicker cooking. Season both 
sides evenly with spices, lemon juice, and olive oil. Set aside to marinate.
2.	 Thinly slice the onions and bell peppers. For the green sauce, blend all sauce 
ingredients until smooth. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed. Set aside.
3.	 Heat a pan over medium heat and spray lightly with cooking spray. Cook 
the chicken fillets for 3–4 minutes per side until nicely browned and fully 
cooked. Remove and set aside.
4.	 In the same pan, add the sliced onions and bell peppers. Cook for 3–4 
minutes until slightly softened.
5.	 Add washed, uncooked rice to the pan with the vegetables. Sprinkle in your 
seasonings, then stir and toast for about 2 minutes.
6.	 Pour in water and bring to a light simmer. Cover the pan, reduce heat to low, 
and cook for 8–10 minutes until the rice is fluffy and tender.
7.	 Slice the cooked chicken into strips. Serve over the rice and top with a 
generous drizzle of the green sauce. Enjoy!
```

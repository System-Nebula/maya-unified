INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Peri Peri Chicken Marinade
	
– 400g (14oz) Raw Chicken Breast
	
– 400g (14oz) Raw Chicken Thighs, 
skinless fat trimmed
	
– 20g (1.5 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 2 Tsp Oregano
	
– 2 Tsp Paprika
	
– 2 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Salt
	
– 2 Tsp Pepper
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Nando’s Medium Peri Peri Marinade
	
– 20g (1oz) Reduced Fat Butter (for cooking)
Pasta
	
– 240g Uncooked/540g Cooked Macaroni
Creamy Peri Peri Cheese Sauce
	
– 100g (3.5oz) White Onion, finely chopped
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Tomato Paste
	
– 300g (10.5oz) 1% Milk
	
– 180g (6.4oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Low Fat Cheddar
	
– 1 Tsp each: Salt, Black Pepper, Onion 
Powder, Oregano, Paprika
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Peri Peri Medium Hot Sauce
	
– 70g (1/4 Cup) Pasta Water
	
– Garnish Fresh Parsley, chopped
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Place your chicken breasts and thighs in a large 
dish, then marinate with minced garlic, oregano, 
paprika, onion powder, salt, pepper, and Nando’s 
marinade. Mix well until the color changes and set 
aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2 
While the chicken marinates, bring a pot of water to 
a boil. Add the raw macaroni pasta and boil for 7–8 
minutes until al dente. Reserve some pasta water 
before draining the rest, then set pasta aside.
	
z S T E P 3
Heat a pan over medium heat, add a little oil or 
cooking spray, and cook the marinated chicken for 
5 minutes on each side until golden brown and juicy. 
Repeat in batches if needed.
	
z S T E P 4
Once all the chicken is cooked, set aside to rest, 
then chop into cubes just before serving with the 
mac n cheese.
	
z S T E P 5
In the same pan on medium heat, add chopped 
onions and tomato paste. Cook for about 5 minutes 
until softened. Then lower the heat and add milk, 
cream cheese, grated cheddar, extra seasonings, 
peri-peri hot sauce, and the reserved pasta water. 
Stir thoroughly until everything melts into a smooth, 
creamy cheese sauce.
	
z S T E P 6
Once the sauce is thick and creamy, add the cooked 
macaroni and mix well to coat. Serve evenly with the 
cubed chicken, and your high-protein meal prep is 
ready to ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Marinate the chicken for at least 30 minutes or overnight for the best flavor.
	
z
You can use all chicken breast, all thighs, or a mix — thighs add extra juiciness and flavor.
	
z
Trim any excess fat from the chicken thighs before marinating.
	
z
Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes before cutting into cubes to lock in the juices.
	
z
When making the sauce, keep the heat low to prevent the milk and cheese from splitting.

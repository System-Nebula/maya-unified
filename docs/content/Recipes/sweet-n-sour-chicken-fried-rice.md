z

z
Then Evenly Distribute into 
4 Equal Servings
How to make
1.	 Cut chicken breast into cubes, then season with salt, pepper, paprika, 
garlic, corn flour, and baking powder. Mix until well coated.
2.	 In a pan over medium-low heat, add cooking spray and mixed veg. 
Cook for 1 minute, then add eggs and scramble. Mix with the veg.
3.	 Add cooked rice along with soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. 
Stir well and set the fried rice aside in a bowl or dish.
4.	 In the same pan, cook the chicken for 4–5 minutes on each side until 
golden and crispy. Set aside.
5.	 On low heat, add garlic and ginger. Cook until fragrant, then pour in 
pineapple juice, soy sauce, honey, tomato sauce, and a corn flour 
slurry. Let it thicken.
6.	 Add crispy chicken, onion, bell pepper, and pineapple pieces to the 
sauce. Mix well, serve with the fried rice, and prep your portions for the 
week!
You can cook the fried rice and 
sweet and sour chicken in 2 
separate pans simultaneously.

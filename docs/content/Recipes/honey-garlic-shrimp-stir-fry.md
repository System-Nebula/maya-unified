Sesame Seeds (For Garnish)
How to make
1.	 Season the shrimp with minced garlic, smoked paprika, pepper, chilli flakes, honey, lemon juice, and 
soy sauce. Mix until everything is well combined, then set aside to marinate.
2.	 Thinly slice the onion, bell peppers, and broccoli. Heat a pan on medium heat with a little olive oil, then 
cook the shrimp for about 2 minutes on each side until golden and pink. Remove and set aside.
3.	 In the same pan, add the sliced vegetables and cook for 3–4 minutes until they begin to soften.
4.	 Push the veggies to the side and add a bit more garlic, honey, and soy sauce in the middle. Let it bubble 
and caramelise for a minute, then mix everything together.
5.	 Add cooked basmati rice (about one portion) and stir to combine well. Return the shrimp to the pan, toss 
everything together, sprinkle sesame seeds on top and enjoy!

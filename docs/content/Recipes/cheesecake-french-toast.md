How to make
1.	 In a wide bowl or shallow dish, crack in the whole eggs and egg whites. Add milk, vanilla extract, and a 
dash of cinnamon. Whisk everything together thoroughly until the mixture is smooth and slightly frothy.
2.	 Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, coating both sides evenly without soaking too long. Set the 
soaked slices aside on a plate, ready to cook.
3.	 In a separate bowl, combine softened cream cheese, finely chopped strawberries, and a spoonful of 
strawberry jam. Mix until well blended into a creamy, fruity filling.
4.	 Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly coat with cooking spray or a bit of olive oil. Cook each 
slice of soaked bread for about 2 minutes per side, or until golden brown and slightly crispy.
5.	 To assemble, place one slice of the cooked French toast on a plate, spread a layer of the strawberry 
cream mixture, top with fresh strawberry slices, then place another slice on top. Add more cream, extra 
berries, and a dusting of powdered sugar. Serve warm and enjoy!

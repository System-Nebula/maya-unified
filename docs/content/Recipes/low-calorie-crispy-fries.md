How to make
1.	 Wash the potatoes thoroughly and cut them into thin, even fries. Spread them out on a lined baking tray. 
Season generously with smoked paprika, black pepper, garlic powder, and a dusting of cornstarch. Toss 
well to ensure all the fries are evenly coated.
2.	 Preheat your oven to 220°C (430°F). Once heated, bake the fries for 20–23 minutes or until golden and 
crispy, flipping them halfway through for even browning.
3.	 While the fries are baking, prepare your dipping sauce of choice. Once the fries are out of the oven, sprinkle 
with salt while still hot and serve immediately with the sauce—enjoy!

How to make
1.	 Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil. Add salt, then stir in the pasta. Cook for 8–10 minutes until 
al dente. Before draining, reserve about ½ cup of pasta water. Drain the pasta and set aside.
2.	 In a hot pan, lightly spray with oil and add garlic cloves, sun-dried tomatoes, and chopped onion. Season 
with salt, pepper, and Italian herbs. Sauté for about 5 minutes until the onions are soft and fragrant.
3.	 Transfer the sautéed mixture to a blender. Add the reserved pasta water, cream cheese, and a splash of 
almond or low-fat milk. Blend for 1–2 minutes until smooth and creamy.
4.	 Season salmon fillets with salt and pepper. Heat a pan over medium-high heat with a little oil or spray. 
Cook the salmon for about 6 minutes per side until golden and cooked through. Set aside.
5.	 Lower the heat and return the pasta to the pan. Pour in the sauce and stir to coat evenly. Place the salmon 
on top, cover briefly to warm everything through, then serve immediately.

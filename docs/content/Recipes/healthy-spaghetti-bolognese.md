How to make
1.	 Dice the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic into small pieces and set aside. These will be used as the base 
of the sauce.
2.	 Heat some olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook briefly, then add the onion, 
celery, and carrots. Sauté until soft and lightly golden.
3.	 Move the vegetables to the side and add the ground beef. Break it up and cook until browned. Stir 
in tomato paste, canned tomatoes, and your seasonings like garlic powder, Italian herbs, salt, and 
pepper. Mix well.
4.	 Meanwhile, cook spaghetti in salted boiling water. Before draining, save a bit of the pasta water for later.
5.	 Add the pasta water to the beef mixture and toss in a few basil leaves if you like. Stir everything 
together, cover, and let it simmer on low heat so the flavors can come together.
6.	 Serve the spaghetti topped with the beef sauce. Garnish with grated parmesan and fresh basil, then 
enjoy!

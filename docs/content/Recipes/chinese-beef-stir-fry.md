How to make
1.	 Thinly slice the beef and place it in a bowl with soy sauce, black 
pepper, garlic powder, and a pinch of baking soda. Mix well to coat 
evenly, then set aside to marinate.
2.	 In a separate bowl, prepare the sticky sauce by mixing soy sauce, 
oyster sauce, sesame oil, water, garlic powder, and sesame seeds 
until fully combined.
3.	 Heat a pan over medium heat, add cooking spray or olive oil, and cook 
the marinated beef for about 3 minutes on each side until browned 
and slightly charred. Remove and set aside.
4.	 In the same pan, add sliced bell peppers and cook for 5 minutes until 
soft. Pour in the sauce and let it bubble gently to thicken.
5.	 Return the cooked beef to the pan along with chopped green onion. 
Stir everything together until well coated, garnish with sesame seeds, 
and serve hot with rice. Enjoy!

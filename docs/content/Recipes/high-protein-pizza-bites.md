High Protein Pizza Bites
Guilt-Free low calorie high protein mini pizzas. You can have all 6 and they’re super filling & satisfying!
Macros per serving
93 CALORIES
10g PROTEIN
7.5g  CARBS
2g FAT
Ingredients (6 pizzas)
	
z
6 Gluten Free Rice Cakes 
	
z
150g (5.3oz) Cooked Chicken 
	
z
60g (2.1oz) Tomato Paste
	
z
50-60g (2.1oz) Low Fat 
Mozzarella
(or Any Shredded Cheese)
Toppings:
	
z
1/2 Red Onion, 
Finely Chopped
	
z
1/2 Bell Pepper, 
Finely Chopped
	
z
1/2 Tomato, Chopped
	
z
1 Tsp Italian Herbs
	
z
1 Tsp Salt
Chicken marinade I used 
(simple): 
	
z
1 Tsp Olive Oil
	
z
1 Tsp Salt
	
z
1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
z
1 Tsp Paprika
	
z
1 Tsp Italian Herbs
How to make
1.	 Cut the chicken breast in half and marinate with olive oil, salt, garlic 
powder, and Italian herbs. While it marinates, finely chop the red 
onion, bell pepper, and tomato.
2.	 Cook the chicken over medium-high heat for 4 minutes on each side 
until fully cooked. Remove from the pan and shred using two forks or a 
knife.
3.	 Arrange 6 rice cakes on a baking tray. Spread a thin layer of tomato 
paste on each one, then top with shredded mozzarella, the cooked 
chicken, and chopped veggies.
4.	 Sprinkle extra Italian herbs and a pinch of salt over the top for added 
flavour.
5.	 Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 8 minutes until the cheese 
is melted and bubbling. Serve hot and enjoy immediately!
You can drizzle some olive oil on 
the pizzas after they’re cooked, 
just be mindful of the extra 
calories.
You could easily substitute the 
chicken with turkey slices or any 
meat slices - will just be less 
protein.

Quick note: you can make this with boneless chicken breast. This is easily one of my favorite chicken recipes, it’s

How to make
1.	 Place your whole chicken or boneless chicken breast in a large bowl, add all the marinade ingredients, 
and mix thoroughly until the chicken is fully coated. Let it marinate for as long as possible—the longer it 
rests, the more flavorful it will be.
2.	 Preheat your oven to 220°C (430°F). Prepare a baking tray by spreading a layer of sliced bell peppers and 
onions (or any vegetables of your choice) to act as a base.
3.	 Arrange the marinated chicken pieces over the vegetables and season the top with salt, chili flakes, and 
parsley. Cover the tray tightly with foil.
4.	 Bake in the oven for 20 minutes at 220°C, then remove the foil and return the tray to the oven. Broil for an 
additional 5–7 minutes to develop a golden, slightly charred color on the chicken.
5.	 Once done, baste the chicken with the flavorful juices from the tray and serve with your favorite carbs like 
roasted potatoes or rice. Enjoy!

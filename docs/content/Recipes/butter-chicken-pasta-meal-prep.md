Butter Chicken Pasta Meal Prep
If you love Butter Chicken and Pasta, why not combine the two? This is one of the best 
combos, taste absolutely incredible, perfect meal prep for the week ahead!
Macros per serving
422 CALORIES
 41g PROTEIN
43g  CARBS
10g FAT
Ingredients (4 Servings)
Chicken:
	
z
600g (21.2oz) Chicken Breast, 
cubed 
(You can use Chicken Thighs 
instead for better flavour)
	
z
1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
z
1 Tsp Paprika
	
z
1 Tsp Chilli Powder (optional)
	
z
1 Tsp Turmeric
	
z
1 Tsp Garam Masala
	
z
1/2 Lemon Juice 
	
z
100g (3.5oz) Fat Free Yogurt
	
z
1-2 tsp Olive Oil or Cooking 
Spray for Cooking
Butter Chicken Sauce:
	
z
20g (1oz) Light Butter 
	
z
1 Medium Onion Sliced
	
z
2 Large Tomatoes Chopped or 
1 Canned Tomatoes (400g)
	
z
20g Cashew Nuts
	
z
100g Fat Free Yogurt 
	
z
1/4 or 1/2 cup pasta water
Pasta:
	
z
200g (7.1oz) Uncooked 
Penne Pasta
How to make
1.	 Cut the chicken into cubes and marinate with spices, lemon juice, and yogurt. Mix well and let it sit for at 
least 30 minutes. 
2.	 While the chicken marinates, slice the onion. In a pan over medium heat, melt a bit of light butter, then 
cook the onions for 3 minutes. Add chopped tomatoes, cashews, and extra seasoning. Cook for 5 minutes, 
then stir in yogurt.
3.	 Let the mixture cool slightly, then blend until smooth and creamy. Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to 
package instructions, then drain and set aside.
4.	 In the same pan, cook the marinated chicken for about 4 minutes on each side until golden and fully cooked.
5.	 Pour the blended sauce into the pan with the chicken, then add the cooked pasta. Mix well until everything 
is fully coated and heated through.
6.	 Garnish with fresh coriander, divide into 4 portions, and enjoy!

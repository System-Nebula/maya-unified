How to make
1.	 Place a non-stick pan over medium heat and add the turkey bacon. Cook until crispy, turning occasionally 
to ensure even browning.
2.	 Once the bacon is cooked, pour the beaten eggs directly over it in the pan, spreading evenly.
3.	 Reduce the heat to low and immediately place a tortilla wrap on top of the eggs while they’re still wet. 
Gently press the wrap down so it sticks. Let the eggs cook through completely, then carefully flip the entire 
wrap using a wide spatula. Toast the underside for 1–2 minutes until lightly golden.
4.	 With the egg side facing out, layer on fresh spinach, tomato slices, a pinch of salt and pepper, sliced 
avocado, and a drizzle of spicy mayo. Fold the wrap over, remove from the pan, and serve warm.

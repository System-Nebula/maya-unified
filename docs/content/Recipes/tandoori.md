– 1.5 Tsp Cumin

– 4 Tsp Olive Oil (20ml) 
	
– 80g (2.8oz / ~3 Tbsp) Fat Free Yogurt 
	
– Juice of 3/4 Lemon
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Trim any excess fat from the boneless chicken 
thighs, then place them in a large mixing bowl. 
Season with tandoori masala, black pepper, 
smoked paprika, garlic powder, cumin, and 
coriander powder, then add olive oil, yogurt, and a 
squeeze of lemon juice. Mix well until fully combined 
and the colour deepens. Set aside to marinate - the 
longer it rests, the more intense the flavour will be.
	
z S T E P 2 
Line a sheet pan with parchment paper. Spread the 
marinated chicken thighs in a single layer »»
	» Continue
and sprinkle a little extra tandoori seasoning on top. 
Oven bake or air fry at 200°C / 400°F for 20-25 
minutes, until cooked through, golden and crispy on 
the edges. Once the chicken is cooked, set it aside to 
rest.
	
z S T E P 3
For best meal prep results, keep the chicken whole 
when storing to retain juiciness. Make sure to include 
any resting juices from the sheet pan with the 
chicken for extra flavour and moisture. Slice into cubes 
only when serving for maximum tenderness. ENJOY!
BEST WAYS TO SERVE AND PAIR THIS PROTEIN 
This Tandoori Chicken works extremely well in simple, flavourful combinations, for example: 
	
–with lemon herb rice 
	
–with roasted potatoes or air fried chips/fries and a simple creamy coleslaw or salad 
	
–in a warm wrap with lettuce and onions
SAUCE PAIRING SUGGESTIONS 
This chicken also pairs well with:
	
–mint yogurt sauce 
	
–garlic yogurt sauce 
	
–mango chutney for sweetness

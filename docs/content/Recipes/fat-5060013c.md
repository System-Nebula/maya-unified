fragrant garlic herb rice.

INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Garlic Herb Chicken
	
– 900g (32oz) Raw Chicken 
Thighs, Boneless Skinless
	
– 20g (1.5 Tbsp) Garlic Paste or Minced Garlic
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 1.5 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Mixed Herbs (Oregano, Basil, Thyme)
	
– 1.5 Tsp Parsley
	
– 1.5 Tsp Paprika
	
– 1 Tsp Chilli Flakes (omit for milder flavour)
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Reduced Fat Butter (for cooking)
Fragrant & Fluffy Rice
	
– 20g (1.5 Tbsp) Garlic Cloves, chopped
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Red Onion, chopped
	
– 75g (2.7oz) Red Bell Pepper, chopped 
	
– 75g (2.7oz) Green Bell Pepper, chopped
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 1 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Mixed Herbs
	
– 1.5 Tsp Parsley
	
– 2 Tsp Paprika
	
– 210g Uncooked Basmati Rice/Approx 560g Cooked
	
– 1 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, chopped (Optional)
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Place the chicken thighs in a mixing bowl. Add garlic 
paste, salt, black pepper, mixed herbs, parsley, 
paprika, chili flakes, and olive oil. Mix thoroughly until 
well coated, then set aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2 
While the chicken marinates, cook the rice by boiling 
it in a pot of water for 8–10 minutes. Once done, 
drain any excess water and set aside. While the rice 
cooks, chop the garlic, red onion, and bell peppers.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan over medium heat, melt butter, and cook 
the chicken thighs for 5–6 minutes per side until a 
deep golden crust forms. Once cooked, set aside to 
rest. Repeat for the rest of the chicken.
	
z S T E P 4
In the same pan over medium-low heat, sauté 
the chopped garlic, onion, and bell peppers for 
5–6 minutes until soft and fragrant. Stir in extra 
seasoning (salt, pepper, mixed herbs, parsley, 
paprika) till the the onions and peppers catch 
colour
	
z S T E P 5
Add the cooked basmati rice and mix well until 
every grain is coated in the spices and takes on a 
vibrant orange color.
	
z S T E P 6
Garnish the rice with freshly chopped parsley. Slice 
the chicken thighs into cubes, serve with the rice, 
and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Marinate the chicken for at least 30 minutes or overnight for the best flavor.
	
z
Cook the chicken thighs over medium heat with a little butter or cooking spray for 5–6 minutes per side 
until a deep golden crust forms.
	
z
Mix the cooked rice thoroughly to ensure every grain is coated in seasoning and vibrant in color. Add extra 
paprika and salt if needed.
	
z
Slice the chicken thighs into cubes and serve over the rice. Finish with a squeeze of lemon juice or pair with 
a sauce of your choice.

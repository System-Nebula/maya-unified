Beefy Pasta
Simple, easy & tastes absolutely banging!
Macros per serving
550 CALORIES
39g PROTEIN
50g  CARBS
13g FAT
Ingredients (2 servings)
Pasta:

200g (7.1oz) Ground/Mince Beef (5% Fat) 
	
z
1.5 to 2 Cups Wholewheat Pasta
	
z
1 Tbsp Olive Oil
	
z
4 Cloves of Garlic
	
z
1 Red Bell Pepper
	
z
1 Tomato, chopped
	
z
1-2 Tsp Salt & Pepper
	
z
1 Tbsp Italian Herbs
	
z
1 Tsp Red Chilli Flakes
	
z
1-2 Tbsp Low/Zero Fat Cream Cheese
Topping
	
z
Parmesan Cheese
How to make
1.	 Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook until tender. Reserve some pasta water 
before draining, then set the pasta aside.
2.	 In a pan over medium heat, add olive oil and whole garlic cloves. Cook until fragrant and lightly 
golden. Add chopped bell pepper and tomato, then season with salt, pepper, Italian herbs, and chili 
flakes. Sauté until softened.
3.	 Transfer the cooked vegetables and garlic to a blender. Add the reserved pasta water and cream 
cheese. Blend until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
4.	 In the same pan, cook the ground beef over medium heat. Season with salt, pepper, Italian herbs, 
and paprika. Cook for about 5 minutes or until browned.
5.	 Add the cooked pasta and creamy sauce to the pan with the beef. Stir everything together and let it 
simmer for a couple of minutes. Finish with a sprinkle of parmesan cheese and serve warm.

– 150g (5.3oz) Carrots, grated

– 600g (21oz) Canned Chopped Tomatoes

Crispy Potatoes
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Potatoes, cubed
	
– 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1 Tsp Chilli Flakes
	
– 1 Tsp Paprika
	
– 1 Tsp Oregano
	
– 1 Tsp Dried Basil
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Wash the potatoes and cut them into small cubes. 
Finely chop the onions, celery, and garlic, and grate 
2 medium carrots.
	
z S T E P 2 
Place the potato cubes on a lined baking tray. 
Season with garlic powder, chilli flakes, paprika, 
oregano, basil, and a drizzle of olive oil. Toss until 
well coated.
	
z S T E P 3 
Air fry or oven-bake the potatoes at 200°C (400°F) 
for 20 minutes. Meanwhile, heat a pan over medium 
heat, add light butter, and sauté the chopped 
onions, celery, garlic, and grated carrots for 5–6 
minutes until soft.
	
z S T E P 4
Season the vegetable mix with salt and pepper, 
cook for another 2 minutes, then remove from the 
pan and set aside.
	
z S T E P 5
In the same pan, add a little extra light butter 
and increase the heat to medium-high. Cook the 
ground beef for 5–7 minutes, breaking it up with a 
spatula until slightly charred and fully cooked.
	
z S T E P 6 
Stir in tomato paste, salt, basil, oregano, chilli flakes, 
and paprika, then add ½ cans of chopped tomatoes. 
Let the sauce simmer on low for 5 minutes, then stir 
in the reserved vegetable mix.
	
z S T E P 7
Mix thoroughly and add grated Parmesan. Cover 
the pan and simmer for 8–10 minutes to meld the 
flavors. Adjust seasoning and add a splash of 
water if needed.
	
z S T E P 8 
Finally, add the crispy potatoes to the pan and 
gently fold them in until evenly combined. Top with 
mozzarella cheese and fresh parsley, cover until 
the cheese melts, then serve and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z

z

FAT
Tastes absolutely phenomenal, extremely easy to make with 
minimal ingredients. This is the best meal prep i’ve made in a 
while!

INGREDIENTS (5 SERVINGS)
Chicken & Seasoning
	
– 1000g (35oz) Raw Chicken Breast, cut into cubes 
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt 
	
– 2 Tsp Parsley 
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder 
	
– 2 Tsp Onion Powder 
	
– 2 Tsp Smoked Paprika 
	
– 1.5 Tsp Cumin 
	
– 3 Tsp Olive Oil 
	
– Juice of 1/2 or 1 Lime 
	
– 25g (1oz) Light Butter (for cooking all chicken) 
	
– 2 Tsp Minced Garlic 
	
– 2 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, finely chopped
Street Corn Salsa
	
– 400g (14.1oz) Canned Sweetcorn, drained 
	
– 60g (2.1oz) Light Mayo 
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Fat Free Greek Yogurt 
	
– 50g (1.7oz) Cholula Hot Sauce, 
or hot sauce of choice 
	
– 50g (1.7oz) Jalapeños, de seeded 
and finely chopped 
	
– 2 Tbsp Fresh Coriander, finely chopped 
	
– 2 Tsp Tajin Seasoning 
	
– Juice of 1/2 Lime 
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Crumbled Feta Cheese
Coriander/Cilantro Rice
	
– 190g (6.7oz) Uncooked Basmati Rice 
	
– 3 Tbsp Fresh Coriander, finely chopped 
	
– Optional Squeeze of Lime, i didn’t add it as the 
other components already have lime juice
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1
Slice the chicken breasts into cubes and place 
them in a mixing bowl. Season with salt, garlic 
powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, cumin, 
parsley, olive oil, and a good squeeze of lime juice. 
Toss until evenly coated and set aside to marinate 
while you prep the rice.
	
z S T E P 2
In a pot, add water using a 1:2 rice-to-water ratio. 
Add the washed uncooked rice, bring to a light 
boil, then reduce to a simmer for 10–12 minutes or 
until almost fully cooked. Once slightly underdone, 
remove from heat, drain any excess water, and cover 
with a lid to let the rice steam and ﬂuff up. Stir in 
a big handful of chopped fresh coriander and a 
pinch of salt. Mix gently to combine.
	
z S T E P 3
Heat a pan over medium heat and melt some 
butter. Add the marinated chicken and cook for 
4–5 minutes per side until golden and seared. Lower 
the heat, then stir in extra butter, minced garlic, 
and chopped fresh parsley. Toss the chicken to 
coat it in the buttery, fragrant glaze.
	
z S T E P 4
In a large bowl, combine sweetcorn, light mayo, Greek 
yogurt, chopped and deseeded jalapeños, fresh 
coriander, Cholula hot sauce, Tajín, lime juice, and 
crumbled feta. Mix until creamy and well combined. 
Store in a separate container for best freshness.
	
z S T E P 5
Divide the chicken, coriander rice, and creamy 
corn salad into 5 equal portions. Your high-protein 
meals are now ready to fuel your week!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Let the chicken marinate for at least 20 mins for better ﬂavour.
	
z
Cook chicken on medium heat for 6-8 mins till golden. Lower the heat when adding minced garlic and parsley.
	
z
Store the corn salsa in a separate container to make reheating easier.

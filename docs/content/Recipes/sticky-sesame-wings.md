How to make
1.	 Wash the chicken wings and pat them completely dry using tissue paper to help them crisp up in the oven.
2.	 Place the wings in a large oven dish and season with paprika, oregano, chilli flakes, chopped garlic, lime 
juice, light brown sugar, sesame seeds, and light soy sauce. Mix well until all wings are evenly coated.
3.	 Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F). Bake the wings for 30 minutes, or air fry at the same temperature for 
15–18 minutes until golden and cooked through.
4.	 Once cooked, remove from the oven or air fryer and transfer to a serving plate.
5.	 Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and serve hot.

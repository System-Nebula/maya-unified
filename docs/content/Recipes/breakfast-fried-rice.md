Breakfast Fried Rice
Switch up your breakfast with this easy

 120-150g (5.3oz) Leftover Cooked Rice

z

z

z

z
Oil or Cooking Spray
Note:
Calories and macros will vary based on the ingredients 
you use. You can reduce calories and fat by using just 
egg whites! (350 calories & 6g fat with 5 egg whites).
How to make
1.	 Thinly dice the onion and bell pepper. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat, spray with oil, then add the 
veggies and turkey bacon. Cover and cook until the bacon is crispy.
2.	 Remove the bacon, cut it into small pieces, and set aside. Push the veggies to one side of the pan.
3.	 Pour in the eggs and stir continuously over low heat until softly scrambled.
4.	 Mix the scrambled eggs with the veggies, then add cooked rice and the chopped bacon back into the pan.
5.	 Season with salt, chili flakes, and parsley. Cook everything together for 2–3 minutes on low-medium heat, 
garnish with spring onion, and enjoy!

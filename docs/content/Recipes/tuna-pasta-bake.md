How to make
1.	 Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. While it’s cooking, dice the onion and bell pepper, 
and finely chop the garlic.
2.	 In a pan, heat a bit of olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, bell pepper, and chopped 
tomatoes. Season with salt, pepper, Italian herbs, and chilli flakes. 
3.	 Stir and cook for about 5 minutes until the vegetables soften. Stir in the tuna and the cooked pasta. Mix 
everything well, then transfer to a baking dish.
4.	 Top with shredded mozzarella and bake in a preheated oven at 180ºC for 10 minutes. Remove from the 
oven and serve hot—enjoy!

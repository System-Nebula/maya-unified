Honey Lemon Chicken Meal Prep
There’s no reason to eat plain chicken & rice when you can make this incredibly 
easy and delicious recipe. One of my favourite meal preps!
Macros per serving
415 CALORIES
 40g PROTEIN
44g  CARBS
8g FAT
Ingredients (4 Servings)
Chicken:
	
z
700g (24.7oz) Cubed Raw Chicken Breast
	
z
20ml (1oz) Light & Dark Soy Sauce 
(Low Sodium)
	
z
1 Tsp Paprika
	
z
1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
z
1 Lemon, Juiced
	
z
2 Tsp Olive Oil For Cooking
	
z
10-15g (0.5oz) Honey
Rice:
	
z
480g (17oz) Cooked Basmati Rice 
(120g (4.1oz) Per Serve) 
	
z
1 Red Onion, Finely Sliced
	
z
1 Red Bell Pepper, Chopped
	
z
15ml (0.5oz) Light Soy Sauce
	
z
10g (0.3oz) Honey
	
z
1/2 Lemon Juice
	
z
Garnish Green Onion
	
z
Distribute Into 4 Equal Servings
How to make
1.	 Cut the chicken into cubes and season with soy sauce, paprika, 
garlic powder, and lemon juice. Mix until the color changes. (Optional: 
marinate for 30 minutes.)
2.	 Heat a pan on medium with olive oil. Cook the chicken for 3–4 minutes 
on each side until golden. Turn off the heat, drizzle with honey, and toss 
to coat. Set aside.
3.	 In the same pan, add chopped onion and bell pepper. Cook for about 
4 minutes until soft.
4.	 Add cooked rice to the pan, followed by more soy sauce, honey, and 
lemon juice. Mix well.
5.	 Return the chicken to the pan, combine everything, garnish with green 
onion, and portion into 4 servings. Enjoy!
When adding the honey to the 
chicken make sure the heat is 
turned off.
You can store the meal preps 
for up to 5 days and freeze for 
months. Reheat in a microwave 
or on a pan.
You can marinate the chicken 
for 30 mins or overnight for best 
flavour.

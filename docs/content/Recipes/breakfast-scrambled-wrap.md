```
Breakfast Scrambled Wrap
One of my go-to breakfasts so easy to make - nutritious, tastes delicious and fills you up
Macros per serving
450-500 CALORIES
 25g PROTEIN
35g  CARBS
18g FAT
Ingredients (1 wrap)
	
z
2-3 Eggs 
	
z
1 Low Calorie Tortilla Wrap  
	
z
1 Tsp Olive Oil
	
z
1/2 Onion, Chopped
	
z
1/2 Bell Pepper, Chopped
	
z
Handful Spinach, Chopped 1 Tsp Salt & Pepper
	
z
1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
z
1 Tsp Paprika
	
z
15g (0.5oz) Shredded Mozzarella
	
z
15g (0.5oz) Sriracha
	
z
1/2 Avocado, Sliced
How to make
1.	 Dice the onion, bell pepper, and spinach. Heat olive oil in a pan over 
medium heat, then add the onion and bell pepper. Season with salt, 
pepper, garlic powder, and paprika, and cook for 2 minutes.
2.	 Add the spinach, stir to combine, then push all the veggies to one side 
of the pan.
3.	 Pour the eggs into the empty side and stir continuously until cooked 
through. Mix the eggs with the veggies.
4.	 Place a low-calorie tortilla on a plate, add the egg and veggie mixture 
in the center, top with mozzarella, sriracha, and sliced avocado. Roll it 
up and enjoy!
You can reduce fat by using just 
egg whites.
```

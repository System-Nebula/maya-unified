INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breasts into thin strips, then 
marinate with minced garlic, tomato paste, salt, 
pepper, onion powder, cinnamon, paprika, olive oil, 
and lemon juice. Mix thoroughly and let it marinate.
	
z S T E P 2 
For the golden rice, bring a pot of water to a boil 
and add raw basmati rice with salt, turmeric, and 
paprika. Stir and cook for 8–10 minutes until fully 
cooked and fluffy.
	
z S T E P 3
Lay the marinated chicken flat on a lined sheet 
pan, ensuring an even layer, then oven bake or air 
fry at 190°C (375°F) for 12–15 minutes until golden 
brown and slightly charred.

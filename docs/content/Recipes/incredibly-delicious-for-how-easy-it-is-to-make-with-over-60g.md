Incredibly delicious for how easy it is to make with over 60g 
of Protein! A popular dish also known as Yalanci Manti. Juicy 
ground beef serve with pasta of choice topped with mint 
garlic sauce and chilli butter

INGREDIENTS (5 SERVINGS)
Juicy Ground Beef
	
– 1000g (35oz) 5% Fat Raw Ground Beef 
	
– Avocado Oil Cooking Spray (or any of choice) 
	
– 250g (8.8oz) White Onion, chopped 
	
– 1 Tsp Salt 
	
– 1 Tsp Black Pepper 
	
– 1.5 Tsp Paprika 
	
– 1 Tsp Cumin 
	
– 20g (1oz) Minced Garlic 
	
– 60g (2.1oz) Tomato Paste
Extra Seasoning
	
– 1/2 Tsp Salt & Pepper 
	
– 1 Tsp Paprika 
	
– 1 Tsp Cumin 
	
– Add extra tomato paste if needed 
	
– 260g (9.1oz) Uncooked Pasta of choice
Garlic Mint Sauce
	
– 400-500g (17.6oz) Skyr Fat Free Yogurt 
	
– 1.5 Tsp Minced Garlic 
	
– 1 Tsp Dried Mint 
	
– Pinch of Salt 
	
– 1/2 Lemon Juice
Chilli Butter Sauce
	
– 40-50g (1.7oz) Light Butter 
	
– 1/2 Tsp Paprika 
	
– Pinch of Salt (not too much!) 
	
– 1/2 Tsp Chilli Flakes (to your preference) 
	
– 15g (0.5oz) Tomato Paste 
	
– Pasta Water for extra volume
Garnish
	
– Cherry Tomatoes, chopped 
	
– Fresh Mint Leaves, chopped 
	
– Chilli Garlic seasoning (optional)
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
In a large bowl, combine a generous amount of thick 
yogurt (I used Skyr for its high protein content), 
minced garlic, dried or fresh mint, a pinch of salt, 
and a good squeeze of lemon juice. Stir until smooth 
and creamy. Transfer to a separate container and 
refrigerate to keep it fresh for the week. 
	
z S T E P 2 
Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add your pasta of choice 
(I used a mix of conchiglie and fusilli) and cook for 
about 8 minutes or until al dente. Reserve some of the 
pasta water, drain the rest, and set the pasta aside. 
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan over medium heat and add avocado oil 
spray. Sauté chopped onions for 8–10 minutes until soft 
and golden brown. Then add salt, black pepper, paprika, 
cumin, minced garlic, and tomato paste. Cook for 
another 3-4 minutes until fragrant and well combined. 
	
z S T E P 4 
Increase the heat to high, add the lean ground beef 
to the pan, and use a spatula to break it up. Cook 
for about 8 minutes until browned and fully cooked 
through. Season again to taste with salt, pepper, and 
spices. Mix well until juicy and flavourful, then set aside. 
	
z S T E P 5 
In a small pan over low heat, melt a little light butter. Add 
paprika, chilli flakes, tomato paste, a pinch of salt, and 
a splash of the reserved pasta water. Stir until smooth 
and let it simmer gently until rich and glossy.
	
z STEP 6
Divide the pasta and juicy beef into 5 equal portions. 
Top each with a few spoonfuls of the garlic mint 
yogurt and a drizzle of the chilli butter sauce. 
Garnish with chopped cherry tomatoes, fresh mint and 
chilli garlic seasoning. Your delicious high protein meals 
are ready to ENJOY!

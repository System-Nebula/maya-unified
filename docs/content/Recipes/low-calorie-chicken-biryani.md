Low Calorie Chicken Biryani
This has to be one of the tastiest meals I’ve made, the flavours are just incredible and it’s packed with protein 
and low calorie. A perfect example that you can still enjoy the foods you love and lose weight!
Macros per serving
476 CALORIES
 55g PROTEIN
44g  CARBS
9g FAT
Ingredients (2 Servings)
Chicken marinade:
	
z
300g (10.6oz) Raw Chicken Breast, cubed
	
z
120g (4.2oz) Low Fat Yogurt (Greek Yogurt Works) 
	
z
4 Garlic Cloves, Minced
	
z
1 Tbsp Ginger, Crushed
	
z
1/2 Tsp Chilli Powder or 
1 Tbsp Paprika (If You Don’t Like Spicy)
	
z
1 Tsp Garam Masala
	
z
1/2-1 Tsp Turmeric
	
z
1 Tsp Salt & Pepper
	
z
1/2 Lemon Juice
For cooking:
	
z
2 Tsp Olive Oil
	
z
2 Medium Onions, Thinly Sliced
	
z
1/2 Tsp Cinnamon
	
z
1 Tsp Cumin
	
z
Add the Chicken
	
z
1 Medium Tomato, Chopped
	
z
280g (9.8oz) Cooked Basmati Rice
(Make Sure It’s Fluffy Rice and Not Overcooked) 
	
z
Garnish with Coriander/Cilantro
	
z
Saffron Water (Saffron Mixed in Boiling Water)
	
z
Garnish with Crispy Onion (Optional)
How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken into bite-sized cubes and add all the marinade ingredients to a bowl. Mix thoroughly 
until the chicken is fully coated, then cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to let the flavours absorb.
2.	 Heat a pan on medium-high heat with olive oil, add two sliced onions and cook for 8–10 minutes until deeply 
browned and caramelised. Stir occasionally and cover the pan to help soften and brown the onions evenly.
3.	 Once the onions are golden, add cinnamon and cumin. Toast for 30 seconds, then add the marinated 
chicken and one chopped tomato. Cook for about 8 minutes, stirring once halfway through. Crush the 
tomato gently with a spatula to help it release its juices into the sauce.
4.	 In a separate pot, bring salted water to a boil and cook the rice until it’s 80% done (slightly firm inside). 
Drain the water, turn off the heat, and cover the pot to let the rice finish cooking in its own steam.
5.	 Spoon the cooked rice over the chicken mixture in the pan. Garnish with fresh coriander, saffron water, 
crispy onions, and optional green chillies. Gently fold the mixture to combine, being careful not to break 
the rice, then serve and enjoy.

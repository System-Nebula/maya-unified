INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Crispy Sweet Potatoes
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Sweet Potatoes, cubed small
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 1 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 1 Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
Special Sauce
	
– 100g (3.5oz) 50% Fat Mayo
	
– 80g (2.8OZ) Reduced Sugar Ketchup
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Yellow Mustard
	
– 1 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– ½ Tsp Black Pepper »»
	» Continued
	
– ½ Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– 15-20g (1oz) Pickle Juice 
Cheeseburger Beef
	
– 800g (28oz) 95% Ground/Minced Beef
	
– 2 Tsp Salt
	
– 2 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Reduced Fat Butter (for cooking)
	
– 80g (2.8oz) Light Cheddar Cheese (or any cheese)
Pickle Salad
	
– 1 Whole Iceberg Lettuce, thinly sliced
	
– 200g (7oz) Red Onion, chopped
	
– 200 (7oz) Tomatoes, chopped
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Pickled Gherkins, chopped
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Wash and slice the sweet potatoes into small 
cubes. Arrange them on a tray and season with 
salt, black pepper, garlic & onion powder, smoked 
paprika, and olive oil.
	
z S T E P 2 
Toss the potatoes until evenly coated, then bake 
or air fry at 200°C (400°F) for 25–30 minutes until 
golden crispy.
	
z S T E P 3 
Meanwhile, prepare the special sauce. In a bowl, 
combine light mayo, ketchup, yellow mustard, 
onion powder, black pepper, smoked paprika, and 
pickle juice. Mix until smooth. (You can bottle and 
refrigerate the sauce for later use)
	
z S T E P 4
For the pickle salad, shred the lettuce and combine 
it in a bowl with chopped red onion, tomatoes, and 
pickles. Toss well and set aside.
	
z S T E P 5
Heat a pan over medium-high heat and add butter. 
Cook the ground beef for 6–8 minutes until a deep, 
brown crust forms, stirring with a spatula to break up 
the meat. Season with salt and pepper.
	
z S T E P 6
Once the beef is cooked, add a little more butter to 
the pan and mix it through. Serve the beef (topped 
with a sprinkle of cheese, if desired) alongside the 
crispy sweet potatoes, fresh salad, and a generous 
drizzle of the special sauce. ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
While the potatoes are cooking, prepare both the special sauce and salad to save time.
	
z
Be sure to include the pickle juice in the sauce—it adds a fantastic flavor. Bottle the sauce and store it in 
the fridge.
	
z
For the salad, store the lettuce separately from the other vegetables to prevent sogginess; combine them 
just before serving.
	
z
Reheat the beef and potatoes together on low heat in a pan or in an air fryer for 5 minutes.

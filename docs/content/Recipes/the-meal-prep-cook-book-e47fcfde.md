Onion Mix
	
– 250g (8.8oz) Red Onion, thinly sliced

– 1.5 Tbsp Sumac
	
– Juice of 1 Lemons
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 : M A R I N AT E T H E B E E F
Thinly slice the sirloin beef into extra thin strips. 
Place in a large mixing bowl and add 2.5 servings 
of shawarma spice mix, olive oil, and lemon juice. 
Mix thoroughly until fully coated and set aside to 
marinate.
	
z S T E P 2 : P R E PA R E T H E O N I O N M I X
In a separate bowl, combine the thinly sliced red 
onion, chopped parsley, sumac, and lemon juice. Mix 
well and set aside. This will be used as a fresh topping. 
	
z STEP 3: COOK THE BEEF
Heat a pan on high heat and melt some of the 
butter. Sear the marinated beef for 1-2 minutes to 
get a quick char. Reduce heat to medium, add the 
remaining butter and parsley, and sauté for another 
1-2 minutes until evenly coated and golden brown. 
Remove the beef from the pan and set aside. Repeat 
until all beef is cooked.

STORAGE, FREEZING & REHEATING GUIDE
(For All Chicken & Ground Beef Meal Prep Recipes)
This master guide applies to every protein recipe in this section - including chicken thighs, chicken 
breast, and ground/minced beef.
The goal: keep your proteins juicy, tender, and flavourful all week.
HOW TO KEEP PROTEINS JUICY ALL WEEK
	
–Do not slice chicken until serving.
	
–Always include leftover juices/glaze with chicken.
	
–Store in shallow containers for faster cooling.
	
–Avoid overcooking - reheating always reduces moisture slightly.
	
–Air fryer is the best tool to revive texture.
	
–Only reheat the portion you plan to eat - avoid reheating multiple times.
STORAGE - FRIDGE (4-5 DAYS)
Chicken (Whole Thighs or Cubed Thigh Meat)
	
–Allow chicken to cool completely before storing.
	
–Store whole thighs whenever possible - this preserves 
juiciness better than sliced or cubed chicken.
	
–Add sheet pan juices, glaze, or cooking sauce to the container for moisture and flavour (do not 
over-saturate).
	
–Store in airtight glass containers for maximum freshness.
Ground / Minced Beef
	
–Allow beef to cool fully before storing.
	
–Store in shallow airtight containers to prevent moisture buildup.
	
–Store all sauces separately.
	
–Seasoned or tomato-based beef will continue to develop flavour over 24 hours - this is normal 
and enhances taste.
FREEZING (UP TO 2-3 MONTHS)
Chicken
	
–Freeze cooked chicken whole for best texture.
	
–Add a small amount of glaze/sauce to prevent drying (avoid flooding the container).
	
–Freeze in airtight containers or freezer bags.
	
–Defrost overnight in the fridge before reheating.
Ground / Minced Beef
	
–Freeze in flat portions (bags or thin containers) for faster thawing.
	
–Add just enough pan juices to prevent dryness.
	
–Do not freeze yogurt based, mayo-based, or citrus-heavy sauces - store those fresh only.

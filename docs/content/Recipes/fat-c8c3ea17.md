INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Chicken Shawarma
	
– 900g (32oz) Raw Chicken Breast,
butterflied halves
	
– 30g (2 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 2.5 Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– 2.5 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 1 Tsp Cinnamon
	
– 1 Tsp Cumin
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 1.5 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
	
– 60g (2.1oz) Tomato Paste
	
– Juice of 1 Lemon
Crispy Fries
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Potatoes, cut into fries »»
	» Continue
	
– 1 Tsp each: Salt, Black Pepper, Oregano, Paprika
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
Garlic Sauce
	
– 200g Low Fat Greek Yogurt
	
– 75g Light Mayonnaise
	
– 1-2 minced Garlic Cloves
	
– ½ tsp salt, pepper
	
– ½ Lemon Juice
Crunchy Slaw
	
– Thinly cut Cabbage
	
– Grated Carrots
	
– Thinly sliced Red Onion
	
– Lemon Juice and Salt
	
– Pickles
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Start by slicing the chicken breasts into butterflied 
halves. Place in a bowl and marinate with fresh 
garlic, smoked paprika, onion powder, cinnamon, 
cumin, salt, pepper, olive oil, tomato paste, and 
lemon juice. Mix thoroughly until every piece is well 
coated, then set aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2 
While the chicken is marinating, slice the potatoes 
into fries and lay them flat on a lined baking tray. 
Season with salt, pepper, oregano, paprika, and 
olive oil, then toss to coat evenly. Bake or air fry at 
200°C (400°F) for 20–25 minutes until golden and 
crispy, flipping halfway if needed.
	
z S T E P 3
Take a loaf tin and layer the marinated chicken 
slices tightly, stacking them to fill the tin completely. 
Bake for 25 minutes at 200°C (400°F) until cooked 
through and juicy with a golden top.
	
z S T E P 4
Once the chicken is done, let it rest for 5 minutes. 
Meanwhile, prepare the garlic sauce and crunchy 
slaw using the ingredients listed (optional: make 
these ahead for quicker assembly).
	
z S T E P 5
After resting, thinly slice the stacked chicken to 
reveal shawarma-style strips. Serve alongside the 
crispy fries, pickles, slaw, and drizzle generously 
with garlic sauce. Enjoy!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Marinate the chicken as long as possible — overnight for best flavor.
	
z
When layering the chicken, stack tightly to avoid pieces falling to the sides. You can also use a regular 
baking dish if you don’t have a loaf tin.
	
z
Bake the fries for 25 minutes or air fry for 15 minutes at 200°C / 400°F.
	
z
Cook the chicken and fries together in the oven for 25 minutes to save time.
	
z
Once the chicken is cooked and slightly cooled, slice thinly using a sharp knife and serve.

How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken breasts into medium-sized cubes, then marinate 
with the listed spices, lemon juice, and yogurt. Mix well and let it sit 
for at least 30 minutes (overnight for best flavor).
2.	 Skewer the marinated chicken pieces along with your choice of 
vegetables—red onion and red/green bell peppers work great.
3.	 To make the mint sauce, blend yogurt, fresh mint leaves, garlic cloves, 
salt, pepper, and lemon juice until smooth and creamy.
4.	 Heat a pan over medium-high heat with olive oil, then cook the skewers 
for about 5 minutes on each side until golden brown. Set aside.
5.	 In the same pan, add chopped onions and bell peppers. Cook for 
1 minute, then add turmeric, cumin, paprika, salt, and uncooked 
basmati rice. Stir and toast for 2–3 minutes.
6.	 Add water, bring to a light bubble, then cover and simmer for 8 minutes 
until the rice is fully cooked. Serve with the chicken skewers and a 
generous spoonful of mint sauce. Enjoy!
You can marinate the chicken 
for 30 mins or overnight for best 
flavour.
You don’t have to use skewers 
for this, you can cook directly on 
the pan. 
You can oven or air fry these for 
20-25 mins at 210°C.
You can make the rice in a normal 
pot if preferred.

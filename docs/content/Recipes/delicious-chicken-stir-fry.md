Delicious Chicken Stir Fry
Chicken & Rice that actually tastes good! A perfect high volume meal and one of my favourite post 
workout meals. So quick and easy to make in 20 mins, this is also great for meal prep
Macros per serving
418 CALORIES
 56g PROTEIN
38g  CARBS
7g FAT
Ingredients (2 servings)
	
z
300g (10.6oz) Raw Chicken Breast, cut into cubes
	
z
1 Heaping Tsp Paprika
	
z
1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
z
1 Tsp Black Pepper
	
z
1.5 Tbsp Soy Sauce
(I Used Dark But You Can Use Light)
Stir fry:
	
z
2 Tsp Olive Oil
	
z
1 Medium Onion, Finely Chopped
	
z
1 Medium Red Bell Pepper, Chopped
	
z
1 Tsp Each of Paprika, Garlic Powder 
& Pepper (For Seasoning)
	
z
1 Tbsp Soy Sauce
	
z
1 Cup Broccoli
	
z
200g (7.1oz) Cooked Basmati Rice
	
z
Sprinkle Sesame Seeds (Optional)
How to make
1.	 Season the chicken with paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, and soy sauce. Mix well until the chicken 
is evenly coated and its color changes slightly.
2.	 Chop all the vegetables, then heat a pan over medium heat and cook the chicken for about 6 minutes until 
fully cooked. Remove from the pan and set aside.
3.	 In the same pan, add the chopped onions and bell peppers. Season with the same spices and a splash 
of soy sauce, then sauté for 2–3 minutes until slightly softened.
4.	 Add broccoli and cooked rice to the pan. Stir everything together until well combined and heated through, 
making sure the flavors are evenly distributed.
5.	 Garnish with sesame seeds and serve hot. Enjoy!

How to make
1.	 Add the shrimp to a mixing bowl and season with salt, garlic powder, paprika, chilli flakes, and freshly 
squeezed lemon juice. Toss thoroughly so the seasoning coats each piece evenly. Set aside to briefly 
marinate while you prep the rest.
2.	 Slice the cherry tomatoes in halves, thinly slice the red onion, and cube or slice the avocado. In a large 
salad bowl, add a base of fresh salad greens, then top with the prepared tomatoes, onion, avocado, and 
a handful of blueberries.
3.	 For the dressing (optional but recommended), whisk together honey mustard, a pinch of salt and pepper, 
garlic powder, and a bit more lemon juice until well combined and creamy.
4.	 Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and cook the marinated shrimp for 2–3 minutes on each side until 
golden and cooked through. Add the shrimp to the salad, drizzle generously with the dressing, and serve 
immediately—fresh, zesty, and full of flavour!

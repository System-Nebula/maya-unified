How to make
1.	 In a large bowl, whisk together egg whites, almond milk, protein powder, vanilla extract, and cinnamon 
until smooth and bubbly. Cut 8 slices of low-calorie bread into cubes, then gently fold them into the 
mixture and let soak for a few minutes.
2.	 Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and lightly grease a muffin tray with cooking spray.
3.	 Divide the soaked bread mixture evenly into the muffin tray cups, topping each with a teaspoon of apple 
sauce.
4.	 Bake for 20–25 minutes until golden and set. Let cool slightly, then enjoy warm with maple syrup if you 
like!

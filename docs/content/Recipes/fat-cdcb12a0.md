INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Chicken & Seasoning
	
– 700g (25oz) Raw Chicken 
Breast, butterflied halves
	
– 2 Tsp Italian Herbs
	
– 2 Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 1.5 Tsp Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
Pasta
	
– 230g (8.1oz) Uncooked Spaghetti Pasta
Alfredo Sauce
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Garlic Cloves, finely chopped
	
– 80g (3oz) White Onion, finely chopped
	
– 300g (10.6oz) 1% Milk
	
– 150g (5.3oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Parmigiano Reggiano
	
– 1 Tsp each: Italian Herbs, Salt, Black Pepper
	
– 50-70g (1/4 Cup) Pasta Water
	
– Garnish Fresh Parsley
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breasts into thinner fillets, then 
season both sides with Italian herbs, smoked 
paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and olive oil. 
Rub the seasoning in evenly, then set aside.
	
z S T E P 2 
Cook the pasta. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil, 
add the spaghetti pasta, and cook for 8 minutes 
until al dente. Reserve some pasta water, then drain 
the rest.
	
z S T E P 3
Prepare the Alfredo sauce by blending milk, cream 
cheese, parmesan, Italian herbs, salt, pepper, and 
the reserved pasta water until smooth. Then set 
aside.
	
z S T E P 4
Heat a pan over medium heat with a little olive oil. 
Cook the seasoned chicken for 3–4 minutes per 
side until golden brown. Keep an eye on the spices 
to prevent burning. Once done, set aside.
	
z S T E P 5
In the same pan, sauté chopped garlic and onion 
over medium heat for 2–3 minutes until fragrant. 
Lower the heat, then pour in the blended Alfredo 
sauce. Stir continuously with a whisk until thick and 
creamy.
	
z S T E P 6
Add the cooked spaghetti to the sauce, mixing 
gently until fully coated. Serve with the sliced 
chicken, garnish with parsley, and drizzle with extra 
sauce. Enjoy!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Ensure both sides of the chicken are evenly coated with seasoning.
	
z
Cook the chicken over medium-low heat for 3–4 minutes per side to prevent the seasoning from burning.
	
z
When adding the Alfredo sauce, keep the heat on low and stir gently until it thickens, then mix in the cooked 
pasta.

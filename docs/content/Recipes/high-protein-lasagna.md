z

Lasagna Sheets / 390g (13.7oz) 
Cooked Lasagna Sheets
	
z
70-80g (2.8oz) Light 
Shredded Mozzarella
How to make
1.	 Start by making the Béchamel sauce—combine all the listed ingredients in 
a blender cup and blend until smooth and creamy, then set aside.
2.	 In a pan over medium heat, add olive oil, chopped onion, grated carrot, 
salt, pepper, and tomato paste. Cook for 5 minutes, then add the ground 
beef and smoked paprika, cooking for another 5 minutes while breaking up 
the beef.
3.	 Add beef stock or water, canned tomatoes, garlic powder, Italian herbs, 
and salt to the pan. Mix well, stir in some basil leaves, cover, and let simmer 
for 10–15 minutes. Meanwhile, boil your lasagna sheets for about 6 minutes 
until al dente.
4.	 In a baking dish, start layering in this order: meat mixture, lasagna sheets, 
then Béchamel sauce. Repeat the process two more times.
5.	 Top with shredded mozzarella and bake at 180°C for 25–30 minutes. Once 
golden and bubbly, remove from the oven and serve hot—enjoy!

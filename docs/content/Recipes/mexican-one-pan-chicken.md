How to make
1.	 Cube the chicken breast and season with garlic powder, paprika, oregano, cumin, chili powder, salt, 
pepper, and lime juice. Mix thoroughly until the color of the chicken changes—this ensures it’s well 
seasoned.
2.	 Heat a pan over high heat, spray with oil, and cook the chicken for 3 minutes. Add chopped onions, bell 
pepper, and black beans, then cook for an additional minute until slightly softened.
3.	 Add washed uncooked rice to the pan along with another sprinkle of the same spices used on the chicken. 
Stir and toast the rice for 1 minute to enhance its flavor.
4.	 Pour in 1 can of chopped tomatoes and add water (enough to cook the rice). Bring the mixture to a boil, 
then reduce the heat and simmer for 12 minutes, stirring once halfway through, until the rice is tender and 
fully cooked.
5.	 Garnish with chopped green onion, fresh coriander, and a squeeze of lime before serving. Enjoy!

How to make
1.	 In a bowl, combine all the marinade ingredients and mix thoroughly 
until smooth and well blended.
2.	 Preheat your oven to 220°C (430°F). Line a baking tray with parchment 
paper and place the salmon fillets on top. Spoon the marinade evenly 
over each piece, spreading it to coat fully.
3.	 Switch your oven setting to broil. Place the salmon in the oven and 
broil for 6–10 minutes, depending on thickness. Keep an eye on it to 
avoid overcooking.
4.	 While the salmon cooks, stir-fry your choice of vegetables in a hot 
pan, or roast them alongside the salmon in the oven if you prefer.
5.	 Once everything is ready, plate the salmon with your veggies and a 
portion of jasmine rice. Serve immediately and enjoy!

INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breasts into bite-sized cubes. 
Season with garlic powder, smoked paprika, cumin, 
salt, black pepper, and olive oil. Mix thoroughly until 
the chicken is well coated and the colour deepens. 
Set aside to marinate while you prepare the potatoes
	
z S T E P 2 
Slice the raw potatoes into small, even cubes 
and place them in a large mixing bowl. Season with 
salt, Italian herbs, smoked paprika, garlic powder, 
onion powder, grated parmesan, and olive oil. Mix 
well until all the potatoes are evenly coated. Spread 
them out in a single layer on a lined baking tray 
(use baking paper for easy cleanup). Oven bake or 
air fry at 200°C (400°F) for 20–22 minutes until 
golden and crispy.
	
z S T E P 3

Add the marinated chicken and cook for 5 minutes

z S T E P 4
In a blender, combine cottage cheese and milk. 
Blend for 2 minutes until completely smooth. Pour 
the mixture into the same pan on low heat. Add 
Italian herbs, garlic powder, and grated parmesan.

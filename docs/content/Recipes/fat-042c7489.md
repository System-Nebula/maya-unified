FAT

INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Garlic Steak Bites
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Topside/Top 
Sirloin Beef, 1 inch cubed
	
– 20g (1.5 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 2 Tsp Salt
	
– 2 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Parsley
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
	
– 20g (1oz) Reduced Fat Butter 
(for cooking)
Cheesy Sweet Potatoes
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Sweet Potatoes, cubed 
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 1.5 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 1.5 Tsp Parsley
	
– 2 Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil (optional)
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Light Cheddar Cheese (or mozzarella)
	
– Garnish Fresh Parsley & Chilli Flakes
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Start by slicing your steaks into bite-sized cubes. 
Place in a mixing bowl, add minced garlic, salt, 
black pepper, parsley, and olive oil. Mix thoroughly 
until every piece is well coated, then set aside to 
marinate while you prep the potatoes.
	
z S T E P 2 
Slice your sweet potatoes into small, even cubes 
for faster and even cooking. Add to a bowl, season 
with salt, pepper, paprika, parsley, minced 
garlic, and a drizzle of olive oil (optional for extra 
crispiness). Toss until all pieces are well coated.
	
z S T E P 3 
Lay the seasoned sweet potatoes out evenly on 
a lined sheet pan without overcrowding. Bake or 
air fry at 200°C (400°F) for 17–19 minutes, shaking 
halfway through, until golden and crispy.
	
z S T E P 4
While the potatoes cook, heat a pan over medium-
high heat, add light butter, and cook the steak 
bites for 2–3 minutes per side until a deep golden-
brown crust forms. Avoid overcrowding to ensure a 
good sear — cook in batches if needed.
	
z S T E P 5
Once all steak bites are done, lower the heat, add a 
handful of fresh parsley, and stir well to coat in the 
butter and herbs.
	
z S T E P 6
Add the crispy sweet potatoes to the steak, toss 
everything together gently, then sprinkle grated 
cheddar over the top. Cover the pan with a lid for 
1–2 minutes to melt the cheese. Finally, distribute 
evenly into 4 portions and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Marinate the steak bites for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to absorb fully.
	
z
Quick tip: Add 1 tsp baking soda to the steak when marinating to help tenderize it.
	
z
Ensure the sweet potatoes are evenly and well-seasoned for maximum flavor.
	
z
To reheat, microwave for 2–3 minutes or warm in a pan over low heat for 3–5 minutes for best texture.

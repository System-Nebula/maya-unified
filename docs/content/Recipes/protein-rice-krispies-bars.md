z
2 Scoops Protein Powder
	
z
Melted Dark Chocolate (Optional)
How to make
1.	 In a large bowl, combine Rice Krispies, peanut butter, honey, and protein powder. Mix thoroughly until 
everything is evenly coated and well combined.
2.	 Transfer the mixture to a lined baking dish and press it down firmly using a spatula or your hands until 
compact. Drizzle melted dark chocolate over the top and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to set.
3.	 Once firm, slice into bars and enjoy your homemade protein-packed snack!

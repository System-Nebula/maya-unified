– 50g (1.8oz / ~2.5 Tbsp) Tomato Paste
	
– 2 servings Taco Spice Mix 
	
– 60g (2.1oz) Shredded Mozzarella 
(or any low-fat cheese) 
	
– 25g (1oz) Grass-Fed Butter
Chipotle Spicy Sauce 
(big batch - stores for the week)
	
– 125g (4.4oz) 0% Greek Yogurt (or yogurt of choice) 
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Light Mayo 
	
– 62g (2.2oz) Chipotle Paste 
	
– 25g (1oz) Buffalo Hot Sauce 
	
– 25g (1oz) Honey 
	
– 1 Tsp each Salt, Paprika, Garlic Powder
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 : M A K E T H E TAC O S P I C E M I X
Combine salt, black pepper, onion powder, garlic 
powder, cumin, paprika, chilli powder, and oregano. 
Double the amounts to create 2 servings for the beef.
	
z S T E P 2 : M A K E T H E C H I P OT L E 
S P I CY S AU C E
Blend the yogurt, mayo, chipotle paste, buffalo hot 
sauce, honey, salt, paprika, and garlic powder until 
smooth and creamy. Bottle and refrigerate for up to 
one week. 
	
z S T E P 3 : C O O K T H E TAC O B E E F
Heat a pan over medium-high heat and melt the butter. 
Add the ground beef and cook for 8-10 minutes, 
breaking it apart until fully browned.
Stir in the tomato paste and 2 servings of taco 
spice mix, then cook for another 3-4 minutes until 
juicy, seasoned, and lightly caramelised.
	
z S T E P 4 : F I N I S H & S TO R E
Top the cooked beef with shredded mozzarella. 
Store the beef in glass containers and cool 
completely before refrigerating.
BEST WAYS TO SERVE AND PAIR THIS PROTEIN 
Perfect Mexican-inspired pairings, simple and flavourful:
	
–with Mexican rice or lime-cilantro rice 
	
–in a tortilla wrap with lettuce and pico de gallo 
	
–in a taco salad bowl with tomatoes, corn, and black beans
SAUCE PAIRING SUGGESTIONS 
This taco beef also pairs well with:
	
–Chipotle Spicy Sauce (included in this recipe) 
	
–lime yogurt sauce (yogurt, lime, salt, garlic) 
	
–honey lime drizzle (honey, lime juice, chilli flakes)

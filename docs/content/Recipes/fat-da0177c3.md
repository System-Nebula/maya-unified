IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
The yogurt, lemon juice, and spices in the marinade help tenderise the chicken and infuse flavour. For the 
best results, marinate for at least 1 hour, or overnight if possible.
	
z
Make sure the chicken thighs are not overlapping. Air circulation is key for crispy edges and caramelisation.
	
z
Once cooked, rest the chicken for 5 minutes before slicing into cubes. Make sure to mix with all the resting 
juices from the pan - no flavour gets wasted!
	
z
Add salt to the fries after cooking. Sprinkling salt later helps prevent excess moisture release during 
cooking, keeping them crisp.
	
z
Make the salad in batches and store it separately in the fridge to keep it fresh and crisp throughout the week.
	
z
Use salt lightly in the coleslaw to prevent it becoming watery.
	
z
When reheating, reheat the chicken and fries in the air fryer for 5 minutes at 190°C for the best texture.

How to make
1.	 Cut the chicken breast in half horizontally to create two thinner pieces, then marinate with olive oil, 
paprika, garlic powder, mixed herbs, salt, and pepper.
2.	 Heat a pan over medium heat, spray with oil, and cook the chicken for 3–4 minutes on one side until lightly 
browned.
3.	 Flip the chicken, reduce the heat to low, and add chopped tomatoes around the chicken in the pan. 
Season the tomatoes with garlic powder, mixed herbs, and a pinch of salt.
4.	 Add a handful of spinach to the pan, cover with a lid, and let everything simmer on low heat for 8 minutes 
until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
5.	 Serve the chicken along with the tomato and spinach sauce over hot rice and enjoy!

How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken thighs into bite-sized pieces, then add fresh 
minced garlic, oregano, paprika, salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Mix 
well and let it marinate for as long as possible.
2.	 Soak wooden skewers in water, then skewer the marinated chicken 
pieces and place them on a baking tray.
3.	 Preheat the oven to 200°C and bake the chicken for 30 minutes 
until golden brown and juicy. Alternatively, you can air fry for about 
18 minutes.
4.	 While the chicken cooks, make the lemon pepper sauce by whisking 
together light mayo, sriracha, salt, pepper, paprika, and lemon 
juice until smooth and creamy.
5.	 Grab Lebanese flatbread or any wrap of your choice. Add shredded 
lettuce, sliced red onion, tomato, the juicy cooked chicken, and a 
generous drizzle of the lemon pepper sauce. Wrap and enjoy!

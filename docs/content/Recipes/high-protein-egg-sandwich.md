High Protein Egg Sandwich
A perfect meal prep sandwich and a new way for you to eat eggs with toast so tasty using simple ingredients.
Macros per serving
324 CALORIES
 26g PROTEIN

7.5g FAT
Ingredients (2 sandwiches)

2 Whole Eggs and 4 Egg Whites
	
z
2 Tbsp Low Fat Cream Cheese
	
z
1/2 Tsp Salt & Pepper
	
z
1/2 Tsp Onion Powder
	
z
1/2 Tsp Paprika
	
z
Green Onion or Scallion, chopped
	
z
4 Slices Wholegrain/Wholemeal Bread
	
z
Lettuce
	
z
Red Chilli Flakes & Parsley (for garnish)
How to make
1.	 Begin by boiling your eggs for 8 minutes until hard-boiled. Once done, cool them under cold water, peel 
carefully, and separate the yolks from the whites.
2.	 In a bowl, place 2 of the yolks and add low-fat cream cheese. Season with salt, pepper, onion powder, 
paprika, and finely chopped green onion. Mix everything together until smooth and creamy.
3.	 Chop the remaining egg whites into small pieces and fold them into the creamy yolk mixture. Stir until 
everything is evenly combined.
4.	 Toast 4 slices of wholemeal bread to your preferred crispiness. Assemble each slice with a layer of fresh 
lettuce, a generous spoonful of the egg mix, and finish with a sprinkle of chilli flakes and chopped parsley. 
Serve and enjoy!

For the fresh salad, combine chopped tomatoes, 
red onions, cucumber, green onions, and parsley 
in a large bowl. Season with your preferred spices 
and a squeeze of lemon juice; toss thoroughly.

Heat a pan over medium-high heat and add butter. 
Cook the marinated chicken for 3-4 minutes per

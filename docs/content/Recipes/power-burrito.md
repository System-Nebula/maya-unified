```
Power Burrito
So much flavor with every bite and the chicken is always so juicy,
melts in your mouth
Macros per serving
520 CALORIES
43g PROTEIN
46g  CARBS
20g FAT
Ingredients (2 burritos)
Chicken:
	
z
280g (9.8oz) Raw Chicken 
Breast, cubed
	
z
1 Tsp Olive Oil
	
z
1 Tbsp Garlic Powder
	
z
1 Tbsp Onion Powder
	
z
1 Tsp Cumin
	
z
1 Tbsp Oregano
	
z
1 Tbsp Paprika
	
z
1 Tsp Salt
Rice:
	
z
80-100g (3.5oz) Black Beans
	
z
1 Cup Uncooked Rice (This Will 
Be More Rice Than Needed)
	
z
1 Tsp of All the Same Seasonings 
You Used For the Chicken
	
z
2 Cups of Water
Burrito:
	
z
1 High Protein Low Calorie Wrap
	
z
40-50g (1.7oz) of Rice
	
z
100g (3.5oz) of Chicken
	
z
10g 0.3oz) Shredded 
Mozzarella (Optional)
	
z
Red Bell Pepper (Cooked 
After the Chicken) 
	
z
1 Tbsp Mashed Avocado
	
z
A Few Jalapeños
How to make
1.	 Season the chicken with olive oil and all the listed spices, ensuring each 
piece is well coated for maximum flavour.
2.	 In a pan over medium heat, spray with oil and add black beans, rice, the 
same seasonings, and 2 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a 
simmer and cook for 8–10 minutes until the rice is fully done. Remove from the 
pan and set aside.
3.	 In the same pan, cook the chicken over medium-high heat for about 5 minutes 
on each side until golden and cooked through. Let it rest for 2–3 minutes before 
cutting into small pieces.
4.	 Lay out a tortilla wrap and add a layer of rice, followed by the chopped 
chicken, cheese, sliced peppers, avocado, and jalapeños.
5.	 Fold the wrap into a burrito, then lightly toast it in the pan or a sandwich press 
until golden and sealed. Serve warm and enjoy!
This recipe make more rice than 
you will need.
Reduce fat and calories by using 
less avocado and/or no cheese!
69

```

How to make
1.	 In a blender, combine cooked rice, whole eggs, egg whites, protein powder, baking powder, cinnamon, 
and vanilla extract. Blend for a few minutes until smooth.
2.	 Adjust the batter consistency if needed—add more egg whites if it’s too thick, or more rice if too runny.
3.	 Heat a non-stick pan over low-medium heat. Pour in 1/4 cup of the batter for each pancake and cook for 
about 1 minute per side, or until golden. You should get around 5 pancakes.
4.	 Stack the pancakes and top with your choice of toppings. I used half a banana, low sugar maple syrup, 
and a dollop of whipped cream. Enjoy!

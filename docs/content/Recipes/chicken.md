INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Place the boneless, skinless chicken thighs into a 
large mixing bowl. Add minced garlic, shawarma 
spices (paprika, onion powder, cumin, cinnamon, 
sumac, salt, pepper), tomato paste, olive oil, and a 
squeeze of fresh lemon juice. Mix thoroughly until 
the chicken is evenly coated and vibrant in colour. 
Set aside to marinate for at least 30 minutes - 
longer develops deeper flavour.

z S T E P 3
Spread the marinated chicken thighs evenly on a 
lined sheet pan. Bake or air fry at 200°C / 400°F 
for 20-23 minutes, until golden, slightly charred, 
and juicy. Once cooked, let the chicken rest for 5 
minutes. For meal prep, keep the chicken whole 
when storing and slice into cubes only when 
serving.
BEST WAYS TO SERVE AND PAIR THIS PROTEIN 
This Chicken Shawarma works extremely well in simple, Middle Eastern–inspired combinations, for example:
	
–with turmeric or lemon rice and fresh cucumber 
	
–in a warm wrap with pickles and shredded lettuce

Creamy Chicken Mac and Cheese
Creamy, Spicy Chicken Mac and Cheese! So easy to make, packed with protein, 
calorie friendly and great for meal prep!
Macros per serving
498 CALORIES
 52g PROTEIN
49g  CARBS
11.5g FAT
Ingredients 
Crispy Chicken
	
z
500g (17.6oz) Chicken Breast, cubed
	
z
2 Tsp Olive Oil
	
z
1 Tsp each: Salt, Pepper, Paprika, Oregano and 
Garlic Powder (adjust to your preference)
	
z
200ml (7.1oz) any milk
	
z
120g (4.2oz) Light or Fat Free Cream Cheese
	
z
40g (1.4oz) Low Fat Cheese (Cheddar or Mozzarella)
	
z
3 Tbsp any Hot Sauce
	
z
170g (6oz) Uncooked Macaroni
(Optional) Coriander or Parsley for Garnish
How to make
1.	 Cut the chicken breasts into cubes and season with olive oil, salt, 
pepper, paprika, oregano, and garlic powder. Mix well to coat evenly 
and set aside to marinate.
2.	 Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add the macaroni pasta and cook 
for 8–10 minutes until al dente. Reserve a bit of pasta water, then drain the rest 
and set the pasta aside.
3.	 Heat a pan over medium heat and spray lightly with cooking spray. Add the 
chicken and cook for 6–8 minutes until golden and fully cooked through. 
Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside.
4.	 In the same pan over low heat, add low-fat milk, light cream cheese, 
cheddar cheese, and a splash of hot sauce. Stir with a whisk or spatula 
until everything melts and the sauce becomes smooth and creamy.
5.	 Add the cooked macaroni to the sauce and stir until well combined. Add the 
cooked chicken back in and mix everything together.
6.	 Garnish with chopped parsley and enjoy!
Make sure to cook your sauce on 
low heat, and give it some time to 
thicken.
If you’d like to speed up the 
process, add 1 tsp of flour! Also, 
adding 1/3 cup pasta water to your 
sauce can also help with the sauce 
sticking to your macaroni.
Use a buffalo hot sauce for a“buffalo 
chicken mac and cheese”!
Double the measurements for 6 
total servings for the entire week as a
meal prep!

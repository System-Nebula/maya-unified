INGREDIENTS (5 SERVINGS)
Sweet n Sour Chicken
	
– 1000g (35oz) Raw Boneless Chicken Thighs, cubed
	
– 2 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 30g (2 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Low Sugar Ketchup
	
– 75ml (2.7oz) Pineapple Juice (fresh preferably)
	
– 20ml (1oz) Rice Vinegar
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Dark Soy Sauce
	
– 250g (5.3oz) Fresh Pineapple, diced and cubed
	
– 75g (2.7oz) Red Onion, chopped
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Red Bell Pepper, chopped
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Yellow Bell Pepper, chopped
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Green Bell Pepper, chopped
	
– 30g (2 Tbsp) Honey
Kimchi Fried Rice
	
– 20g (1oz) Reduced Fat Butter (for cooking)
	
– 25g (1.5 Tbsp) Garlic Cloves, finely chopped
	
– 80g (2.8oz) Green Onion, chopped (white part)
	
– 150g (5.3oz) Carrots, chopped
	
– 3 Whole Eggs
	
– 250g (8.8oz) Uncooked Basmati Rice/600g 
Cooked
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Dark Soy Sauce
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Kimchi, chopped
	
– 80-100g (3.5oz) Kimchi Juice (from the jar)
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Green Onion, chopped (green part)
	
– Garnish Sesame Seeds & Red Pepper Flakes
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice boneless, skinless chicken thighs into bite-
sized cubes and place them into a mixing bowl. 
Season with black pepper, onion powder, minced 
garlic, ketchup, pineapple juice, rice vinegar, and 
soy sauce. Mix thoroughly until the chicken is fully 
coated and vibrant in colour. Set aside to marinate 
while prepping the rest.
	
z S T E P 2 
If you’re not using leftover rice, start by boiling a 
pot of water. Add raw white rice and cook for 8–10 
minutes until fluffy and fully cooked. Drain if needed 
and set aside to cool (cooling prevents clumping 
later). Meanwhile, dice your fresh pineapple (or drain 
canned pineapple), and chop red onion and red, 
yellow, and green bell peppers into small cubes.
	
z S T E P 3 
Spread the marinated chicken evenly on a lined 
sheet pan, followed by the chopped pineapple and 
vegetables. Arrange in a single layer to ensure even 
cooking—don’t overcrowd the tray. If needed, bake 
in batches. Note: Bell peppers and pineapple will 
release liquid during baking, so avoid overloading. 
Drizzle honey lightly across the top for extra glaze 
and sweetness. 
	
z S T E P 4
Oven bake at 200°C (400°F) for 30 minutes until 
the chicken is golden and tender. If using an air fryer, 
cook at 200°C for 25–30 minutes. If the chicken 
hasn’t browned to your liking, broil for an extra 3–5 
minutes, keeping a close eye to avoid burning. The 
juices in the tray should reduce and create a sticky, 
caramelised glaze.
	
z S T E P 5
Heat a large pan over medium-high heat, melt 
some butter, then sauté chopped garlic, the white 
part of green onion, and diced carrots. Cook 
for 5–6 minutes until soft and fragrant. Push the 
veggies to one side of the pan.
	
z S T E P 6
On the empty side, pour in beaten eggs and stir 
continuously until fully scrambled. Mix the eggs 
together with the sautéed vegetables. Add in the 
leftover cooked rice, a splash of soy sauce, chopped 
kimchi, and kimchi juice. Finish with the green part of 
the green onion and stir everything together until well 
incorporated, fluffy, and full of colour.
	
z S T E P 7
Garnish with sesame seeds and red pepper flakes, 
then distribute the kimchi fried rice and sweet & 
sour chicken evenly into 5 meal prep containers. 
Your high-protein, flavour-packed meals are ready 
to ENJOY all week!

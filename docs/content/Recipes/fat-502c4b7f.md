INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Garlic Butter Steak Bites
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Topside/Top 
Sirloin Beef, 1 inch cubed
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 2 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Parsley
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
Cooking and Final Glaze
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Reduced Fat Butter
	
– 30g (2 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 1 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, chopped
Crispy Potatoes
	
– 900g (32oz) Raw Potatoes, cubed
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 1 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 1 Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1 Tsp Parsley
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil (Optional)
Spicy Mayo
	
– 30g (2 Tbsp) 50% Fat Mayonnaise
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Sriracha or any Hot Sauce
	
– Juice of ¼ Lemon
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Cut the steaks into 1-inch cubes. Season with salt, 
black pepper, garlic powder, parsley, and olive oil. 
Mix thoroughly until evenly coated, then set aside.
	
z S T E P 2 
Dice the uncooked potatoes into small cubes 
and place them in a bowl. Season with salt, black 
pepper, smoked paprika, garlic powder, parsley, 
and a drizzle of olive oil (optional). Mix well, then 
spread them on a lined baking sheet (use baking 
paper). Oven bake or air fry at 200°C/400°F for 
20–22 minutes until crispy and golden.
	
z S T E P 3 
While the potatoes cook, heat a pan over medium-
high heat and melt 10g butter. Sear the half of the 
steak bites for 2–3 minutes per side until a rich, 
golden crust forms.
	
z S T E P 4
Lower the heat and add 10g extra butter, minced 
garlic, and chopped parsley. Stir for 1–2 minutes 
until the steak bites are fully coated in this garlic 
butter, then remove from heat. Repeat for the 
second batches.
	
z S T E P 5
Prepare the spicy mayo by mixing light mayo, 
sriracha, and lemon juice in a bowl. Serve the steak 
bites with the crispy potatoes, drizzle with the 
spicy mayo, and enjoy your high-protein meals for 
the week!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Slice beef into 1-inch cubes to keep it juicy and develop a nice crust.
	
z
Air fry steak bites for 12–15 minutes at 200°C (400°F) as an alternative.
	
z
Use store-bought spicy mayo or make your own.
	
z
Reheat in an air fryer or oven for 5 minutes at 180°C (370°F), or in a pan on low heat for 4–5 minutes.

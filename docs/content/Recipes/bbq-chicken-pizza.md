How to make
1.	 Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and place the Lebanese flatbread on a baking tray. In a bowl, mix BBQ 
sauce with hot sauce and spread a thin, even layer over the flatbread.
2.	 Season the chicken breast to your preference, cook in a pan for about 5 minutes on each side until fully 
cooked, then remove and let it rest for a few minutes. Shred the chicken using two forks and mix it with the 
remaining BBQ sauce.
3.	 Top the flatbread with low-fat mozzarella, the shredded BBQ chicken, and thinly sliced red onion.
4.	 Bake in the preheated oven for about 7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and everything is heated 
through.
5.	 Once out of the oven, drizzle with extra BBQ sauce, garnish with chopped spring onion, and enjoy hot!

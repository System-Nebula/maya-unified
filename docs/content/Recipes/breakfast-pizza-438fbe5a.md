How to make
1.	 Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) and place a tortilla wrap into a 
small baking dish or oven-safe bowl, pressing it in so the edges form a 
raised crust.
2.	 Crack a couple of eggs directly into the center of the wrap.
3.	 Add chopped chilli pepper, diced tomato, a handful of spinach, and 
shredded mozzarella on top.
4.	 Season with salt, pepper, and any herbs you like.
5.	 Bake in the oven for about 10 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the 
edges of the tortilla are golden. Remove and enjoy immediately!
Make sure you toast the tortilla 
properly to get a nice hard crust.

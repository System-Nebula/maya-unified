IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Pat the steak dry before seasoning for a better sear. Moisture on the surface prevents browning and can 
cause the steak to steam instead of caramelise.
	
z
• Do not overcrowd the pan when searing. Cook the steak bites in batches to maintain high heat and 
achieve a deep golden crust.
	
z
• Use medium heat for searing, then reduce to low before adding butter, honey, and parsley. This prevents 
the glaze from burning and helps it thicken into a sticky coating.
	
z
• Keep the heat low when making the sauce. Evaporated milk and cream cheese can split if overheated, so 
gentle heat ensures a smooth, creamy texture.

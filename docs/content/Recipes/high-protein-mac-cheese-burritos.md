How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken breast into thinner pieces, season with all the listed spices, and mix until both sides 
are well coated.
2.	 In a blender cup, add all the cheese sauce ingredients and spices. Blend in 10-second intervals until 
completely smooth.
3.	 Heat a pan on medium with cooking spray or olive oil. Cook the chicken for 5 minutes on each side until 
golden, then shred using forks or knives.
4.	 While the chicken cooks, boil the macaroni pasta until al dente, then drain.
5.	 On low heat, add the cooked pasta, cheese sauce, and shredded chicken to the same pan. Mix until 
everything is well combined.
6.	 Spoon the mac & cheese mixture into low-calorie wraps, fold into burritos, toast on a pan, wrap in foil, 
and ENJOY!

How to make
1.	 In a blender cup, combine cottage cheese, coconut flour, eggs, baking powder, 
and vanilla extract. Blend for 2–4 minutes until the batter is completely smooth. 
Let it rest for 10 minutes to thicken slightly.
2.	 Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly spray with oil if needed. Pour 
about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake. Cook for around 2 minutes until the 
edges set and bubbles form, then flip gently and cook for another 1–2 minutes until 
golden brown.
3.	 Stack your pancakes on a plate and top with your favorite toppings—I used low-
sugar maple syrup and fresh blueberries. Time to enjoy!

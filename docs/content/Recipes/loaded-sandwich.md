How to make
1.	 Spray oil on a non-stick pan over low heat. Crack the eggs into the pan 
and immediately place two slices of bread on top.
2.	 Cook until the eggs are almost set, then carefully flip the bread and 
eggs together using a wide spatula.
3.	 Fold the sides of the bread inward, season with salt, pepper, and 
paprika. Layer turkey slices, bell pepper, mixed greens, sriracha, and 
low-fat cheese on top.
4.	 Fold the top slice of bread over, then toast both sides for a few minutes

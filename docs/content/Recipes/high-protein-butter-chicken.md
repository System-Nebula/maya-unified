High Protein Butter Chicken
Butter chicken is one of my favourite comfort foods and this macro friendly version is incredible and such a game changer 
when it comes to weight loss! The flavours are amazing, so easy to make and you can serve it with rice or naan bread!

z

z

z
1 Can Chopped

z
50g (1.7oz) Greek Yogurt
	
z
Serve with 140g Cooked 
Basmati Rice or Naan Bread
	
z
Garnish with Fresh 
Coriander/Cilantro
How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken into cubes, season with the listed ingredients, mix until the color changes, and set aside 
to marinate.
2.	 In a pan over medium heat, melt light butter, add sliced onions and the same seasoning, and cook for 2 
minutes. Then add cashew nuts, chopped tomatoes, and yogurt. Stir until well combined.
3.	 Transfer the mixture to a blender and blend until smooth and creamy.
4.	 In the same pan, cook the marinated chicken for 4 minutes on medium heat until golden.
5.	 Lower the heat, pour in the blended sauce, mix well, and simmer for 2–3 minutes. Serve with rice or naan 
bread and enjoy!

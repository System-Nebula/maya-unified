Creamy Tuscan Chicken Pasta
One of the easiest and tastiest pasta recipes with incredible macros!The 
flavours are incredible and this is easy to meal prep as well.
Macros per serving
572 CALORIES
 67g PROTEIN
52g  CARBS
11g FAT
Ingredients (2 Servings)
Chicken:
	
z
2 Chicken Breasts Cut Into Halves
(Roughly 160g Each Piece)
	
z
1 Tbsp Paprika
	
z
1 Tbsp Garlic Powder
	
z
1 Tsp Onion Powder
	
z
1 Tbsp Oregano
	
z
1/2 Tsp Chilli Flakes
	
z
1 Tsp Salt & Pepper
	
z
1-2 Tsp Olive Oil
Pasta:
	
z
130g (4.6oz) Uncooked Pasta of 
Your Choice, Spaghetti, Linguine 
or Fettuccine Works Great (Can 
Opt for Wholewheat Versions Too) 
	
z
1 Tsp Salt for the Water
Creamy sauce:
	
z
200ml (7.1oz) Low Fat/
Skimmed Milk 
	
z
100g (3.5oz) Light Cream Cheese
	
z
20-30g (1.1oz) Grated Parmesan
	
z
1 Tsp Italian Herbs
	
z
90ml (3.1oz) Pasta Water
For cooking:
	
z
1-2 Tsp Olive Oil
	
z
2-3 Garlic Cloves, Chopped
	
z
1 Small Onion, Finely Diced
	
z
50g (1.7oz) Cherry Tomatoes
	
z
40g (1.4oz) Sundried 
Tomatoes (Optional)
	
z
40g (1.4oz) Tomato Paste
	
z
Handful of Spinach
	
z
Chopped Parsley for Garnish
How to make
1.	 Butterfly cut the chicken breast and season both sides with smoked paprika, 
garlic powder, onion salt, and chilli powder.
2.	 Bring salted water to a boil in a pot, add uncooked pasta, and cook for 8 
minutes until al dente. Reserve ½ cup of pasta water, drain the rest, and set 
the pasta aside.
3.	 In a blender cup, add milk, cream cheese, parmesan, Italian herbs, and the 
reserved pasta water. Blend until smooth and creamy.
4.	 Cook the chicken in a pan over medium heat for 3–4 minutes on each side until 
golden brown, then set aside.
5.	 In the same pan, add olive oil, chopped garlic, and finely chopped onion. 
Cook for 1–2 minutes, then add tomato paste, cherry tomatoes, and sun-
dried tomatoes. Cook for another minute, then turn off the heat and stir in the 
creamy sauce until it begins to thicken.
6.	 Add a handful of spinach and let it wilt, then add the cooked pasta. Turn the 
heat back on low, mix everything together, place the chicken on top, simmer for 
4–5 minutes, garnish with parsley, and enjoy.
When adding the creamy sauce 
to the pan, make sure the heat is 
completely turned OFF!
Otherwise the sauce will split 
and burn. Let it thicken first with 
the pasta then turn the heat 
back on low heat to simmer at 
the end.

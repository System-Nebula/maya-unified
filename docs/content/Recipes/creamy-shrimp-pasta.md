How to make
1.	 Rinse and pat dry the shrimp, then place in a bowl. Add minced fresh garlic, Italian herbs, paprika, salt, 
pepper, chilli flakes, and a splash of lemon juice. Mix until the shrimp are evenly coated, then set aside 
to marinate briefly.
2.	 Heat a pan over medium heat and add olive oil or cooking spray. Once hot, add the shrimp and cook for 
3–4 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and fully cooked. Remove from the pan and set aside.
3.	 Reduce the heat to low. In the same pan, pour in a bit of milk and add cream cheese, grated parmesan, 
Italian herbs, garlic powder, and a splash of reserved pasta water. Stir continuously until the mixture melts 
and forms a smooth, creamy sauce.
4.	 Add a handful of fresh spinach, then return the cooked shrimp to the pan along with your pre-cooked 
pasta. Toss everything together until well combined and the spinach wilts.
5.	 Once the dish is evenly heated and coated in the sauce, serve immediately. Optionally garnish with extra 
parmesan or herbs.

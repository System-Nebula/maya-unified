INGREDIENTS (5 SERVINGS)
Harissa Chicken Marinade
	
– 1000g (35oz) Boneless Chicken Thighs
	
– 200g (7.1oz) Fresh Tomatoes, firm preferably
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Fresh Parsley
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Garlic Cloves
	
– 100g (3.5oz) White Onion
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Red Onion
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Red Bell Peppers
	
– 150g (5.3oz) Yogurt
	
– 160g (5.6oz) Harissa Paste or Red Pepper Paste
	
– 2 Tsp Salt
	
– 2.5 Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– 3 Tsp Oregano
	
– 2 Tsp Cumin
	
– 3 Tsp Olive Oil
After blending the marinade, try to drain any 
excess liquid that builds to the surface. Allows 
for a thicker marinade with less moisture and 
chicken to get a good char/grill
Oven bake or air fry for 20 mins at 200C and 
broil/grill for 5 mins or longer if needed to get 
that charred crust
Golden Fluffy Rice
	
– 250g (8.8oz) Uncooked Basmati 
Rice / Approx 600g Cooked
	
– 15g (0.5oz) Reduced Fat Butter
	
– 100g (3.5oz) White Onion, finely chopped
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 1.5 Tsp Paprika
	
– 1 Tsp Turmeric
	
– 2 Tsp Dried Parsley
Tangy Harissa Sauce
	
– 200g (7.1oz) Low Fat Yogurt
	
– 60g (2.1oz) Light Mayo (Optional)
	
– 60g (2.1oz) Harissa Paste
	
– Splash of Pickle Juice (for desired consistency)
Fresh Cucumber Salad
	
– 300g (10.6oz) Fresh Tomatoes, de 
seeded & finely chopped
	
– 300g (10.6oz) Cucumber, de 
seeded & finely chopped
	
– 200g (7.1oz) Red Onion, finely chopped
	
– Handful Fresh Parsley, finely chopped
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1
In a blender or food processor, add roughly 
chopped tomatoes, a handful of parsley, garlic 
cloves, onion, red bell pepper, yogurt, harissa 
paste, salt, smoked paprika, oregano, cumin, and 
a drizzle of olive oil. Blend in short pulses for 5-8 
seconds until slightly chunky - you want texture, not 
a completely smooth paste.
	
z S T E P 2
Add the marinade to the boneless chicken thighs 
and mix thoroughly with your hands until fully coated. 
Set aside to marinate while you prepare the rice.
	
z S T E P 3
Boil uncooked basmati rice in a pot for 8–10 
minutes until fluffy. Meanwhile, heat a pan over 
medium heat, melt butter, and sauté chopped 
onions for 5 minutes until soft. Add salt, turmeric, 
parsley, paprika, and cumin. Toast for 2 minutes, 
then stir in the cooked rice until it turns golden and 
fluffy. Set aside.
	
z S T E P 4
Spread the marinated chicken evenly on a lined 
sheet pan. Oven bake or air fry at 200°C (400°F) for 
20-25 minutes until golden and juicy. Broil for 2-3 
minutes at the end if needed for extra char.
	
z S T E P 5
While the chicken cooks, make the creamy harissa 
sauce: blend low-fat yogurt, light mayo, harissa 
paste, and a splash of pickle juice until smooth. 
Bottle and refrigerate.
For the salad, combine chopped tomatoes, 
cucumber, red onion, and parsley in a large bowl. 
Store separately to keep fresh and make reheating 
easier.
	
z STEP 6
Once the chicken is done, let it rest for 2–3 minutes, 
then chop into bite-size pieces. Serve into 5 portions 
with the golden rice, salad, and creamy harissa 
sauce. Enjoy your high-protein meals!

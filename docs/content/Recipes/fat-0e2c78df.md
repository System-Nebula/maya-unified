FAT
There’s no reason to be meal prepping plain chicken & rice! 
This is an incredibly delicious meal prep and so simple to make
TAP FOR VIDEO
THE MEAL PREP COOK BOOK 
25
Return to Table of Contents  
INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Honey Chipotle Chicken 
Marinade
	
– 1000g Raw Boneless 
Skinless Chicken Thighs
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 1.5 Tsp Oregano
	
– 1.5 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1.5 Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– 1.5 Tsp Cumin
	
– 70g Chipotle Paste or 
Chipotle Peppers in Adobo
	
– 50g Honey
	
– ¾ of a Lime Juiced
Cilantro Lime Rice 
	
– 20g Light Butter
	
– 100g White Onion Chopped
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 1 Tsp Turmeric
	
– ¾ Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– ¾ Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– ½ Tsp Cumin
	
– 280g Raw Basmati 
Rice (Cook this prior 
to making this)
	
– Big Handful Chopped 
Cilantro/Coriander
	
– 1 Whole Lime Juice
Chipotle Sauce
	
– 100g Low Fat Yogurt 
(Any yogurt works)
	
– 50g Light Mayo
	
– 20-30g Chipotle Paste
	
– 50g Pineapple Chunks 
(Fresh preferably)
	
– ½ Tsp Salt
	
– ½ Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– ½ Tsp Onion Powder
	
– Squeeze of Lime Juice
	
– I like to pair this with a nice side salsa 
consisting of chopped tomatoes, 
red onion, bell peppers, cucumber, 
salt, pepper and pickle juice
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Place the boneless, skinless chicken thighs in a bowl 
and season with salt, oregano, garlic powder, smoked 
paprika, cumin, chipotle paste, honey, and fresh lime 
juice. Mix thoroughly until the chicken is fully coated 
and deeply coloured, then set aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2
While the chicken marinates, heat a pan over 
medium heat, melt a knob of butter, then add 
chopped onions. Cook for 5–6 minutes until soft, 
golden, and fragrant.
	
z S T E P 3
Add the seasoning blend: salt, turmeric, garlic 
powder, smoked paprika, and cumin. Stir well to 
coat the onions and let the spices toast for 1–2 
minutes to deepen the flavour and aroma.
	
z S T E P 4
Add your cooked basmati rice to the pan. Gently 
fold it in using a spatula, mixing thoroughly until 
every grain is coated and turn a bright yellow colour.
	
z S T E P 5
Turn off the heat, then add a generous handful of 
fresh chopped coriander (cilantro) and a squeeze 
of lime juice. Mix to finish the rice — vibrant, fluffy, 
and fragrant.
	
z S T E P 6
Lay the marinated chicken thighs evenly on a lined 
sheet pan. Oven bake or air fry at 200°C (400°F) 
for 20–23 minutes, or until golden, juicy, and slightly 
charred at the edges.
	
z S T E P 7
While the chicken rests make the sauce. In a blender 
cup add yogurt, mayo, chipotle paste, pineapple 
chunks, salt, garlic & onion powder and lime juice. 
Blend for 30–60 seconds until completely smooth 
and creamy.
	
z S T E P 8
Bottle up the sauce for the week (great for wraps, 
bowls). Slice the rested chicken into bite-sized 
cubes, then serve with the golden rice and a generous 
drizzle of the pineapple chipotle sauce.Your flavour-
packed high protein meals are ready to ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Oven bake or air fry for 20-23 mins at 200C / 400F - you can broil at the end for 2-3 mins to get the top 
nicely crispy and charred

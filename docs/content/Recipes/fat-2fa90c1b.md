Beef Marinade

– 150g (5.3oz) White Onion, thinly sliced
	
– 150g (5.3oz) Red Bell Pepper, thinly sliced
	
– 150g (5.3oz) Green Bell Pepper, thinly sliced
Black Pepper Sauce
	
– 200g (7oz) Beef Broth
	
– 20g (1oz) Dark Soy Sauce 
	
– 30g (1.2oz) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 25g (1.1oz) Hoisin Sauce
	
– 20g (1oz) Rice Vinegar
	
– 1 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 10g (1 Tsp) Cornflour/Cornstarch
Garlic Fried Rice
	
– 20g (1oz) Reduced Fat Butter
	
– 50g (3.5 Tbsp) Garlic Cloves, chopped
	
– 200g (7oz) Uncooked White Rice/540g 
Cooked Leftover Rice  (basmati or jasmine)
	
– 20g (1oz) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 1 Tsp Red Pepper Flakes to garnish
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the beef steaks into extra thin strips. In a bowl, 
combine with dark and light soy sauce, black pepper, 
garlic powder, baking soda, cornflour, and olive oil. 
Toss the beef until velvety, then set aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2 
Prepare the black pepper sauce by whisking 
together beef broth, light and dark soy sauce, 
hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, black pepper, and 
cornflour in a separate bowl until smooth.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan over high heat and add a little butter. 
Cook the marinated beef strips for 1–2 minutes 
per side until a golden, crispy crust forms. Remove 
the beef from the pan and set aside. Repeat for the 
remaining beef.

In the same pan, add sliced onions and bell 
peppers. »»
	» Continue
Sauté for about 5 minutes until soft and fragrant, 
then return the crispy beef to the pan and pour in 
the black pepper sauce.
	
z S T E P 5
Stir the mixture and let it come to a gentle bubble 
so the sauce thickens slightly. Simmer on low heat 
for 3–5 minutes until everything is heated through, 
then remove from the heat and set aside.
	
z S T E P 6 
For the garlic fried rice, heat another pan (or reuse 
the cleaned pan) over medium heat and melt a bit 
of butter. Sauté chopped garlic until golden and 
slightly crispy.
	
z S T E P 7
Add the leftover cooked rice along with a dash of 
soy sauce and red pepper flakes. Toss thoroughly 
until well combined. Serve the garlic rice alongside 
the black pepper beef and ENJOY!

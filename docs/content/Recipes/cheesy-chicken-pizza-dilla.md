Optional: Sriracha Mayo
How to make
1.	 Butterfly cut your chicken breast and season with salt, pepper, paprika, oregano, and garlic powder.
2.	 Heat a pan on medium, cook the chicken for 4 minutes on each side, then remove and shred using two 
forks.
3.	 In the same pan, place one tortilla, spread some tomato paste, then layer with shredded chicken, 
chopped onion, tomato, and cheese. Place a second tortilla on top.
4.	 Repeat the process if making more. Transfer to a baking tray and bake at 180°C for 5–8 minutes until golden 
and the cheese is melted.
5.	 Garnish with parsley and drizzle with sriracha mayo. Serve and enjoy!

Chilli Lime Chicken Marinade

– Zest of 1-2 Limes »»

– Juice of 1 Lime 
	
– 20g Light Soy Sauce (1oz / ~1 Tbsp)
Ahi Verde Creamy Sauce
	
– 150g Greek Yogurt (5.3oz / ~6 Tbsp) 
	
– 50g Light Mayo (1.7oz / ~3 Tbsp) 
	
– 10g Honey (0.3oz / ~1 Tsp) 
	
– 1 Green Chilli, deseeded 
	
– Large handful Fresh Coriander/Cilantro 
	
– Pinch of Salt & Pepper
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z STEP 1: PREP & MARINATE THE CHICKEN
Trim excess fat from the chicken thighs and place 
them in a large mixing bowl. Add salt, black pepper, 
minced garlic, chilli flakes, smoked paprika, honey, 
lime zest, lime juice, and light soy sauce. Mix 
thoroughly with your hands until the chicken is fully 
coated and the colour deepens. 
Set aside to marinate for at least 30 minutes (longer 
gives deeper flavour).
	
z S T E P 2 : M A K E T H E A H I V E R D E S AU C E
In a blender, combine Greek yogurt, light mayo, 
honey, deseeded green chilli, fresh coriander, salt, 
and pepper. 
Blend until completely smooth, then bottle and 
refrigerate for the week.
	
z S T E P 3 : C O O K T H E C H I C K E N
Spread the marinated chicken thighs evenly on a 
lined sheet pan, ensuring they sit in a single layer for 
even browning. 
Oven bake or air fry at 200°C / 400°F for 20-22 
minutes, until juicy with lightly charred edges.
	
z S T E P 4 : R E S T & S TO R E T H E C H I C K E N
Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes to retain its 
juices. For meal prep, store the chicken whole in 
airtight glass containers and include all resting 
juices for maximum flavour and moisture. Slice into 
cubes only when serving.
BEST WAYS TO SERVE AND PAIR THIS PROTEIN 
This Chilli Lime Chicken works extremely well in simple, Mexican-inspired combinations, for example:
	
–with cilantro lime rice and fresh pico de gallo 
	
–with crispy air fried seasoned potatoes 
	
–in a Mexican-style salad bowl with black beans, corn, and cucumber
SAUCE PAIRING SUGGESTIONS 
This chicken also pairs well with:
	
–Ahi Verde Creamy Sauce (included in this recipe) 
	
–chipotle yogurt sauce (yogurt, chipotle, lime) 
	
–lime-garlic crema (yogurt, garlic, lime juice)

This is my new favorite way of making chicken, it’s so crispy, juicy and absolutely

2 Eggs + 2 Egg Whites
	
z
240g (8.4oz) Leftover Cooked Rice
	
z

z
1 Tbsp Oyster Sauce
	
z

z
Garnish with Green Onion
How to make
1.	 Cut the chicken into cubes and season with garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, black pepper, soy 
sauce, and cornflour. Mix well until all pieces are fully coated.
2.	 Heat a pan on high heat, spray with cooking spray or add a little olive oil, then cook the chicken for 2–3 
minutes on each side until golden and crispy. Remove and set aside.
3.	 In the same pan, add mixed vegetables and cook for 2 minutes. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan 
and crack in the eggs. Scramble them while cooking, then mix together with the vegetables.
4.	 Add the cooked rice to the pan along with soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Stir everything 
together until evenly coated and heated through.
5.	 Return the crispy chicken to the pan, mix it in well, garnish with chopped green onion, and serve hot.

How to make
1.	 Start by cutting the chicken into cubes. Add all the seasonings listed then mix till well combined and set 
aside. If the colour doesn’t change you need to add more seasoning.
2.	 Make the garlic sauce by adding yogurt, minced garlic, salt, pepper and lemon juice into a blender. 
Blend till smooth. You can do this in a bowl if preferred.
3.	 Make the yellow rice. Add water to a pot then add washed uncooked rice. Add 1/2 tsp turmeric, cumin, 
paprika and salt. Bring to a boil, then lower the heat and let it cook through 10-12 mins.
4.	 While that’s cooking, get a pan on medium heat, spray oil then cook the chicken for 4 mins each side till 
golden brown.
5.	 Serve with cooked yellow rice, shredded lettuce, red onion (mixed with pepper or sumac), pickles,

Buttery Garlic Steak Bites

Beef, 1 inch cubed
	
– 2 Tsp Himalayan Pink Salt
	
– 2 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Dried Parsley
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
	
– 15-20g (1oz) Grass Fed Butter (for cooking)
Spicy Sauce
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Natural Yogurt
	
– 50g (1.8oz) 50% Fat Mayo

– 1 Tsp Smoked Paprika

Seasoned Potatoes
	
– 700g (25oz) Raw Potatoes, cubed
	
– 1 Tsp Himalayan Pink Salt
	
– 1 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 1 Tsp Cumin
	
– 1 Tsp Paprika
	
– 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– Optional 2 Tsp Olive Oil (or cooking spray)
Scrambled Eggs
	
– 8 Whole Eggs (pasture raised preferably)
	
– Pinch of Salt & Black Pepper
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the steaks into 1-inch cubes then season with 
salt, black pepper, garlic powder, parsley, and olive 
oil. Mix thoroughly until evenly coated then set aside.
	
z S T E P 2 
Cut the potatoes into small cubes and season 
with salt, black pepper, cumin, paprika, and garlic 
powder. Add a drizzle of olive oil or use cooking 
spray. Toss well, then spread them on a lined baking 
sheet. Oven-bake or air-fry at 200°C (400°F) for 
20–22 minutes until golden and crispy.
	
z S T E P 3 
For the spicy sauce, blend together yogurt, light 
mayo, chipotle chilli paste, salt, paprika, and garlic 
powder until smooth and creamy. Transfer to a 
squeezy bottle for easy storage.
	
z S T E P 4
Heat a pan over high heat, melt some butter, and 
sear the seasoned steak bites for 2–3 minutes 
per side until golden brown and charred. Lower the 
heat, add fresh minced garlic and fresh parsley, 
and toss until coated in the garlicky butter then set 
aside.
	
z S T E P 5
In the same pan over medium-low heat, add 
whisked eggs and stir continuously until scrambled 
and fluffy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
	
z S T E P 6
Evenly distribute the steak bites, crispy potatoes, 
and scrambled eggs into 4 servings. Drizzle with the 
spicy sauce and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Marinate the steak for 15-30mins to allow the seasonings to fully absorb.
	
z
Prepare the spicy sauce in batches—it can be stored in the fridge for weeks.
	
z
Cook the steak on high heat for 2 minutes per side to develop a flavorful crust.
	
z
Oven-bake or air-fry the potatoes at 200°C (400°F) for 20–22 minutes until crispy.
	
z
Cook the eggs over medium low heat, stirring continuously until fluffy.
	
z
To reheat, place everything in a pan before serving then drizzle with the spicy sauce.

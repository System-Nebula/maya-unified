Snickers Shake
If you’re looking for a tasty high protein shake that tastes very similar 
to a Snickers bar then I’ve got you covered!
Macros per serving
500 CALORIES
 33g PROTEIN
55g  CARBS
20g FAT
Ingredients (1 shake )
	
z
1 Frozen Banana (100g, 3.5oz)
	
z
2 Dates (Soft Dates)
	
z
1 Scoop Chocolate Protein Powder 
	
z
2 Tbsp Crunchy Peanut Butter
	
z
1/2 Tbsp Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
	
z
1 Cup (8oz) Almond Milk
	
z
Handful Ice Cubes
Toppings:
	
z
Sugar Free Chocolate Syrup or 
Melted Dark Chocolate
	
z
Crushed Peanuts
How to make
1.	 Add all your ingredients to a blender one at a time, being careful not to overfill it — the mixture will expand 
slightly as it blends. Secure the lid and blend on high speed for at least 30 seconds until smooth and 
creamy.
2.	 Pour into a glass, drizzle with sugar-free chocolate syrup, and finish with a sprinkle of crushed peanuts 
on top. Serve immediately and enjoy!

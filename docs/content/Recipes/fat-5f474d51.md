INGREDIENTS (5 SERVINGS)
Chicken & Seasoning
	
– 1000g (35oz) Raw Chicken Breast, cubed
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 1.5 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Oregano
	
– 2 Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– 2 Tsp Dried Basil
	
– 3 Tsp Olive Oil
	
– 10g (2 Tsp) Reduced Fat Butter (for cooking)
Caramelized Onion Creamy Sauce
	
– 20g (1oz) Reduced Fat Butter
	
– 350g (12.4oz) Red or White Onion, finely sliced
	
– 1/2 Tsp Salt
	
– 1/2 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 1 Tsp Smoked Paprika »»
	» Continue
	
– 1 Tsp Chilli Flakes
	
– 80g (2.8oz) Sun-dried Tomatoes, drained and 
chopped
	
– 10g (2 Tsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 380g (13.4oz) 1% Milk or Skimmed Milk
	
– 180g (6.4oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Parmigiano Reggiano, grated
	
– 1 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, chopped
Lemon Butter Herb Rice 
	
– 10g (2 Tsp) Reduced Fat Butter
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 1/2 Tsp Salt or to taste
	
– 2 Tsp Mixed Herbs
	
– 250g (8.8oz) Uncooked Basmati Rice/600g Cooked
	
– 1.5 Tsp Lemon Zest
	
– Juice of 1/2 Lemon
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breasts into bite-sized cubes 
and transfer to a mixing bowl. Season with salt, 
black pepper, garlic powder, oregano, smoked 
paprika, dried basil, and a drizzle of olive oil. Mix 
thoroughly until each piece is evenly coated and the 
colour deepens. Set aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2 
Heat a large pan over medium heat, melt down a 
little butter, then add the chicken in a single layer. 
Sear for 4–5 minutes per side until golden brown 
and slightly charred. Work in batches if needed. 
Once cooked, set aside to rest.
	
z S T E P 3 
In the same pan, reduce heat to medium-low. Add 
more butter and toss in all the sliced onions. Cook 
slowly for 15 minutes or longer, stirring occasionally 
until deep golden and caramelised—this step builds 
flavour, so take your time. Meanwhile, boil and cook 
your white basmati rice in a separate pot for later 
use. Once onions are caramelised, season with salt, 
black pepper, paprika, and chilli flakes. Then add 
minced garlic and sun-dried tomatoes, cooking for 
an additional 2 minutes until fragrant. 
	
z S T E P 4
Lower the heat fully and pour in milk, followed by 
cream cheese and grated parmesan. Stir gently 
and continuously until the mixture is smooth, 
creamy, and well combined. Now return the seared 
chicken to the pan along with a handful of freshly 
chopped parsley, giving everything a final stir until 
the chicken is coated in the creamy, savoury sauce.
	
z S T E P 5
For the rice, use a clean pan or the same one if large 
enough. On medium heat, melt a small amount of 
butter, then sauté minced garlic for 2 minutes until 
aromatic. Add salt to taste and a generous pinch 
of mixed or Italian herbs. Toast the herbs briefly 
for 30 seconds to release their oils, then add in the 
cooked rice.
	
z S T E P 6
Mix the rice well so the flavours fully combine. 
Remove from the heat, then grate over some fresh 
lemon zest and add a squeeze of lemon juice. Stir 
one last time until the rice is light, fragrant, and herby.
	
z S T E P 7
Evenly portion the lemon butter herb rice and 
creamy chicken into 5 meal prep containers. 
Garnish with more parsley if desired, and your high-
protein meals are ready to ENJOY all week long!

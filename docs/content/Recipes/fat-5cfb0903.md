```
FAT
Everyone loves Biryani, this is absolutely flavour packed with incredible 
macros making this an absolute treat for your weight loss journey.
COOKBOOK EXCLUSIVE
THE MEAL PREP COOK BOOK 
179
Return to Table of Contents  
INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Chicken Marinade
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Chicken Breast, medium cubed
	
– 30g (2 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 20g (1.5 Tbsp) Tbsp Ginger Paste
	
– 2 Tsp Garam Masala
	
– 2 Tsp Salt
	
– 2 Tsp Paprika
	
– 2 Tsp Cumin
	
– 1 Tsp Turmeric
	
– Juice of 1 Lemon
	
– 120g (4.2oz) Fat Free Yogurt
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
	
– 20g (1oz) Reduced Fat Butter (for cooking)
Crispy Onions
	
– 250g (8.8oz) Red Onion, thinly sliced
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil 
Biryani Mix
	
– 200g (7oz) Tomatoes, chopped
	
– 1 tsp Garam Masala
	
– 1/2 tsp Salt or to taste
	
– 2 Tbsp Fresh Coriander/Cilantro, chopped
	
– 1 Lemon, thinly sliced
Serve With
	
– 210g (7.4oz) Uncooked Basmati Rice/560g Cooked
	
– 125g (1/2 Cup) Saffron Infused Water
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breasts into medium cubes. 
Marinate with minced garlic and ginger, garam 
masala, salt, paprika, cumin, turmeric, yogurt, 
lemon juice, and olive oil. Mix thoroughly then set 
aside to marinate for 30 minutes or overnight for 
best flavour. 
	
z S T E P 2 
Meanwhile, wash and cook the basmati rice, 
then thinly slice the red onions and chop up the 
tomatoes to use for later.
	
z S T E P 3 
Spread the sliced onions on a lined baking tray, 
spray with a little olive oil, and toss to coat. Oven-
bake or air-fry at 190°C (380°F) for 10–12 minutes 
until golden and crispy. Keep an eye on them to 
prevent burning.
	
z S T E P 4
While the onions cook, heat a pan over medium high 
heat, melt some butter, and cook the marinated 
chicken cubes for 4 minutes per side until golden 
brown and charred.
	
z S T E P 5
Once cooked, lower the heat, then add half of the 
crispy onions, the chopped tomatoes, freshly 
chopped coriander, extra salt to taste, and garam 
masala.
	
z S T E P 6 
Give this a thorough mix until combined, cover, and 
cook on low heat for 10 minutes until the tomatoes 
break down into a thick curry sauce. Use a spatula 
to help mash them. This will smell incredibly fragrant! 
	
z S T E P 7
Once the chicken curry is ready, start layering in 
the pan: chicken mix, rice, lemon slices, crispy 
onions, and chopped coriander. Repeat the layers 
once more and finsih the last layer with saffron 
infused water.
	
z S T E P 8
Cover for 5-10 mins on low heat to let the flavours 
intensify between the layers. Once done, you’re ready 
to serve and divide evenly into 4 portions and ENJOY!
```

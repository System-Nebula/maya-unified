Creamy Scrambled Eggs on Toast
Takes literally 10 mins and tastes amazing with a sweet and spicy hint.
Macros per serving
425 CALORIES
 35g PROTEIN
36g  CARBS
12g FAT
Ingredients (2 scrambled eggs on toast)

2 Whole Eggs + 2 Egg Whites
	
z

z

z
2 Slices Wholemeal Bread (toasted)
	
z
Chilli Flakes (for seasoning)
	
z
Chives (for seasoning)
	
z
Spicy Honey or regular Honey
How to make
1.	 Crack your eggs into a bowl and whisk them thoroughly until well combined. Pour the eggs into 
a non-stick pan over low heat and begin scrambling, stirring gently until they are about 80% 
cooked but still slightly soft.
2.	 Add dollops of cream cheese to the eggs and slowly fold it in, allowing it to melt and create a 
creamy texture. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
3.	 Toast your wholemeal bread, then spoon the creamy scrambled eggs on top. Sprinkle with 
chili flakes, chopped chives, and drizzle some spicy honey over everything. You’re ready to 
enjoy!

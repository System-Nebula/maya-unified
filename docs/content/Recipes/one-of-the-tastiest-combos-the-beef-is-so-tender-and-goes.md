INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Mongolian Beef
	
– 700g (25oz) Raw Topside/Top 
Sirloin Beef, cut into thin strips
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 15g (0.7oz) Dark Soy Sauce
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– ½ Tsp Baking Soda (beef tenderiser)
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
	
– 20g (1oz) Cornflour/Cornstarch (for crispiness)
Noodles
	
– 220g (8oz) Uncooked Egg Noodles »»
	» Continued
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Green Onion/Scallion, white part
	
– 80g (3oz) Green Onion/Scallion, green part
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Garlic Cloves, chopped
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Red Onion, thinly sliced 
For the Sauce
	
– 20g (1oz) Dark Soy Sauce
	
– 20g (1oz) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Oyster Sauce
	
– 125g (½ Cup) Water
	
– 5-10g (1-2 Tsp) Cornflour/Cornstarch 
Mixed with 15g Water
	
– Garnish with Chilli Flakes & Sesame Seeds
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the beef steaks into extra-thin strips. 
Marinate with light and dark soy sauce, garlic 
powder, black pepper, baking soda, olive oil, and 
cornflour. Mix thoroughly until velvety and well 
coated, then let it marinate for 15 minutes. 
	
z S T E P 2 
While the beef marinates, cook the egg noodles 
according to packet instructions or boil for 4–5 
minutes until slightly underdone. Drain and rinse 
under cold water to stop the cooking process.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan over medium-high heat and spray with 
cooking oil. Cook the marinated beef for 2–3 
minutes per side until golden brown and crispy, then 
set aside. Repeat for the remaining beef.
	
z S T E P 4
In the same pan over medium heat, add chopped 
garlic, the white part of the green onion, and red 
onion. Cook for 4–5 minutes until fragrant, then 
pour in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and water. Bring to 
a light simmer, then stir in a cornstarch slurry.
	
z S T E P 5
Let the sauce thicken slightly, then add the cooked 
beef, the green part of the green onion, and the 
cooked noodles. Toss everything together until fully 
combined and coated in the rich sauce.
	
z S T E P 6
Garnish with chili flakes and sesame seeds, then 
divide into 4 equal servings. ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Marinate the beef for at least 15 minutes to allow the baking soda to tenderize it properly.
	
z
Use cooking spray when searing the beef—cook for 2–3 minutes per side to avoid overcooking.
	
z
Let the sauce simmer and bubble before adding the cornflour slurry. Allow it to thicken for 2–3 minutes 
before returning the beef.
	
z
Cook the noodles as per packet instructions—I simmered mine in boiling water on low heat for 5–6 minutes.

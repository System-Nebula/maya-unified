INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breasts into bite-sized cubes. 
Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, 
and cornflour. Mix thoroughly until well coated, then 
set aside.
	
z S T E P 2 
Cook the egg noodles according to packet 
instructions. Once done, rinse under cold water to 
stop the cooking process, then set aside.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a pan over medium-high heat, add olive oil, 
and cook the seasoned chicken for 4 minutes per 
side until golden and a crispy crust forms. Set aside 
and repeat with any remaining chicken.

In the same pan over medium heat, add chopped

Cook for 2 minutes until fragrant, then stir in orange

z S T E P 5
Let the sauce bubble and reduce for 5 minutes on 
medium-low heat. Once it slightly thickens, add a 
cornstarch slurry (water mixed with cornstarch), 
stirring until the sauce further thickens.
	
z S T E P 6
Return the crispy chicken to the pan and coat it 
well in the sauce. Add the cooked noodles and 
chopped green onion, then mix everything until well 
combined and evenly coated.

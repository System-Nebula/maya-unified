INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Cajun Spice Mix (1 Serving)
	
– 1 Tsp each: Salt, Black Pepper, Garlic Powder, 
Onion Powder, Paprika, Chilli Powder or 
Cayenne Pepper, Thyme, Oregano 
Garlic Butter Steak Bites
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Topside/Top 
Sirloin Beef, 1 inch cubed
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 2 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Parsley
	
– 1 Tsp Chilli Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
Cooking & Final Glaze
	
– 40g (1.5oz) Reduced Fat Butter
	
– 30g (2 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 2 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, chopped
Cajun Mac n Cheese 
	
– 280g (10oz) 1% Milk
	
– 150g (5.3oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 35g (1.3oz) Light Cheddar
	
– 35g (1.3oz) Shredded Mozzarella
	
– 1 Serving Cajun Spice Mix
Pasta
	
– 200g (7oz) Uncooked Macaroni 
Pasta (or pasta of choice)
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Cut the steaks into 1 inch cubes. In a bowl, season 
the cubes with salt, black pepper, garlic powder, 
parsley, chilli powder and olive oil. Mix well, then let 
the meat marinate for 10 minutes.
	
z S T E P 2 
Bring a pot of water to a boil and add the macaroni 
pasta. Cook for 8 minutes until al dente. While the 
pasta is boiling, begin cooking the steak bites. Once 
the pasta is ready, drain and set it aside. 
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a large pan over medium-high heat and melt 10g 
of butter. Sear half of the steak bites for about 3 
minutes on each side until a golden brown crust forms.
	
z S T E P 4 
Lower the heat to low, add extra 10g of butter, 
minced garlic, and chopped parsley to the pan. 
Toss the steak bites in this flavorful butter until each 
piece is well coated. Remove the beef from the pan 
and keep warm. Repeat this for the second batch.
	
z S T E P 5
Using the same pan on low heat, add the milk, 
cream cheese, cheddar, mozzarella, and the 
Cajun spice mix. Stir continuously until the sauce 
becomes smooth and indulgently creamy.
	
z S T E P 6
Now add the cooked pasta in the sauce, give it a 
good mix till combined. Garnish with parsley then 
serve with the delicious steak bites and your high 
protein meals are ready for the week!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Sear steak bites over medium-high heat for 3–4 minutes per side until golden brown.
	
z
Lower heat, add butter, garlic, and parsley. Stir for 1 minute until well coated, then set aside.
	
z

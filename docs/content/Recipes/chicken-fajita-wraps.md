How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken into strips, then season with paprika, parsley, 
cumin, garlic powder, chilli powder, salt, pepper, and lime juice. Mix

2.	 Heat a pan over medium heat with olive oil or cooking spray. Cook the

3.	 In the same pan, add sliced bell peppers and onions with the same

4.	 To make the lemon pepper sauce, whisk together light mayo, sriracha, 
salt, pepper, paprika, and lemon juice until smooth and creamy.
5.	 Grab Lebanese flatbread or your wrap of choice. Add shredded lettuce, 
sliced red onion, tomato, the cooked chicken, and a generous drizzle 
of the lemon pepper sauce. Wrap it up and enjoy!

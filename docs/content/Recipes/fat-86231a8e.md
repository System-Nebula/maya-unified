Seasoned Crispy 
Potatoes
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Potatoes, cubed
	
– 1 Tsp each Salt, Garlic Powder,

INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Cut the chicken breasts into cubes. In a bowl, 
combine with minced garlic, onion powder, mixed 
herbs, chilli flakes, paprika, salt, and olive oil. Mix 
well until evenly coated, then set aside. 
	
z S T E P 2 
Slice the potatoes into cubes and place them in a 
mixing bowl. Season with salt, garlic powder, mixed 
herbs, chilli flakes, paprika, and olive oil. Toss 
thoroughly to coat each piece.
	
z S T E P 3 
Spread the seasoned potatoes onto a lined baking 
sheet (use baking paper). Oven bake or air fry at 
200°C/400°F for 20–23 minutes until golden brown 
and crispy.
	
z S T E P 4
While the potatoes cook, heat a large pan over 
medium-high heat and melt the butter. Cook the 
chicken in batches, stirring occasionally, for 6–8 
minutes until golden brown. »»
	» Continue
Once all the chicken is cooked, lower the heat and 
return all the chicken to the pan.
	
z S T E P 5
With the heat on low, add milk, cream cheese, 
parmesan, salt, garlic, and mixed herbs to the 
pan. Stir continuously until it all melts into a smooth 
sauce, then let it simmer for 3–4 minutes until 
slightly thickened.
	
z S T E P 6
By now, the potatoes should be ready. Add them 
to the pan with the creamy chicken and mix 
thoroughly until creamy and well combined.
	
z S T E P 7
Sprinkle shredded mozzarella and fresh parsley 
over the dish. Cover with a lid and let it sit on low 
heat for 3–4 minutes until the cheese melts. Divide 
into 4 servings and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Avoid burning or drying out the potatoes. Use cooking spray instead of olive oil if preferred.
	
z

– 160g (5.7oz) 8 Slices Turkey Bacon
Creamy Scrambled Eggs
	
– 4 Large Eggs
	
– 8 Egg Whites (remove the yolk)
	
– 100g (3.5oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– Garnish Parsley, Chilli Flakes
Spicy Mayo
	
– 40g (1.4oz) 50% Fat Mayo
	
– 20g (0.7oz) Sriracha
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Cut the potatoes into small cubes and season with 
oregano, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. 
Toss until evenly coated, then spread them on a 
plate and microwave for 5 minutes until soft and 
fork-tender.
	
z S T E P 2
Heat a pan over medium heat and add olive oil. 
Cook the microwaved potatoes for 4 minutes to 
crisp them up slightly, then add paprika, chopped 
red onion, red bell pepper, and chopped turkey 
bacon.
	
z S T E P 3
Stir everything together until the potatoes are well 
mixed with the vegetables and turkey bacon. Cook 
for another 5 minutes until golden brown and crispy, 
then set aside.
	
z S T E P 4
In the same pan over low heat, pour in the whisked 
eggs. Stir gently with a spatula for 4–5 minutes, 
scrambling them slowly. Just before they fully cook, 
add cream cheese and fold it in gently until melted, 
creating a fluffy, creamy texture.
	
z S T E P 5
Serve the creamy scrambled eggs alongside the 
crispy potato mix, drizzle with spicy mayo, garnish 
with fresh parsley, and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES

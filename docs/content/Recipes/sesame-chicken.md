Sesame Chicken
Healthy Chinese Sesame Chicken! Super easy and a great way to change how you eat chicken and rice! 
This would be perfect for meal prep too.
Macros per serving
460 CALORIES
53g PROTEIN
45g  CARBS
5g FAT

Chicken marinade:
	
z
300g (10.6oz) Raw Chicken 
Breast, cut into cubes
	
z
4 Garlic Cloves, Minced
	
z
2 Tbsp Low Sodium Soy Sauce
	
z
1.5 Tsp Chinese 5 Spice
	
z

z

Special Sauce:
	
z
1 Tbsp Soy Sauce
	
z
1 Tbsp Hoisin Sauce
	
z
1 Tsp Sesame Oil
	
z
1 Tbsp Corn Flour
	
z
1/2 Cup Water
For the cooking process:
	
z
1 Tsp Olive Oil or Cooking Spray
	
z
1 Onion, Chopped
	
z
1 Bell Pepper, Chopped
	
z
2 Carrots, Chopped
	
z
1/2 Cup Sweetcorn
	
z
Sprinkle of Sesame 
Seeds (Optional)
	
z
Serve with 120-140g  (5oz)
Cooked Rice per Serving
How to make
1.	 Cut the chicken into cubes and season with garlic, soy sauce, Chinese 
5 spice, and paprika. Mix thoroughly until the chicken is well coated.
2.	 Heat a pan over medium heat, add olive oil or cooking spray, and cook 
the chicken for about 5 minutes on each side until golden and cooked 
through. Remove from the pan and set aside.
3.	 In the same pan, add chopped onion and cook until browned. Then add 
carrots, bell pepper, sweet corn, and the special sauce. Let it cook 
on low heat until the sauce begins to thicken.
4.	 Return the cooked chicken to the pan, mix well, and let it simmer on low 
heat for another 4–5 minutes to absorb the flavors.
5.	 Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
Add more water with corn flour 
for more sauce.

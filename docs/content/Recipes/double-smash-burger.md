z

How to make
1.	 Take your lean beef mince and shape it into two equal-sized balls. Lightly season a plate with salt and 
pepper, then roll the meatballs in it to coat.
2.	 Heat a grill or non-stick pan over high heat. Add a small piece of butter, then place the meatballs in the 
pan. Use a spatula to smash them into patties and add sliced onion to the pan beside them. Cook for about 
2 minutes, then flip everything.
3.	 Lower the heat, place a slice of cheese on one of the patties, and stack the other patty on top. Toast your 
burger buns in the same pan until golden.
4.	 Mix mustard, sriracha, and light mayo in a bowl to make the sauce. Spread the sauce generously on both 
sides of the buns.
5.	 Assemble the burger in this order: lettuce, the double-stacked patties, tomato slice, grilled onion, and 
gherkin slices. Serve and enjoy!

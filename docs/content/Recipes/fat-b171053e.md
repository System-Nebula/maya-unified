INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Chicken Marinade
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Chicken Breasts, cubed
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 1 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 1 Tsp Chilli Flakes (omit for milder flavour)
	
– 1.5 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1.5 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 1.5 Tsp Paprika (or Smoked Paprika)
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
Cooking & Final Glaze
	
– 40g (1.5oz) Reduced Fat Butter
	
– 20g (1.5 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 30g (2 Tbsp) Honey
	
– 1.5 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, chopped
Garlic Mac n Cheese 
	
– 30g (2 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 300g (10.5oz) 1% Milk (or any milk)
	
– 160g (5.6oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 1 Tsp Salt (or to taste)
	
– ½ Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 1 Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– ½ Tsp Nutmeg (adds a great deep flavour!)
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Low Fat Cheddar 
Cheese (or any low fat cheese)
Pasta
	
– 220g (8oz) Uncooked Macaroni 
Pasta (or pasta of choice)
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breasts into cubes. In a bowl, 
season with salt, pepper, chili flakes, garlic 
powder, onion powder, paprika and olive oil. Mix 
until evenly coated, then set aside.
	
z S T E P 2 
Bring a pot of water to a boil and add the macaroni. 
Cook for 8 minutes until the pasta is al dente, then 
drain immediately and set aside, as the pasta will 
finish cooking in the sauce.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a large pan over medium heat and melt 10g of 
butter. Add half of the chicken cubes in a single 
layer and cook for 5 minutes per side until golden 
brown and crispy
	
z S T E P 4 
Now lower the heat, add an extra 10g of butter, 
minced garlic, a drizzle of honey, and freshly 
chopped parsley. »»
	» Continue
Toss the chicken until it’s well coated in these 
vibrant flavours. Remove the chicken and set aside. 
Repeat for the second batch.
	
z S T E P 5
In the same pan on low heat with the flavorful butter 
left behind, add more minced garlic and sauté until 
fragrant, about 2 minutes. Stir in milk and cream 
cheese, then season with salt, black pepper, smoked 
paprika, and a pinch of nutmeg. Mix until the sauce is 
smooth.
	
z S T E P 6
Stir in grated cheddar cheese until it melts into 
the sauce and thickens slightly. Add the drained 
macaroni and gently toss to ensure everything is 
evenly coated.
	
z S T E P 7
Portion the mac n cheese with the juicy honey 
garlic chicken into 4 servings and ENJOY!

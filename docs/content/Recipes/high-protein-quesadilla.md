How to make
1.	 Heat a non-stick pan over low heat and lightly spray with oil. Pour in your 
whisked eggs and let them begin to set for about 1 minute. Then gently 
press a tortilla onto the eggs and flip it so the tortilla sits underneath 
and the egg is on top.
2.	 Let the egg cook for another 3–4 minutes until fully set. Use a spatula to 
gently loosen the edges and slide underneath the tortilla to ensure it’s not 
sticking. Carefully flip it over so the tortilla side is now on the pan.
3.	 On one half of the tortilla, layer fresh spinach, tomato slices, cooked 
turkey bacon, shredded cheese, a drizzle of sriracha, and any desired 
seasoning.
4.	 Fold the other half of the tortilla over to close it. If it’s not golden and crisp 
enough, toast each side for another minute or two. Serve warm and enjoy!
Make sure you toast the tortilla 
properly to get a nice hard crust.

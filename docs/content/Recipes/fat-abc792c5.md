INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw Topside Beef, thin strips
	
– 2 Tsp Salt
	
– 2 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Paprika
	
– 1.5 Tsp Chilli Flakes
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil
	
– 25g (1oz) Reduced Fat Butter (for Cooking)
Creamy Sauce
	
– 280g (10oz) 1% Milk
	
– 150-180g (5.3oz-6.4oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Grated Parmesan
	
– 1/2 Tsp each Salt, Black Pepper
	
– 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– Big Handful Spinach
Serve with
	
– 540g (19oz) Cooked White Rice (135g per Serving)
	
– Season with Mixed Herbs, Salt, Chilli Flakes
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice your beef steaks into thin strips, then season 
with salt, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, 
chilli flakes, and olive oil. Mix well and marinate for 
at least 15 minutes (or longer for deeper flavor).
	
z S T E P 2 
Prepare the seasoned rice. In a pot, add water and 
raw rice, bring to a boil, then lower to a simmer and 
cook for 8–10 minutes until fully cooked. Once done, 
season with mixed herbs, salt, and chilli flakes. Stir 
to combine until fragrant and set aside.
	
z S T E P 3
Heat a pan over medium-high heat, add light butter, 
and cook the marinated beef for 5–6 minutes until 
golden brown and juicy. Avoid overcrowding the pan 
for best sear.
	
z S T E P 4
Lower the heat to medium-low, then add milk, 
cream cheese, grated parmesan, extra salt, black 
pepper, and garlic powder.
	
z S T E P 5
Stir everything together until smooth and creamy. 
Add a large handful of spinach and mix it in. Let it 
simmer for 5–8 minutes until the sauce thickens and 
spinach is wilted.
	
z S T E P 6
Once the sauce is ready, serve with the seasoned 
rice, distribute evenly into 4 portions, and ENJOY 
your high-protein meals!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z

z

z

z

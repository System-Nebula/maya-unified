Creamy Fajita Mac n Cheese 
	
– 300g (10.6oz) 1% Milk
	
– 120g (4.2oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Fat Free Cottage Cheese
	
– 80g (2.8oz) Low Fat Cheddar Cheese, grated
	
– 275g (9.7oz) Uncooked Macaroni Pasta
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice boneless, skinless chicken breasts into bite-
size cubes and place them in a mixing bowl. Season 
with salt, oregano, smoked paprika, garlic powder, 
onion powder, cumin, and a drizzle of olive oil. Mix 
thoroughly until the chicken is evenly coated and 
takes on a deep reddish hue. Set aside to marinate 
for at least 20–30 minutes.
	
z S T E P 2 
Bring a pot of water to a boil and cook the macaroni 
pasta for 8–10 minutes, or until al dente. Once 
cooked, reserve a little pasta water (optional), drain 
the rest, and set the pasta aside for later.
	
z S T E P 3 
Heat a large pan over medium-high heat, melt a 
little butter, then add finely chopped onions and 
red, yellow, and green bell peppers. Cook for 5–6 
minutes until softened and fragrant. Add a generous 
amount of fajita seasoning and minced garlic, and 
continue cooking for another 4-5 minutes, stirring 
occasionally, until the veggies are golden and slightly 
caramelised. Transfer this fajita veggie mix to a 
bowl and set aside.
	
z S T E P 4
In the same pan, melt a bit more butter and cook 
the marinated chicken on medium heat for 5 
minutes per side, »» 
	» Continue
or until golden brown and seared. Lower the heat, 
then add a few spoonfuls of the fajita veggie mix, a 
drizzle of honey, and fresh chopped parsley. Toss the 
chicken until well coated in the sweet, savoury mixture. 
Set aside and repeat if cooking in batches.
	
z S T E P 5
To the same pan on medium-low heat, return the 
remaining fajita veggie mix. Pour in the blended 
cheese sauce (made from milk, cream cheese, 
and cottage cheese, blended until smooth). Stir 
continuously until the sauce is warm and velvety, 
then add low-fat cheddar cheese. Let it melt and 
thicken slightly, stirring as needed.
	
z S T E P 6
Add your cooked macaroni to the pan and gently 
fold it through the sauce until every piece is coated 
and the mac is ultra creamy. Let it simmer for a 
minute if needed to reach your preferred consistency.
	
z S T E P 7
Serve into 5 meal prep containers, topping each with 
the juicy fajita chicken. Garnish with fresh parsley 
or a sprinkle of cheese (optional), and your high-
protein Chicken Fajita Mac N Cheese is ready to 
ENJOY all week long!

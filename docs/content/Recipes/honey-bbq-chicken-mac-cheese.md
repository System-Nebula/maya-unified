How to make
1.	 Cut the chicken breasts into small cubes. In a bowl, mix the chicken with minced 
garlic, black pepper, paprika, salt, and olive oil. Stir until the chicken is evenly 
coated. Optional: Add a little extra paprika or a dash of turmeric for deeper color.
2.	 While the chicken is marinating, bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the 
macaroni pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and 
set aside.
3.	 Heat a pan over medium-high heat and spray lightly with cooking spray (or add 
a small amount of oil). Add the seasoned chicken cubes and cook for 3–4 
minutes on each side until golden brown and slightly charred. Ensure the chicken is 
fully cooked through. Turn off the heat completely. Drizzle in the honey and BBQ 
sauce, then toss the chicken to coat evenly. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. 
4.	 In the same pan (no need to clean it), reduce the heat to low. Pour in the milk and 
hot sauce, then add the cream cheese and shredded cheddar. Gently 
whisk until the cheese has melted and the sauce is smooth, thick, and creamy.
5.	 Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and stir to combine. Serve 
in bowls and top with the BBQ honey chicken. Garnish with chopped parsley 
and enjoy!
Make sure you TURN OFF the 
heat before adding honey and 
BBQ sauce.
You can freeze these meals and 
reheat in a pan or microwave. 
Keep in the fridge for up to 4 
days.

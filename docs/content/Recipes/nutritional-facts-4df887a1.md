– 20g Reduced Fat Butter for cooking (1oz / ~1 Tbsp)
	
– Splash of Water (optional, if needed for moisture)
Creamy Chipotle Sauce (enough for 8+ meals) 
	
– 250g Low Fat Greek Yogurt (8.8oz / ~1 cup) 
	
– 100g Light Mayo (3.5oz / ~7 Tbsp) 
	
– 80g Chipotle Paste (2.8oz / ~2.5 Tbsp) 
	
– 25g Honey (1oz / ~1 Tbsp) 
	
– Juice of 1/2 Lime 
	
– 1/2 Tsp Salt 
	
– 1/2 Tsp Garlic Powder 
	
– 1/2 Tsp Paprika
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Prepare the creamy chipotle sauce. In a blender, 
combine Greek yogurt, light mayo, chipotle paste, 
honey, lime juice, salt, garlic powder, and paprika. 
Blend until completely smooth, then bottle and 
refrigerate. This sauce keeps well for the entire week.
	
z S T E P 2 
Heat a pan over high heat, melt the reduced fat 
butter, then add the ground beef. Cook for 6-8 
minutes, breaking it apart, until browned and fully 
cooked. 
	
z S T E P 3
Reduce the heat to medium. Add salt, garlic 
powder, onion powder, parsley, paprika, cumin, 
and chipotle paste. Mix thoroughly so the spices 
coat the beef evenly. If the mixture seems dry, add a 
small splash of water. 
Finish with a drizzle of honey and cook until 
the beef becomes juicy, flavourful, and lightly 
caramelised. Remove from heat. For meal prep, 
allow the beef to cool, then store it as is (do not 
press, pack, or shred).
BEST WAYS TO SERVE AND PAIR THIS PROTEIN 
This Chipotle Ground Beef works extremely well in simple, Mexican-inspired combinations, for example:
	
–with Mexican rice or cilantro lime rice 
	
–in a warm tortilla wrap with lettuce and pickled onions 
	
–in a taco bowl with corn, black beans, and shredded lettuce 
	
–over roasted potatoes with fresh salsa
SAUCE PAIRING SUGGESTIONS 
This beef also pairs well with:
	
–Creamy Chipotle Sauce (included in this recipe) 
	
–lime yogurt drizzle (yogurt, lime, garlic, salt) 
	
–salsa verde or simple blended green sauce

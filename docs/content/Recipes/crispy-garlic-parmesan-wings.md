Crispy Garlic Parmesan Wings
Satisfy your cravings without the crazy calories, these wings are incredibly crispy, no deep 
frying needed and using minimal oil! Perfect for a side dish or a snack!

z

z

Cooking Spray

3 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped
	
z
20g  (1oz) Parmesan
	
z
Fresh Parsley, Chopped
How to make
1.	 Wash the chicken wings and pat them dry thoroughly using tissue 
paper.
2.	 Place the wings in a large bowl and season with garlic powder, onion 
powder, oregano, paprika, salt, pepper, and baking powder. Mix until 
each wing is evenly coated.
3.	 Lay the wings flat on a baking tray or preferably a wired rack. Spray 
with cooking spray and bake in a preheated oven at 200ºC (400ºF) for 
30 minutes.
4.	 While the wings are baking, chop 2–3 garlic cloves, parsley, and grate 
some parmesan cheese.
5.	 Once the wings are cooked, transfer them to a bowl. Add the garlic, 
parmesan, and parsley. Toss well to coat and ENJOY!

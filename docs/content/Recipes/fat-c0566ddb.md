INGREDIENTS (5 SERVINGS)
Honey Butter Chicken
	
– 1000g (35oz) Raw Chicken Breast, small cubes
	
– 1.5 Tsp Salt
	
– 1.5 Tsp Chilli Flakes
	
– 2 Tsp Parsley
	
– 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 2 Tsp Smoked Paprika
	
– 3 Tsp Olive Oil
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Reduced Fat Butter 
(for cooking + glaze)
	
– 20g (1oz) Honey (for all the chicken)
	
– 15g (0.5oz) Soy Sauce (for all the chicken)
	
– 2 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, chopped
Tuscan Garlic Alfredo
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Fresh Minced Garlic
	
– 150g (5.3oz) Sundried Tomatoes, 
drained & chopped
	
– 1 Tbsp Italian Herbs
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 350-375g (12.4oz) 1% Milk (or milk of choice)
	
– 200g (7.1oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 60g (2.1oz) Grated Parmesan
	
– 2 Pieces Parmesan Rind - Optional(outer 
layer, adds so much flavour!)
	
– 275g (9.7oz) Uncooked Linguine 
or Fettuccine Pasta
	
– Garnish with Parsley
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1
Slice the chicken breasts into small cubes and 
place in a mixing bowl. Season with salt, chilli flakes, 
parsley, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked 
paprika, and a drizzle of olive oil. Mix thoroughly 
with your hands until the colour deepens and the 
chicken is evenly coated. Set aside to marinate.
	
z S T E P 2
Bring a pot of water to a boil and add your pasta of 
choice - linguine works great. Cook for 8 minutes or 
until al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.
	
z S T E P 3
Heat a pan on medium heat and melt some butter. 
Cook the chicken cubes for 3-4 minutes per side 
until golden brown and crispy. Once cooked, lower 
the heat and add a little more butter, a drizzle of 
honey, soy sauce, and fresh parsley. Mix everything 
well until the chicken is fully glazed and coated in 
the sauce. Set aside and repeat for the remaining 
chicken if needed.
	
z S T E P 4
In the same pan on medium-low heat, add lots of 
minced garlic and chopped sun-dried tomatoes. 
Cook for 3 minutes until soft and fragrant. Then 
add Italian herbs, salt, low-fat milk, light cream 
cheese, grated parmesan, and a couple of 
parmesan rind pieces (optional for depth of 
flavour). Keep stirring gently until the sauce is 
completely smooth and slightly thickened.
	
z STEP 5
Add the cooked pasta along with more fresh 
parsley. Gently mix everything together until the 
sauce coats each strand and the pasta is creamy and 
indulgent.
	
z F I N A L S T E P
Serve and distribute evenly into 5 meal prep 
containers with the honey butter chicken. Garnish 
if desired - your high-protein meals are ready to 
ENJOY!

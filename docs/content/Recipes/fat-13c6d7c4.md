Cajun Creamy Sauce 
	
– 10g (2 Tsp) Reduced Fat Butter
	
– 1/2 Tsp Salt
	
– 1/2 Tsp Paprika
	
– 1/2 Tsp Garlic Powder
	
– 1/2 Tsp Onion Powder
	
– 1/2 Tsp Italian Herbs
	
– 1/2 Tsp Parsley
	
– 250g (8.8oz) 1% Milk
	
– 150g (5.3oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Parmigiano Reggiano, grated
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the beef sirloin steaks into 1-inch cubes, then 
place them in a large mixing bowl. Season with salt, 
Italian herbs, chilli flakes, garlic powder, onion 
powder, parsley, smoked paprika, and olive oil. Mix 
thoroughly until each piece is evenly coated and 
the colour deepens. Set aside to marinate while you 
prep the veg.
	
z S T E P 2 
Slice the washed, uncooked potatoes into small 
cubes. Place them on a large tray along with 
chopped red onion, broccoli florets, and red bell 
peppers. Season generously with Italian herbs, 
garlic powder, paprika, parsley, and grated 
parmesan. Use clean hands to toss everything 
thoroughly until all the vegetables are well coated. 
Add more seasoning if needed.
	
z S T E P 3 
Transfer the seasoned vegetables onto a lined 
baking tray. Oven bake or air fry at 180°C (360°F) 
for 20–24 minutes, or until the potatoes and 
vegetables are tender, golden, and lightly crisped on 
the edges. Once done, set aside. 
	
z S T E P 4
Heat a pan over medium-high heat, melt a little 
butter, then add the marinated steak bites in a 
single layer. Cook for 3–4 minutes per side until 
golden brown and seared. Avoid overcrowding the 
pan - work in batches if needed. Once cooked, 
lower the heat and add minced garlic and fresh 
parsley, stirring until the beef is coated in a garlicky 
glaze. Set aside to rest.
	
z S T E P 5
In the same pan over medium-low heat, melt a 
bit more butter and toast the extra seasonings 
for 1–2 minutes until aromatic. Then pour in milk, 
followed by cream cheese and grated parmesan. 
Stir continuously until the sauce becomes smooth, 
melted, and creamy.
	
z S T E P 6
Let the sauce simmer gently for 2–3 minutes until it 
thickens slightly. Then assemble your meals: divide 
the roasted parmesan potatoes and veggies 
evenly into 5 portions, top with the juicy steak 
bites, and drizzle with the creamy sauce. Garnish 
with extra parsley if desired—and your high-protein 
meal prep is ready to ENJOY all week!

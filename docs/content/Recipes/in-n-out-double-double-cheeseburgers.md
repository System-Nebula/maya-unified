In N Out Double Double Cheeseburgers
One of the most popular fast food items made healthier with bettermacros AND lower

z

z

z

How to make
1.	 Divide the beef into four equal 100g balls, season each with salt and 
pepper, and place them in the fridge to firm up while you prepare the 
other ingredients.
2.	 Thinly slice the onion and lettuce, and cut the tomato into slices. Set 
all the vegetables aside for assembling the burgers later.
3.	 In a bowl, mix together all the ingredients for the special sauce until 
smooth and well combined. Set the sauce aside.
4.	 Remove the beef from the fridge and heat a pan on high. Add a little 
light butter, place two beef balls in the pan, and use the back of a 
wide spatula to smash each one flat.
5.	 Cook the patties for 2–3 minutes, then flip them and add a slice of 
American cheese to each. Cook for another 2 minutes, then turn off the 
heat, splash in a bit of water, and cover with a lid to melt the cheese.
6.	 Toast the burger buns, spread the special sauce on both sides, and 
assemble with lettuce, a tomato slice, one cheesy patty, sliced 
onion, a second patty, and the top bun. Serve hot and enjoy!
If you don’t have a wide spatula 
to smash the burgers in the pan, 
I would recommend using and 
plate to small the balls before 
placing into the pan to cook.

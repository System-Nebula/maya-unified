How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken breasts into halves and season both sides with garlic powder, paprika, black pepper, 
and soy sauce.
2.	 In a bowl, mix light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, and chilli flakes to make the sauce.
3.	 Cook the chicken in a pan over medium heat for 4–5 minutes each side. Lower the heat, add garlic and the 
sauce, let it caramelise for 2–3 minutes, then set the chicken aside.
4.	 In the same pan, cook sliced red onion and bell pepper for 2 minutes, then add cooked rice and the 
remaining sauce. Mix well.
5.	 Serve the rice with the chicken on top, garnish as desired, and ENJOY!

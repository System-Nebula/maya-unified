INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Honey Garlic Chicken
	
– 800 (28oz) Raw Chicken Breast, cubed
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 2 Tsp Paprika
	
– 2 Tsp Black Pepper
	
– 25g (1oz) Sriracha
	
– 20g (1oz) Light Soy Sauce 
	
– 20g (1oz) Dark Soy Sauce
	
– 30g (1.1oz) Honey
	
– 2 Tsp Olive Oil (for cooking)
Rice Stir Fry
	
– 10g (2 Tsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Red Onion, thinly sliced
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Red Bell Pepper, chopped
	
– 200g (7oz) Uncooked White 
Rice/540g (19oz) Cooked Rice
	
– 10g (2 Tsp) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 10g (2 Tsp) Dark Soy Sauce
	
– 15g (1 Tbsp) Honey
	
– 2 Tsp Sesame Oil
	
– Garnish Green Onion & Sesame Seeds
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Slice the chicken breasts into bite-sized cubes. 
Add minced garlic, paprika, black pepper, 
sriracha, and soy sauce. Mix thoroughly until the 
chicken is fully coatet and let this marinate for 
enhanced flavour.
	
z S T E P 2 
Heat a pan on medium heat, add olive oil, and cook 
the marinated chicken for about 8 minutes, stirring 
occasionally until golden and cooked through. Lower 
the heat completely, drizzle in honey, and stir well 
to glaze the chicken. Once coated, set the chicken 
aside.
	
z S T E P 3 
In the same pan, add minced garlic and sauté for 1 
minute until fragrant. Then add sliced red onion and 
bell pepper, cooking for 5 minutes until softened.
	
z S T E P 4
Add cooked rice to the pan, followed by soy sauce, 
honey, and sesame oil. Stir well until the rice is 
evenly coated and turns a darker color. Return the 
chicken to the pan along with chopped green 
onion, and mix everything together.
	
z S T E P 5
Garnish with sesame seeds. Distribute into 4 equal 
portions for meal prep or serve immediately and 
ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Marinate the chicken for 1–2 hours for deeper flavor.
	
z
Turn off the heat before adding honey to the chicken — mix well to coat, then set aside.
	
z
Sauté garlic for 1–2 minutes until fragrant before adding onions and bell peppers.
	
z
After adding the rice, chicken, and sauces, mix thoroughly until well combined — you can save some chicken 
to serve on top for presentation.

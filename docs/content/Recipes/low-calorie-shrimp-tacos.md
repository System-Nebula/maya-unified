Low Calorie Shrimp Tacos
You don’t have to eat boring foods to lose weight! These are absolutely incredible and bursting with flavour! That 
yellow mango sauce is such a game changer paired with perfectly seasoned shrimp, avocado and salsa!

Tacos:

z
About 8 Pieces of Shrimp

z
3 Thin Slices of Avocado Per Taco
	
z
1 Tbsp or More Mango Dressing
	
z
Coriander and Lime 
Juice (For Garnish)
How to make
1.	 Season your shrimp with paprika, garlic powder, cumin, salt, pepper, lime 
juice, and a bit of olive oil. Mix everything thoroughly to coat the shrimp evenly, 
then set aside to marinate while you prepare the sauce.
2.	 In a blender, add fresh mango, plain yogurt, a pinch of salt and pepper, garlic 
powder, chilli flakes, and a squeeze of lime juice. Blend until smooth and 
creamy. Adjust the seasoning to taste, then set the sauce aside.
3.	 Heat a pan over medium-high heat and lightly spray with oil. Add the shrimp 
in a single layer and cook for 2–3 minutes on each side until golden brown and 
cooked through. Remove from the pan and set aside. In a bowl, prepare the salsa.
4.	 Toast your mini tortillas over the stove flame for a few seconds on each side 
until lightly charred or warm.
5.	 Assemble the tacos, lay the shrimp on each tortilla, spoon over the salsa, add 
a few slices of avocado, and drizzle a generous amount of the mango sauce on 
top. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve immediately. Enjoy!
Keep in mind you will only be using 
a small amount of the mango 
sauce per taco, can store the rest 
in the fridge for up to a week!
This recipe makes 4-5 tacos 
depending on how much 
filling you put in each taco

Healthy Pad Thai
One of my favorite comfort foods, so much flavor and you can enjoy this guilt free! 
Substitutions available too!

The Sauce Mix
	
z
2 Whole Eggs or Egg

z

(For Cooking and Garnish)
	
z
Extra Green Onion
	
z
Handful Bean Sprouts (Optional)
How to make
1.	 Marinate the chicken with soy sauce, garlic powder, and black pepper, then set aside. Chop the garlic 
cloves, shallots, and green onions for later use.
2.	 In a bowl, prepare the sauce by mixing soy sauce, tamarind paste, fish sauce, lime juice, water, and 
brown sugar until well combined.
3.	 Cook the marinated chicken in a hot pan over high heat for about 5 minutes until golden brown and cooked 
through.Remove and set aside. Meanwhile, soak rice noodles in boiling water for 4–5 minutes, then drain.
4.	 In the same pan, heat 2 teaspoons of olive oil and sauté the chopped garlic, shallots, and green onions 
for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Add the soaked noodles and pour in the prepared sauce. Crack in the 
eggs and scramble, mixing everything together well.
5.	 Add crushed peanuts, additional green onions, bean sprouts, and the cooked chicken. Toss everything 
together until evenly combined and heated through, then serve hot. Enjoy!

How to make
1.	 In a mixing bowl, combine blended oats, protein powder, cocoa 
powder, baking powder, applesauce, and honey or sugar-free 
syrup. Stir until the mixture is smooth, then fold in dark chocolate 
chips.
2.	 Line a baking tray with parchment paper and spoon the mixture into 
6 equal portions. Shape into cookies and, if desired, press in extra 
chocolate chips and sprinkle a little flaky salt on top.
3.	 Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for about 7 minutes. Once 
baked, transfer to a wire rack and let them cool for 5 minutes. Enjoy 
with a cold glass of milk!
You can reduce calories and 
carbs by using sugar free syrup

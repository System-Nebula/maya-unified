z

For the cooking process:
	
z
3-4 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped
	
z

z

z

z

z

z
More Jerk Seasoning
	
z

z
110g (3.8oz) Uncooked Penne Pasta
(Use wholewheat if Possible)
How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken breast into thin fillets, add a drizzle of olive oil, and season 
with the listed spices. Mix well until the chicken is evenly coated.
2.	 In a blender cup, combine milk, cream cheese, mozzarella, garlic powder, 
and a bit of pasta water. Blend for about 30 seconds until completely 
smooth and creamy.
3.	 Cook your pasta according to the package instructions. While it cooks, 
heat a pan over medium heat, spray with oil, and cook the chicken for 
3–4 minutes on each side. Once done, remove from the pan and let it rest 
before cutting into cubes.
4.	 In the same pan, add finely chopped garlic and onion. Sauté for 3–4 
minutes until softened, then add the bell peppers and cook for another 
4 minutes. Pour in the creamy blended sauce and add extra seasoning if 
needed.
5.	 Stir the sauce well and let it thicken slightly. Add the cooked pasta and 
cubed chicken back into the pan, give everything a final mix, then serve 
hot and enjoy!
Make sure the chicken is well 
seasoned and once cooked 
allow it to rest before cutting 
into cubes.
When adding the sauce, lower 
the heat so the sauce does not 
curdle.

4 Whole Eggs or Egg whites

z

z
1 Red Chilli Pepper, chopped
	
z
1 Tsp Salt & Pepper
	
z
1 Tsp Mixed Herbs
	
z
1 Tsp Paprika
	
z
1 Tbsp Olive Oil or Cooking Spray
How to make
1.	 Cut the potato into thin chips or fries. Heat a pan over medium-high 
heat and add olive oil. Once hot, add the potatoes and cook for 5–6 
minutes, stirring occasionally until they turn crispy. To speed up cooking 
and promote even crispiness, cover the pan with a lid.
2.	 Add diced onions, bell pepper, and red chili pepper to the pan. Season 
everything with mixed herbs and paprika, then cook for another 2–3 
minutes until the vegetables soften.
3.	 Lower the heat and crack in 4 eggs or egg whites evenly over the 
vegetables. Season again to taste. Cover the pan with a lid and let 
the eggs cook through, spreading the egg whites evenly for better, 
consistent cooking. Serve and enjoy!

to make the potatoes crispier, 
add paprika when you first cook

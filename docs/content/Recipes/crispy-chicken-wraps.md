How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken breasts into 5–6 strips each (about 10–12 total). 
Season with paprika, oregano, garlic, salt, and buffalo sauce. Mix until 
evenly coated.
2.	 On a separate plate, add crushed cornflakes and season with the same 
spices used on the chicken (omit the salt if preferred).
3.	 Coat each chicken strip fully in the cornflake mixture, pressing to 
ensure they’re well covered. Place on a wire rack or baking tray, spray 
with cooking spray, and bake for 20 minutes at 200°C or air fry for 12 
minutes at 200°C.
4.	 While the chicken cooks, make the Buffalo Garlic Sauce by mixing all 
listed ingredients in a bowl until smooth.
5.	 Once the chicken is done, grab a low-calorie wrap and layer with 
shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, the crispy chicken, and a generous 
drizzle of buffalo garlic sauce. Wrap it up and enjoy!

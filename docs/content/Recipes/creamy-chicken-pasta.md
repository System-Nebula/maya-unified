Pasta:
	
z

z
Boil in Water with 1 Tsp Salt
How to make
1.	 Cube the chicken and season with paprika, garlic, basil, chili flakes, 
salt, and pepper. Mix well and set aside to marinate.
2.	 Bring a pot of salted water to a boil, then add the uncooked penne 
pasta. Cook for 8 minutes until al dente. Reserve some pasta water, 
then drain the rest.
3.	 Heat a pan on medium, add olive oil, garlic cloves, red bell pepper, 
and tomatoes. Season with salt, pepper, paprika, and basil. Cook for 
5 minutes until soft, then transfer the mixture to a blender cup.
4.	 In the blender cup, add cream cheese, reserved pasta water, and fresh 
basil. Blend until smooth and creamy.
5.	 In the same pan on medium heat, cook the chicken for 8 minutes. Add 
the cooked pasta and creamy sauce, mix well on low heat, then serve 
and enjoy!

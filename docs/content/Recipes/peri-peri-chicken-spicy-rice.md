How to make
1.	 Marinate chicken thighs with Nando’s Peri Peri marinade, smoked 
paprika, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well and let sit for at least 20 
minutes or overnight for deeper flavor.
2.	 Chop 1 onion and 1 red bell pepper. Rinse 120g of uncooked basmati rice 
and set aside for later.
3.	 Heat a pan over medium-high heat, add a bit of light butter, and cook the 
chicken thighs for 4 minutes on each side until golden with grill/sear marks. 
Set aside to cool.
4.	 In the same pan, add the chopped onion and red bell pepper. Cook for 1 
minute, then add the spices and tomato paste. Stir and cook for another 
2–3 minutes.
5.	 Add the rinsed basmati rice and stir until everything is well combined. Pour 
in 250ml water or chicken stock, bring to a boil, then lower the heat and 
simmer for 8–10 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and fluffy.
6.	 Slice the cooked chicken into strips, layer over the rice, garnish with 
chopped coriander, drizzle with Nando’s Peri Peri sauce, and enjoy!
You can marinate the chicken 
overnight for better flavour and 
cook the next day.
You can cook the rice in a pot but 
for this recipe I prefer to cook it 
in a pan.

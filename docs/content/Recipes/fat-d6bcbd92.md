FAT

INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Crispy Beef 
	
– 800g (28oz) Raw 95% Ground/Minced Beef 
	
– 20g (1oz) Reduced Fat Butter (for cooking)
	
– 100g (3.5oz) Green Onion, chopped
	
– 30g (2 Tbsp) Garlic Cloves, chopped
	
– 20g (1.5 Tbsp) Fresh Ginger, chopped
	
– 150g (5.3oz) Red Onion, thinly sliced
	
– 150g (5.3oz) Red Bell Pepper, thinly sliced
Noodles
	
– 220g (7.8oz) Medium Egg Noodles (Dry Weight)
	
– Garnish Green Onion & Sesame Seeds
Sauce
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Light Soy Sauce
	
– 20g (1oz) Dark Soy Sauce
	
– 30g (1.2oz) Honey
	
– 35g (1.3oz) Reduced Sugar Sweet Chilli Sauce
	
– 25g (1.1oz) Rice Vinegar
	
– 60g (2.1oz) Chilli Garlic Sauce
	
– 1 Tsp Red Pepper Flakes
	
– 1 Tsp Sesame Seeds
	
– 20g (1oz) Water
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1 
Prepare the sauce by combining light soy sauce, dark 
soy sauce, honey, sweet chilli sauce, rice vinegar, 
chilli garlic paste, red pepper flakes, sesame seeds, 
and water in a bowl. Stir until well blended.
	
z S T E P 2 
Heat a pan over medium-high heat and melt a 
little butter. Add all the ground beef and cook for 
6–8 minutes until browned and most of the liquid 
evaporates, breaking it up with a spatula while it cooks.
	
z S T E P 3 
Reduce the heat to medium and stir in half of the 
prepared sauce along with chopped green onions. 
Continue cooking for 5 minutes until the beef 
becomes golden and crispy, then transfer to a plate.
	
z S T E P 4
Cook the noodles by placing them in boiling 
water for 5 minutes. Drain immediately to prevent 
overcooking.
	
z S T E P 5
In the same pan, add chopped garlic, ginger, 
and green onions. Sauté for 3–4 minutes until 
fragrant, then add sliced red onions and bell 
peppers. Cook for an additional 5 minutes until the 
vegetables are soft.
	
z S T E P 6 
Return the crispy beef to the pan and add 
the remaining sauce along with the cooked 
noodles and extra green onions. Toss gently until 
everything is evenly coated and heated through. 
Serve and ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Cook the beef over medium-high heat for 6–8 minutes until most of the liquid evaporates, allowing a good 
sear before adding the sauce.
	
z
After adding the sauce, cook for another 5 minutes until golden brown and crispy, then set aside.
	
z
This dish tastes even better after a day in the fridge. Reheat in the microwave or in a pan with a splash of 
water to restore moisture.

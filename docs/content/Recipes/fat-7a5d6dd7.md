FAT
This will guarantee be your new favourite pasta meal prep!
Perfect balance of sweet and spicy - incredibly flavourful 
chicken paired with spicy chipotle creamy pasta.

INGREDIENTS (4 SERVINGS)
Chipotle Chicken
	
– 700g (25oz) Raw Chicken Breast, cubed
	
– 80g (2.8oz) Chipotle Paste
	
– 20g (1.5 Tbsp) Minced Garlic
	
– 25g (1oz) Honey
	
– Juice of 1 Whole Lime
	
– 1.5 tsp each: Salt, Cumin, Smoked Paprika
	
– 25g (1oz) Reduced Fat Butter (for cooking)
	
– 200g (7oz) Uncooked Penne 
Pasta (or pasta of choice)
Creamy Sauce
	
– 300g (10.6oz) 1% Milk (or any milk)
	
– 160g (5.7oz) 50% Fat Cream Cheese
	
– 40g (1.4oz) Grated Parmigiano Reggiano
	
– 50g (1.8oz) Chipotle Paste
	
– 1 Tsp Chipotle Chilli Flakes (omit for less heat)
	
– 1 Tsp Salt
	
– 50g (¼ Cup) Pasta Water
	
– Garnish Fresh Parsley chopped
INSTRUCTIONS
	
z S T E P 1
Start by slicing the chicken breasts into cubes, 
then season with chipotle paste, minced garlic, 
honey, lime juice, salt, cumin and smoked 
paprika. Mix till the color changes then set aside 
to marinate
	
z S T E P 2
Now cook your pasta. Get water in a pot, bring to a 
boil then add your pasta and boil for 8-9 mins till al 
dente. Once cooked, remove ½ cup pasta water and 
drain the rest then set aside
	
z S T E P 3 
Next for the creamy sauce, in a blender cup add 
milk, cream cheese, parmesan,chipotle paste, 
chili flakes, salt, pasta water. Blend till smooth and 
creamy!
	
z S T E P 4
Then get a pan on medium heat, melt down butter 
and cook the chicken for 8-10 min (4-5 mins each 
side) till golden brown and charred
	
z S T E P 5
Once the chicken is cooked, lower the heat then 
add the cooked pasta with the creamy sauce 
over it and give it all a thorough gentle mix till 
well incorporated. Let it simmer for 1-2 mins to 
thicken the sauce then you’re ready to serve and 
distribute evenly, garnish with fresh parsley and 
ENJOY!
IMPORTANT COOKING NOTES
	
z
Marinate the chicken for at least 30 minutes or overnight for deeper flavor.
	
z
Swap chipotle paste for other chili pastes or tomato paste if needed.
	
z
Ensure the sauce isn’t too watery before blending; it will thicken when heated in the pan.
	
z
Store for up to 4 days and reheat in a pan or microwave when ready to eat.

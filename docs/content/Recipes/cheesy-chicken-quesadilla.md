How to make
1.	 Slice the chicken breast into 2 thin cutlets, season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and 
oregano, then mix well.
2.	 In a blender cup, combine avocado, yogurt, coriander, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and lime juice. Blend 
until smooth and creamy, adding water if needed.
3.	 Cook the chicken on medium heat for 8–10 minutes until golden, then remove and shred using forks or a 
knife.
4.	 On low heat, pour whisked eggs into a pan and place a low-calorie tortilla wrap over them. Let it cook, 
then flip.
5.	 Add tomato slices, shredded chicken, and cheese to the wrap, fold, toast both sides for 1–2 minutes, 
then serve with the green dipping sauce and enjoy!

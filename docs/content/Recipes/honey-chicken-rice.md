Honey Chicken & Rice
This one is a BANGER! Super simple, hint of sweetness with a perfect balance
Macros per serving
510 CALORIES

60g  CARBS

Ingredients (2 servings)
	
z
300g (10.6oz) Chicken Breast, 
cut into bite size pieces
	
z
1 Tbsp Soy Sauce (Light)
	
z
1 Tbsp Paprika
	
z
1 Tbsp Garlic Powder
	
z
1 Lemon, Juiced
	
z
Cooking Spray
	
z
1 Tsp or Tbsp of Honey
	
z
1 Bell Pepper
	
z
Handful Spinach
	
z
1 Cup Cooked Rice
How to make
1.	 Chop the chicken into bite-sized pieces and season with soy sauce, garlic powder, paprika, and the juice 
of half a lemon. Mix well until the chicken is evenly coated.
2.	 Heat a pan over medium heat, spray with oil, and add the chicken. Cook for about 2 minutes on each side 
until lightly browned.
3.	 Turn off the heat, drizzle in the honey, and toss the chicken until fully coated. Transfer to a plate and set 
aside.
4.	 Dice the bell pepper and add it to the same pan along with the spinach. Cover and cook until the 
vegetables are softened.
5.	 Add the chicken back into the pan along with cooked rice, and adjust the flavor with more honey, soy 
sauce, and lemon juice to taste. Mix well and serve hot!
